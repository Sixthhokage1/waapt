Affiliations: Penumbra Clan (Exiled) Ark Clan (Self-proclaimed leader) Region of Origin: Mirage Forest, Holon Personality: Ark is a generally quite young lady who is curious about the world. She is fairly easily impressed by any modern technology and can be very naïve about how the human world works. Ark is by no means dumb though, as what she lacks in knowledge she makes up for in intelligence and guile. Ark is slow to trust others completely, but you have a loyal friend for life once you earn her trust. Ark is not very easily intimidated and only runs from a fight she cant win as a reluctant last resort. Backstory: Ark was found by the Penumbra clan as a small child in Mirage Forest. A pair of Zoroark took in the small child and raised her alongside their son as their own. Ark learned to hunt, survive, and most things a young Pokémon would learn. Ark always knew she was different from the other Pokémon in the tribe. She could not do any attacks, illusions, powers, nothing. The closest things she could do were kicking, scratching, biting, punching, and etc. She was always ultimately the weakest link in the clan. This bothered her only when at the age of 16 she wandered off and saw humans for the first time. They brought Pokémon in strange rocks with them. They spoke in a strange way she could not understand. Ark got too close and they noticed her. They noticed her and freaked out. Ark ran once they started chasing her and only escaped once her parents intervened. Things got worse after that, as days later humans started flooding the area, searching for something. Connections were made and it was realized they were searching for her. Ark had brought humans into the territory and it was obvious that they would not leave until she was found. So it was decided that Ark for leading humans into the area would be exiled. Ark was mortified, but there was nothing she could do. She broke the rules and had to pay. Without the help of the clan, Ark was soon captured by the humans and brought to a strange place far, far, away from the forest she knew. She would later learn the place she was brought was a rehab center in Castellia City. There she was clothed and was to learn how to be civilized. Ark learned basic reading and writing, among other things she would need to survive in their world. She could speak their language somewhat fluently. Still, Ark was miserable. She hated the way the humans looked at her with pity. The white walls and small room were suffocating to be in. It was two years later that Zoro found her. Her younger brother had left the clan and had been following her as best he could, and after two years had finally found her. A plan was devised to break out and leave. Zoro began stealing supplies and clothes that were stockpiled in an alleyway next to the center. The two made their escape off into the sewers and into Castelia Park. Realizing they had literally no plans beyond escaping, the two made a base there and kept stealing to survive. One day Ark accidently captured Zoro in a Pokeball she stole. Once Ark realized what they were, she decided upon a plan. She couldnt return to her own clan, so she should make her own. By using these Pokeballs she could build her own clan. It was then that Ark decided that she and Zorro would be the founders of best clan no wait that sounded stupid okay Ark clan was a much better name. So it was decided, Arks new mission in life. Form a clan. Absolutely nothing could go wrong with this. Nothing at all.
