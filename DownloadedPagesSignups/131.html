    3257 BittersweetNSour19th Feb 2017 08:22:37 AM from After the fact Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Every rose has its thorn
    Knocked down twice but she Rose again
    Yeah, that's correct. For official league-sanctioned battles and such, a 4-move limit is imposed, but that's more a matter of strategically choosing which four moves out of their moveset they use, and it isn't put in place for wild Pokemon battles. Also, generally younger Pokemon won't know their entire moveset even without an official level system, since it takes time to learn stuff; basically in-game levels are more of a vague guideline rather than an actual rule.
