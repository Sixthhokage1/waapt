    • Backstory: She grew up in a comfortable but boring existence where her natural oddness didn't make her much in the way of friends. She was very close to her family however and enjoyed learning different things about Pokemon. Despite this she was too attached to her family to set out on her own Pokemon journey. She took care of her family's Stoutland and Leipard but didn't go far from her home. Over time she grew to be a bit immature but nonetheless curious, cheerful, and well educated, being a fan of music throughout her life. She to this day loves showtunes and will sing them to herself and any Pokemon nearby when alone. She's not always the most perceptive of the people around her, as she often has issues understanding the nuances of communicating with other people. However she is generally dedicated to being useful whenever someone gives her a clear simple task. She tends to get frustrated however when given more complicated tasks without clear instruction on what to do.

    That recently changed when her elderly grandmother died. She had been a lifetime Pokemon breeder and had deep regrets that she wasn't able to see her last six Pokemon grow up. To fulfill her grandmother's wishes she decided to take the six young Pokemon with her on a journey, hoping to make it through a Pokemon League and watch them grow in the process. The other last request her grandmother had was to take a message to Professor Rowan in Sinnoh, and old friend of hers. Thus she decided to head to the Sinnoh Pokemon league to test herself.
