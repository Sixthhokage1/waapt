    Character

    Name: Zhen Lei

    Age: 18

    Gender: Female

    Ethnicity: Chinese

    Brief description of his/her appearance: Zhen Lei has a very petite build, standing at 5'6". Her skin is a pale peach-like colour and her hair is a dark pinkish mauve, tied up into a ponytail that hangs from the front, a single hair pin keeping it in place. Her eyes are a dark brown and she tends to dress elegantly in traditional Chinese wear, keeping a shoulder bag at all times to store her items in, with the Pokeballs held at her waist upon a sash.

    Region of Origin: Hoenn

    Ties to any organizations: None

    Bio: Zhen Lei has lived a very quiet life with her family in Hoenn. Her parents run a quaint little road side inn to support traveling trainers and their Pokemon who happen to pass by and need a place to rest. At a very young age Zhen Lei would help her family in the house by tending to domestic chores, cooking meals and making sure that the guests were comfortable at all times. This sort of experience allowed her to meet all sorts of different people and their various Pokemon, and sometimes she would sit with them as they told her stories of their adventures. Inspired, the young girl became fascinated with the idea of going on her own adventure one day. Such fascination eventually lead her to developing a passion for story books, and she would often invest her time in all different sorts when she could. Sometimes writing her own works of fiction, although she keeps them to herself. Having been traveling trainers themselves in their youth, her parents fully supported their daughter's wishes and would often sit down to tell her about their own tales. When they felt that she was ready to leave home in pursuit of her own adventure, they gave her the hair pin she wears now as a parting gift.

    Personality: Zhen Lei is a very mellow girl. She is always very soft spoken and speaks eloquently, sometimes carrying a sense of poetic prose in her words. She is very kind to everyone she meets and will often go out of her way for others, even if they are complete strangers. Although far from being naive, Zhen Lei's curiosity for others often borders sheer recklessness, leaving her vunerable and putting her in less than pleasant situations. She is, for better or worse, quick to forgive others for their wrong doings, but will defend herself nonetheless should it be necessary. As an aspiring writer, Zhen Lei has a passion for stories of all kinds and enjoys reading. She loves engaging with others in the stories of their lives and learning the history of each town she visits. She is a girl of very simple pleasures and doesn't ask for much other than a happy life on the road with her Pokemon.

    Pokemon

    Species: Misdreavus

    Nickname: Lian

    Gender: Male

    Ability: Levitate

    Bio: Lian had been a part of Zhen's life since she was 12. Originally an Eevee mistreated by their trainer, he was abandoned at the inn when the guest left in the middle of the night. Malnourished and sick, Zhen Lei watched over the frail Eevee and tried to nurse him back to health during that one night as best as she could, although her efforts were in vain when he eventually passed in her arms on the way to a Pokemon Center the next morning. In the passing month however, she quickly discovered that her room was being haunted by a small little Misdreavus, and since then the two were inseperable.

    Personality: Lian is a very naughty Misdreavus who likes to tease and cling to his trainer. His behavior comes off as a bit possessive but he is good natured at heart, if a bit of a trickster. He loves to receive attention from Zhen Lei whenever possible and hates being around strangers, not nearly as quick to befriend others as Zhen due to his experiences in life.

    I believe the words are Timorous Trevenant and Sassy Seel!

    [[folder:Character]]

    Name: John Bikes

    Age: Early 20's

    Gender: Male

    Ethnicity: Caucasian

    Appearance: John Bikes has a medium height, around 5'11", with short, dark brown hair, and eyes so dark brown they seem black at first sight. His skin is a dark tan color, and his wear is almost always casual, mostly wearing a graphic tee in the summer, and a jacket (loaded with highly-functional pockets) over a graphic tee in the winter. The three most unique parts of John's appearance are his Wheely's, shoes with wheels in the bottom, his one-of-a-kind smart watch that he designed himself, and a baseball cap with a very familiar mouse on it.

    Region of Origin: Hoenn

    Organizational Ties: None

    Description/Bio: At a very early age, John Bikes had an accident with an angry Kirlia, which caused some brain damage that was thought to be guaranteed fatal. However, through some miraculous circumstances and extreme love and care from his parents, John lived, though with many challenges. Over the years, John grew more and more, using his injury to his advantage, for his social skills were impaired by the brain injury, but drastically increased his logical and analytical intelligence skills. One of the most important parts of John's life was receiving a little Riolu, whom he named Ninjaura, whose Aura helped him learn new things, and gave John his lifelong best friend. It has also allowed John to communicate almost perfectly with Ninjaura, though he has trouble with his other Pokemon. As time went on, John grew fascinated with computers and technology, and excelled at their study, creating a one-of-a-kind smart watch that he constantly improves upon, adding new features and functionality. His ultimate passion is finding the most strategic of opponents, and battling them in different ways to both test and improve his own skill, as well as just to have a great time bonding with his mon.

    [[//folder]]
    [[folder:Pokemon]] Starter Species: Lucario Nickname: Ninjaura Gender: Male Ability: Steadfast Personality: Ninjaura is a pokemon who has been with John since he can remember, all the way back to when he hatched as a Riolu. He has had a big part in helping John overcome his conditions and become the man he is today, and so John is a huge part of his life, but is equally as large a part of John's life. He's fiercely loyal to John, sometimes to the point of being a bit over-protective, but John knows that's just because Ninjaura cares for him so much. Ninjaura matches many personality traits with his trainer, making him John's best friend, though the more accurate analogy would be John's perfect friend, almost a twin in terms of similarities. Ninjaura prefers to stay side-by-side with John, and he likes to stay outside of his pokeball, John never has any objection to that preference, and won't ever put Ninjaura in his pokeball unless absolutely necessary. Ninjaura's quiet, and not exactly social with pokemon outside of John's party, whom he gets along with fairly well, but outside of that group he mainly only serves as the translator for other pokemon, thanks to his aura capabilities.

    Pokemon 2 Species: Frogadier Nickname: Aquario Gender: Male Ability: Protean Personality: Born in the wild, Aquario originally was rather embarrased of his name, especially since it was based off of his owner's first pokemon right after it evolved, but he has learned to love that name as a unique gift from his trainer. Aquario is a rather mischievous pokemon, he loves to sneak around and try to jump-scare the rest of his team, though he has to be careful who he does that too, with an electric type and an over-protective Lucario as teammates. One of the more unusual parts of Frogadier's personality is that he's the most social on the team, a bit unusual for most of his species. He has been a huge help to John in social situations with other trainers, helping John (through Lucario at first) when he felt too awkward to confidently and naturally do well in social situations, and this skill has helped Aquario lead Metashley through different situations as well.

    Pokemon 3 Species: Metagross Nickname: Metashley Gender: Female Ability: Clear Body Personality: A gift from a scientist's son in Rustboro City, Metashley was a pokemon that further fueled John's love for knowledge. She was a major help when John was having problems with a certain subject, as Metashley was a very motherly pokemon to John, even though he had a very good human mother at home as well. She is not nearly as over-protective as Ninjaura, and even with her vast intellect, she can be a bit ditzy and says the wrong thing in social situations from time to time, taking things a bit too literally or missing a joke entirely. Aquario picks on her because of this deficiency of "social intelligence", but when it comes to friendship, these two have become as close as brother and sister.

    Pokemon 4 Species: Jolteon Nickname: Eevee Gender: Female Ability: Static Personality: Originally an Eevee, John's first caught wild pokemon, her electric-type designation came as a rather unintended and originally unwanted accident when Eevee stepped on a thunder stone, as John had intended to keep Eevee as such, thus why her name is still Eevee. However, she has learned to love it's electric typing, Eevee loves the speed and power that comes with being an electric type, and John loves the benefits that come with having a constant power source with him. John never abuses that power though, Eevee loves supplying her trainer with the power he needs to get further. If Ninjaura is like John's twin brother, then Eevee is like his supportive little sister, her nature makes her a bit docile in battle, but her loyalty can overcome that if her trainer is ever hurt or in need. She is very social, one of the most social and playful in the group, but sometimes she and Aquario have conflicts due to Eevee's Static.

    Pokemon 5 Species: Porygon2 Nickname: Program Gender: N/A Ability: Download Personality: John's most recent addition, Program was found frightened and alone in an old abandoned computer lab that John came across, and out of both pity and fascination with this digital pokemon, John lovingly accepted it into his team. Obviously, Program has some social deficiencies, being incomplete and inorganic, but that's one of the reasons that John bonded with it so quickly. Not even Ninjaura can communicate with this mon, but in many cases John understands the logical, analytical, impersonal actions and meanings of Program even without Ninjaura's help. Program has the most trouble outside of battle, struggling to socialize and get to know the other members of John's party, but he's a strong asset in battle, but somewhere inside Program, he knows that he can improve, and that John does love him, and that together, they may be able to find Program's missing piece, but Program can't help but feel that the missing piece sometimes feels filled when bonding with John. Program is still struggling, finding out exactly what feelings are, how they work, but he trusts John to lead him along the way, and he's determined to help John in whatever his pursuits are.
