    Sinister Scyther Magnificent Mankey

    Name: Ark Lostlorn

    Age: 18

    Gender: Female

    Ethnicity: Caucasian

    Picture:[1]◊

    Appearance: She has matted long black hair and light blue eyes. Her mouth covered by a long black scarf that trails almost down to her feet, slowly turning a dark red. She wears a long damaged grey jacket with too-long sleeves over a black t-shirt. Her pants are black and torn in several places, barely making it to the scruffy grey sneakers she wears. She has an old black backpack that is scuffed in several places.

    Affiliations: Penumbra Pack (Exiled) Ark Pack (Self-proclaimed leader)

    Region of Origin: Lostlorn Forest, Unova

    Personality: Ark is a generally quiet young lady who is curious about the world. She is fairly easily impressed by any modern technology and can be very naïve about how the human world works. Ark is by no means dumb though, as what she lacks in knowledge she makes up for in intelligence and guile. Ark is slow to trust others completely, but you have a loyal friend for life once you earn her trust. Ark is not very easily intimidated and only runs from a fight she cant win as a reluctant last resort. Ark is very, very, stubborn when it comes down to it, and will generally only listen to sound logic if you want her to cooperate with you.

    Notes: Feels more comfortable speaking in the language of Pokémon then the human language. Barely knows anything about modern technology. Has a basic grasp of how money works. Her writing is peppered with misspellings and errors. Has well trained if a bit rusty reflexes from living in the wild most of her life. Mentally identifies herself as Pokemon Zoroark specifically-, but is aware and a bit bitter about being a human. Her clothes are a mix of from the trash and some stolen from costume stores.

    Backstory: Ark was found by the Penumbra pack as a small child in Lostlorn Forest. A pair of Zoroark took in the small child and raised her alongside their son as their own. Ark learned to hunt, survive, and most things a young Pokémon would learn. Ark always knew she was different from the other Pokémon in the tribe. She could not do any attacks, illusions, powers, nothing. The closest things she could do were kicking, scratching, biting, punching, and etc. She was always ultimately the weakest link in the pack. This bothered her only when at the age of 16 she wandered off outside of the illusionary barriers and saw humans for the first time.

    The elders always said to never go outside of the illusions, for they protected the pack from outsiders. Ark obeyed that rule for 16 years. Shes heard others complain and murmur whenever something they called humans got too close to the barrier. Ark and the other children were kept in the camp whenever this happened. It was only when she overheard one of the adults mention to her parents that Ark was probably a human, that her curiosity was piqued. Surely there were mistaken, Ark was just a really weird looking Zorua or maybe Zoroark probably Zoroark. She could stand on two legs after all. Ark tried to ignore these thoughts, she really did, but now that she started to seriously consider that maybe she wasnt just different in appearance from her tribe. Was she really an entirely different species? Curiosity was always Arks fatal flaw, and as usual it got the best of her.

    They brought Pokémon in strange rocks with them. They spoke in a strange way she could not understand. Ark got too close and they noticed her. They noticed her and freaked out. Ark ran once they started chasing her and accidently led them through the barrier. She only escaped once her parents intervened.

    Things got worse after that, as days later humans started flooding the area, searching for something. Connections were made and it was realized they were searching for her. Ark had brought humans into the territory and it was obvious that they would not leave until she was found. So it was decided that Ark for leading humans into the area would be exiled.

    Ark was mortified, but there was nothing she could do. She broke the rules and had to pay. Without the help of the pack, Ark was soon captured by the humans and brought to a strange place far, far, away from the forest she knew. She would later learn the place she was brought was a rehab center in Castellia City known as Kazam Rehabilitaton Center. There she was clothed and was to learn how to be civilized.

    Ark learned basic reading and writing, among other things she would need to survive in their world. She could speak their language somewhat fluently. Still, Ark was miserable. She hated the way the humans looked at her with pity. The white walls and small room were suffocating to be in. The humans sometimes called her the Lostlorn Girl. Eventually this became what humans referred to as her last name. Ark Lostlorn, the feral girl from Lostlorn Forest. Much to her displeasure, Ark started to use it too on reflex. She wanted to curl up in a ball and die after that, but the therapists gave her this delicious thing called a Casteliacone once she started doing it. Ark decided to humor them in return for the delicious thing, which she considered the only nice thing about the place. She wanted to go back home. But she couldnt. She was exiled. She had no home now.

    It was two years later that Kiaran found her. Her older brother had left the pack and had been following her as best he could, and after two years had finally found her. A plan was devised to break out and leave. Kiaran began stealing supplies and clothes that were stockpiled in an alleyway next to the center. The two made their escape off into the sewers and into Castelia Park. Realizing they had literally no plans beyond escaping, the two made a base there and kept stealing to survive.

    One day Ark accidently captured Kiaran in a Pokeball she stole. Once Ark realized what they were, she decided upon a plan. She couldnt return to her own pack, so she should make her own. By using these Pokeballs she could build her own pack. It was then that Ark decided that she and Kiaran would be the founders of best pack no wait that sounded stupid okay Ark pack was a much better name. So it was decided, Arks new mission in life. Form a pack. Absolutely nothing could go wrong with this. Nothing at all.

    Species: Zorua

    Nickname: Kiaran Age: 19 in human years Level: 25

    Ability: Illusion

    Appearance: Regular Zorua, if a bit more scruffy and wearing a red mini-scarf tied around his neck.

    Gender: Male

    Personality: Kiaran is the adoptive older brother of Ark. Kiaran is generally a worrywart at best and over protective at worst. Kiaran is like his younger sister, cautious of others after being exiled from their pack. However, Kiaran will put all of this aside when he considers Ark to be in danger and throw caution to the wind for her sake. Kiaran is hesitant to attempt to make bonds, but once you get in his good graces Kiarans inner social butterfree comes out and quickly makes friends.

    Notes: Supposedly Kiaran means little black one in the anglicized form of Irish Gaelic Ciarán
