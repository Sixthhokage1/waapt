He was about to receive his first assignment, when he heard the news. His father had been killed in action. That day, he vowed to find the perpetrator and serve him piping hot justice just like his father would have. In the present, he is a soft-spoken, amiable, big-hearted man with a love for fishing, boxing, Pokemon battling, his mother (to whom he sends money regularly) and JUSTICE, and as a result of his studying as a scientist-to-be, he's very well educated and has a wide vocabulary. But beware, if you are a criminal, a jerk, or a Kalosian, he will make you cry.

    Species: Typhlosion

    Nickname: Ember

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Flash Fire

Basically, Tex was being held at knifepoint by an accomplice of a crime lord he was attempting to track down, and Ember saved him while horribly disfiguring the accomplice, who is currently in prison.

    Species: Arcanine

    Nickname: Scout

    Gender: Male

    Ability: Intimidate

    Bio: John Barrister's partner and family pet. Wise, but a really chill dude with a healthy sense of humor. Runs and hits like a freight train.     Species: Metagross (shiny)

    Gender: None (masculine personality)

    Ability: Clear Body
