    1140 Mrsunshinesprinkles20th Feb 2014 06:07:13 AM Get RP Mod
    Timezone: Phillipines Time

    Interest; I attached an Oc to my first ever "real" team, and I feel like Rping.

    Trainer: Robin

    Age: 13

    Apperance: Blond hair, blue eyes, black jacket, blue jeans. Somewhat skinny, 5''6, and has this look of tiredness with him, even when he's pumped.

    Personality: First impressions usually leave a mark of a poisonously pessimistic snot, who's way to depressed for his age. For the most part, it's kind of true, but though he can be a bit of a jerk, he opens up to people he trusts, which is a list that basically consists anyone he's known long enough. He is also easily swept up in the heat of the moment, and can get carried away/panick easily whenever things get intense.

    Region/City of Origin: Aspertia City, Unova; Moved to Kalos, Lumiose City

    Notable ties: N/A

    Backstory: At first, Robin was reluctant to go on a Pokemon Journey. He didn't feel like becoming the best, or grabbing the world's attention; he wasn't all hot on the idea of battling or contests. He didn't see any reason to beat all the gyms, or take on the Pokemon League. He found it all pointless, and adamantly refused to go out there.

    And then he saw his buddy qwistfully looking out the window. With that, he decided to set off on a journey, fueled by a promise made to his lifetime companions to see new sights, and meet new friends.

    ...After his family finishes packing for Kalos. Well, he did promise to be more open with new opportunities...

    Pokemon:

    (Starter)Male Solosis -> Duoison:

    Ability: Magic Guard

    His Main Pokemon, and best friend. It is a somewhat nicer version of his trainer; Usually quiet, but filled with curiousity, willing to meet new friends, and like Robin, often gets pumped, when things get hot. It can listen to its trainer's thoughts via telepathy, though it only nods in response. It evolved into Duoison, thanks to years of accumulated experience, gained by doing meager tasks for its trainer, or shooing off pests. Most of his other second stage 'mons evolved this way.

    Female Larvesta:

    Ability: Flame Body

    It was hatched from an egg given to him by a strange, old wise man. Larvesta is somewhat shy and timid, but loyal to its trainer, and more than capable of holding its own, if push came to shove. It is unaware of its capabilities, and often scorches things by accident, even and especially Robin himself.

    Male Azurril -> Marril

    Ability: Huge Power

    A playful little blue rodent, befriended and eventually caught by Robin. They met when Robin was playing around in the Floeccesy Ranch, and it instantly took a liking to him (Spent a week latching on to him, before being formally caught). It easily makes friends with everyone, but sends people flying, if they push him too far.

    Male Timburr -> Gurdurr

    Ability:Guts

    A proud little thing, that Robin acquired over a trade with one of his breeder friends. Though it disobeys his trainer from time to time, it usually does so with the best intentions; believing it knows a better way of winning than his trainer does. It is the least likely to accidentaly harm Robin. Unlike most other Timburr, it knows the moves Mach Punch and Drain Punch; something that can be attributed to its parents.

    Female Zubat -> Golbat:

    Ability: Infiltrator

    Found by Robin, lost, injured, and under the pouring rain, Zubat's origins are the most mysterious out of The Team. It knows Brave Bird, which no regular Zubat should know, and it is of a rare strain. His trainer was never one to over think things, however, and otherwise, it is just like any other Pokemon.

    Male Ferroseed

    Ability: Iron Barbs

    The last of Robin's team. A wild Ferroseed that Robin befriended, on a field trip to Chargestone Cave. Fitting of something so sturdy, it is protective of not only its Trainer, but his fellow team members as well; usually, it is the first to jump into action and take a hit for the team.
