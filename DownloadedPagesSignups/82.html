    • Your Trainer's name: Camryn Yarrow.
    • Age: 17.
    • Gender: Female.
    • Ethnicity: Pacific Islander.
    • Brief description of his/her appearance: Tall (around 5' 10"), with tanned skin and black hair to her mid-back, usually kept in a simple ponytail. She generally wears jeans and various t-shirts. There is a small but noticeable slant to her eyes as a result of her ancestry. She also wears glasses.
    • Any ties to an organization: Her dad is the former Dewford Gym Leader, and she befriended Brawly, so that's something. Otherwise, none.
    • Region of origin: Hoenn.
    • Backstory: Camryn was born in Dewford Town to the then-Gym Leader and his wife (her dad retired when she was about 5, leaving the position open for Brawly), and lived a generally happy life, exploring Granite Cave and Dewford Island's small forest, playing with other children, and basically having a grand old time. None of that really changed when she grew older, though she took to reading books and playing video games after exploring the forest and Granite Cave thoroughly, and because none of it changed, Camryn began to grow massively, incurably bored. Sure, Dewford was nice, her life was nice, and her parents were awesome, but good God, did it get boring. To that end, she and her Pokemon set out on a journey. With her parent's permission, of course. She's been on her journey for a few years, and in lieu of a regular starter, uses her first Pokemon, a Sableye she caught in Granite Cave. Camryn's attitude to most anything she hasn't seen before is "OHMYGOD THAT'S SO COOOOOOL~".

    Pokemon
    • Species: Sableye.
    • Nickname: Taonga.
    • Gender: Male.
    • Ability: Prankster.
    • Personality/Bio: Taonga was Camryn's first Pokemon, and as such, is her best friend while out on the road, though she's been with all of her Pokemon for a while. He's pretty much incapable of taking anything seriously, so if he stops smiling or cracking jokes, you know something's gone horribly wrong. He also has Sucker Punch as an Egg Move.

    • Species: Lairon.
    • Nickname: Tīra. (Pronounced with a long i)
    • Gender: Female.
    • Ability: Heavy Metal.
    • Personality/Bio: Tīra was found as an Aron by Camryn when she decided to climb to the top of the small mountain that Granite Cave's entrance is in the base of. When Camryn reached the top, Tīra was already sitting there, and after the two started talking (so to speak), they became friends. Tīra has always liked heights, which is a strange juxtaposition with her typing and ability. In fact, it's because of her type and ability. Her biggest dream is to fly, even though she likely never will. She's the most level-headed of the group.

    • Species: Jumpluff.
    • Nickname: Uri.
    • Gender: Male.
    • Ability: Leaf Guard.
    • Personality/Bio: Camryn met Uri as a Hoppip when she was exploring the forest. She had found a small clearing that opened out to the ocean when Uri blew in and narrowly missed her head. Uri loves seeing new things, which is part of why him and Camryn are friends. If either of them notice something new, you can count on them to obsess over it together. Other than that, Uri is very laid-back, almost too much for his own good.
