    Again, we're just trying to make sure we don't get overwhelmed again. However, if extenuating circumstances prevent you from posting your signup in time, we'll probably still let you in! We don't wanna be too unfair.

    [The rules have been changed a bit to reflect a change in how the queue works due to unexpectedness with when it was previously defined by arcs.]

    Oh, to clarify on said queue system:

    • In the middle of every month or two, we'll decide if it's a good time to let more people join.
    • If so, we'll contact the next 3 people in the queue and tell them they're free to post their signup sheets.
    • If someone does not post in the Signup thread or contact one of us within a week from when they were first
    • If the last thing we heard from someone going through the Signup Process was 10 days ago or if they tell us they're no longer interested, they're removed from the queue and the next person in the queue is contacted to say they're free to sign up. [Though if you're going to be occupied in the middle or are still working on aspects of your Signup or something like that, please tell us and we'll give you the time you need.]
    • If you haven't been around for a bit and got passed over in the queue, you are still eligible to sign up. It would kind of suck to be gone for two weeks, get back, then find out you got skipped over. The rule's mostly there to keep the queue going if we're not sure if someone's still interested or not/account for people who may no longer be on TVT.

    But for the people who expressed interest before we put this system in place, you're still free to signup after Hoenn-2, but we'll PM you in two groups so we can handle the signup process/getting you integrated into the RP easier.

    Hopefully you're still interested despite the potential wait time and if so, make sure to note your interest so we'll know to PM you when we can.
