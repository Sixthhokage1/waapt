    After a lot of schedule delayment and such, here is my Signup.

    Disclaimer: I am Italian and my English isn't perfect. What's more, I am writing from my phone. Thus, I apologize in advance for any errors there could be

    • Name: Alexander Ruthal
    • Age: 17
    • Gender: Male
    • Ethnicity: European (I guess? I don't really understand how this works)
    • Appearence: Alexander is a tall, slim, average 17-year-old guy. He has dark, short hair and brown eyes. He likes to listen to music, thus he carries his Mp3 and headphones with him, usually. He also wears sunglasses. As for clothes, a t-shirt, jeans and a hoodie if it is cold.
    • Region of Origin: Sinnoh, Canalave City
    • Backstory: Alexander is just a normal guy with a normal life. Just, he's a Pokemon Trainer. He has been in contact with Pokemons since he was little, as her mother, who worked in the nearby Pokemon Center, would bring home her Chansey for him to play with. Sometimes, she even brought some of the Mons she was curing, to see if they had indeed gotten better.

    One day, while Alexander was still 6, her mother got home carrying a wounded Elekid in her hands. It was badly wounded, and would've probably died if not taken care of.

    She had found it on her way home from the PC, and brought it home to cure him and, eventually, release him in the wild. It seemed, as its isn't a native species to that zone, that it was brought in by a trainer and then for whichever reason released.

    Alexander helped her cure it, her mother being able to do so as she knew some first aid, working where she does, and kinda grew attached to him, as he usually not only helped with its treatment but also played with it.

    When the time to release him came, Alexander opposed. After some discussion and some responsability-themed speeches, he was allowed to keep it.

    They grew together, kind of like brothers. Just a little... genetically different ones. Still, it soon enough became part of the family. Eventually, it evolved into Electabuzz , then was made to evolve into an Electivire, while Alexander himself was growing up.

    But he isn't the only Pokemon Alexander has had for quite some time. For his 12th birthday, his grandparents gave him another Pokemon: a Trapinch (they wanted to give him an "exotic" and "rare Pokemon" so that "he would've gotten popular with his friends", in their words)

    It got along quite nicely with his Electabuzz, and they would use to play with each other simulating battles, as well as being put, now alongside Electabuzz, in battles against his friends. And, soon enough, it evolved into a Vibrava.

    Some time later, around the age of 15, he decided that he wanted to add another mon to his collection. Thus, he caught a wild Starlie. Which after the usual training and battles with friends evolved into a Staravia.

    And then, when he was 17, he decided that he wanted to travel the world and become a Pokemon master. It was hard to convince his parents, but he managed to convince them with the promise he'd phone them every so often.

    And now his (great?) adventure is about to unfold...

    • Timezone: I am Italian, so that makes it, if I am not mistaken, GMT+1
    • Random Words: Exalted Electabuzz and Despondent Delibird

    • Electivire[STARTER]
      • Species: Electivire
      • Nickname: Na N
      • Gender: Male
      • Ability: Motor Drive
      • Personality:Electivire is a fun-loving guy, always ready to crack a joke or laugh. He always enjoy finding new ways to have fun. However, all this love for fun also serves to distract him from thinking about what happened with his other trainer. Because yeah, he doesn't really like to talk about it. Another thing he doesn't like is losing.
      • Bio: When he was an Elekid, he used to wander around Kanto, where he was born... Then, he was caught by a trainer. Suffice to say, his life was not easy with him. Stored up in the PC for quite some time, when he was finally taken out for a battle in Sinnoh, near Canalave City...he was kind of unprepared. And he lost. His trainer, expecting nothing short of perfection, released him and left him alone (after some shouting of "You Fool!", for good measure)
    It was then that, after finding his way into Canalave City, that he fainted and was foud by Alexander's mother. The rest is history.

    • Vibrava
      • Species: Vibrava
      • Nickname: Na N
      • Gender: Female
      • Ability: Levitate
      • Personality: Vibrava is an energetic, freedom-loving, brave and determined Pokemon, who will fight no matter what and try to stand up- and fight even harder- after each wound (basically a Determinator. This can lead her to be too reckless, in which case she may get herself in avoidable danger. Still, she usually respects her trainer's commands... But not always.
      • Bio: She, as a Trapinch, was caught in the wyld when she was little, to be sold at a store to customers. As Alex's grandparents bought her, she ended up being Alex's Pokemon. At first she was kinda reluctant to be a caught Pokemon, having to obey a trainer and stuff. But she soon began to appreciate Alex, and she also got along quite nicely with him, and with his other mons too.

      • Species: Staravia
      • Nickname: Na N (Alex doesn't like nicknames that much)

Known to sometimes sprout random philosophical sentences amidst battles, which sometimes do even make sense, it is also the newest acquisition in Alexander's team, but is getting along quite well, having already understood he has little to no other choice anyway and thus trying to enjoy his new life.
