    Gender: Cis female

    Ethnicity: White with Israeli influences from a maternal grandfather

She has dark hair with a reddish-brown undertone in certain, but not all lights. It is very thick and difficult to tame, so she doesn’t let it grow past the tops of her shoulder blades and keeps it in a messy bun so that it doesn’t get in the way. She has greyish eyes and an aquiline nose. She wears little makeup. She wears dark purple corduroy dungarees over a light cream shirt. Though she wears boots for practicality’s sake, she prefers heels.

    Any ties to an organization (i.e Pokemon Rangers, regional evil team, many possibilities beyond those): Nothing too interesting, though she did do some work experience for the Goldenrod Radio Tower.

    Region of origin: Johto, specifically Goldenrod City (or, more specifically, just on the outskirts of Goldenrod City: it’s a pain getting anywhere, but she can’t use it as an excuse for being late.)

    Backstory: Sylvie had a pretty normal upbringing: the child of a single mother who is divorced quite amicably from her (Sylvie's) father, she has two older brothers, one very brash and one rather quiet. The result of constantly mediating between the two initially gave her a very placating disposition, but since realising that the constant arguments were stupid, she’s polished her angry parent voice, and is now a bonafide wizard at yelling at people. This came in handy at her first secondary school, a low funding inner-city school which left her behind academically (relatively speaking: she’s actually slightly ahead, but an average school might have put her a fair amount ahead), much to her dismay. The result, however, was that she has since had a tendency towards brashness and arrogance. This has not been a huge issue at her new school, a grammar school, but did put her in poor stead with the other students at her old school.

    Because she is the youngest of three, and the only girl, her mother can be very protective, and when – in 2014 – she did her work experience at the radio station, it took several charts and her middle-brother acting as her attorney (he is not qualified to be an attorney) to convince her that it was perfectly safe for her to attend. She succeeded, and to her dismay she was a fairly average worker. Now, she is aware that she wants to be significant in the world. She doesn’t know how this would happen, she just knows that it’s what she wants.

    Species: Meditite

    Nickname: Lombardy

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Pure Power

    Personality/Bio: A hard worker, but probably couldn’t defeat a Monstrous Machamp. She’s sort of the opposite to Sylvie; where Sylvie is constantly striving to be something, Lombardy is perfectly happy just to be. As a result, she’s very good at calming Sylvie down when the need arises – which is why Sylvie was given a meditative Pokémon rather than a Pokémon like Beedrill (also, she’s scared of bees, which is another reason why nobody gave her a Beedrill.)

    Species: Graveler

    Nickname: Pavye

    Gender: Male

    Ability: Sturdy
