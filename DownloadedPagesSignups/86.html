    The eternal dusk returns

    Are you someone wanting to join the RP but rather intimidated by the large queue we have? Even worse, are you already in said queue? If the latter, are you upset at the prospect of waiting anywhere from a month to several months to get in on the fun?

    Well, good news, because we've found a way to potentially ease the wait!

    You see, a while back, one of our current members, Fletcha, expressed his interest much like any other post-queue-system member, but with a twist - he asked permission to hang out in the chat and Discussion thread in order to get to know the RP and its community better while waiting for his turn to "properly" join. And since the sole reason we implemented a queue system in the first place was that large influxes of newbies were causing the RP itself to reach ludicrous speeds pace-wise, we decided letting him go ahead and get involved in the meta (which rarely suffers such problems) was totally fine, and we said yes. And... It worked out really well. And so recently I started thinking... If we could let Fletcha do that, why not every other prospective newbie?

    And thus we bring you...

    The WAAPT Early Access Program!

    Yes, we're implementing an early access program! And unlike most other such programs you don't have to pay an exorbitant fee or sell your soul to Yveltal or anything like that; in fact, you don't have to do anything different at all!

    Yveltal: <Wait, we aren't going ahead with the souls plan? Dammit.> —flies off in a huff-

    The premise is simple: From now on, as long as you go through the motions of expressing your interest in joining and being added to the queue, the only major thing you'll be barred from doing in the RP's community before we get to you in said queue is posting in the RP thread itself (As well as canon-to-the-RP threads on our second forum). Almost everything else about the RP's community is free for you to access from the start. Talking it up in the chats? You're good to go. Talking it up in Discussion instead? Feel free. Helping with the wiki? Yes, even that! Heck, feel free to start following the RP thread once you've joined the meta, if you want - It may help you jump in easier once you "properly" join.

    The point of this is to (hopefully) provide various benefits to all you newbies: You'll get to know the RP and it's community long before you actually join (hopefully increasing your chances of sticking around), have some form of participation and fun before truly joining, toss ideas for your characters around to see what works and what doesn't before even writing an actual signup, and being a much quicker way for you to decide whether this RP and its community are a good fit for you (as opposed to waiting several months, going through the whole signup shebang, and finally joining... only to realize a week in or less it's not your cup of tea).

    However, we've obviously got to have some rules for this, and here are the baseline ones which will likely be adjusted as necessary:

    • As mentioned, no actually participating in the RP until we get to you in the queue. You can read the thread and ask questions and stuff about the RP all you want, but no actual R Ping until you go through the whole shebang of the signup process.

    • As I also mentioned, we want you guys to work on the wiki, but there's some kinks to work out first. I'll update this when we do.

    • Early Access newbies probably won't get much say in most big RP decisions, though we're likely to make exceptions depending on circumstances.

    • You know that bit of the signup OP saying to follow the rules and not be a jerk and/or idiot lest you risk reprimand or punishment? This goes DOUBLE for Early Access newbies. Problem players are much harder to handle once they've properly joined, so unfortunately we're gonna have to tougher on you guys. If any early access newbie is deemed problematic, they will receive one warning telling them what they're doing wrong. If this does not lead to enough improvement they will be booted from the Early Access Program, be removed from the queue, and barred from joining the RP until/unless they've improved on their issues and have concrete evidence of such.

    But that's about it. Newbies just seeing this now, express your interest and get right on in. Newbies already in the queue... Well, we figure some of them are watching this thread to some degree, so...

    The Discussion Tread

    Feel free to jump right in to either- or better, both- of those. I'll add the wiki once the aforementioned kinks are worked out.

    For those of you not following this tread I'm gonna wait a few days then send a link to this post to anyone who hasn't responded to it - though only two per day.

    And in the end if you wanna skip this and just wait your turn for everything, that's fine. But you're likely missing out. :P

    (IMPORTANT NOTE: This post will likely undergo several tweaks, and is eventually going to be adapted into a new section of this thread's OP, so stay tuned.)
