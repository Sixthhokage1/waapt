    OK, random but rather large note: I did a rule patch a while back in the wake of an - ahem - major problem, and have been procrastinating on announcing it here. However, after some consideration and consultation with CorvusAtrox and Tangent128, today I expanded that into rewriting the "what does it take to be a WAAP Ter" section of the OP to accommodate, since it'd gotten kinda incoherent. Changes below, additional notes in pink.

    Also, we're very much a LGBTQ+-friendly RP, so if you're on the spectrum feel free to hop aboard! (Moved from relevant section to above section since, really, it belongs there)

    Ooh, sounds neat. What does it take to be a WAAPTer?

    You have to be the very best, like no one ever was Not TOO much. This RP welcomes anyone who enjoys creative writing and has a decent knowledge of and enthusiasm for the Pokemon franchise in any of its major incarnations, bar folks like Genwunners and other fandom jerks (like, if you've only played up to G2 and/or only watched a few seasons of the anime series or something like that there'll be a steeper learning curve but we'll gladly let you in provided you follow the other rules and are willing to work with rather than reject new franchise developments). (For the record, yes, we've had plenty of players who only had seen the anime or stopped playing after G3 or something and managed to learn the ropes.) Other than that, it's generally more about what you shouldn't do in order to maintain a safe and healthy environment for your fellow players:

    • Don't harass people: Don't flame other people's RPing, don't dismiss or ostracize other people in general (especially if they are trying to help you or you're being cliquey), don't be condescending or put others down in any way, don't go after people over disagreements over anything, don't be a jerk to other RPers with different tastes than you, don't insult things just because they're brought up, just... be civil when you have a problem with someone or what they do or whatever, alright?

    • Don't be obnoxious: Spamming the chatroom or Discussion Thread with shitposts, constantly asking Captain Obvious questions, intentionally tying to get a rise out of people, being unncessarily negative during something positive or gushy for no reason but being a joykiller, being cold, inconsiderate or worse flippant when someone is genuinely upset over an issue that's legitimately wounded them... yeah, all that annoys us fast.

    • Make sure your grammar isn't painful on the eyes: This is a play-by-post RP. It should be obvious your writing should be coherent and not eyebleed-worthy on the technical end if you want to join. On that note, though...

    • Be reasonable in your RPing: Note we don't say "good"; again, we accept anyone who likes creative writing, likes Pokemon, and is willing to work with aspects of the Pokemon franchise they're unfamiliar with, and we consider this fun writing practice anyway, so if you like to write but have cold feet regarding your skills, we heartily encourage you to give us a try! However, that doesn't mean you can drop a blatant Mary Sue/Gary Stu/etc. on us, start breaking in-universe rules willy-nilly, and call it a day. This is decidedly no high-end writing competition, but we still have standards you need to follow so everyone else can have fun too - and so you can learn to be a better writer. (tl;dr we don't give a crap about your R Ping skill level near as much as your rule-following skill level. Everybody's a beginner at some point, we don't judge you for that unless it means you start causing problems in-universe and/or out.)

    • Don't be sociopolitically insensitive: It should be clear that intolerance of any national, racial, ethnic, religious, LGBT+, neurodivergent, etc. group will not be tolerated in and of itself, especially if you pull stereotypically politically incorrect arguments out of your ass trying to defend yourself. Yes, yes, it sounds like stereotypical Tumblr, but we're a diverse group whose needs as a whole need to be respected. (This was the origional rule patch that eventually prompted this; let's just say the major problem involved serious violation of this.)

    This RP is meant to be a safe, enjoyable place for its members, and making people feel too uncomfortable over any of the above will require us to take action. We've banned people for these things and would rather not do so again, so while we'll accept genuine slipups to a point, if you outright aren't willing to comply with the above rules or the ones in the required reading later in this post... Sorry, find another RP. If you are willing... Great, let's get started!

    Okay, I'll play by the rules, I want in on this. How do I join?

    (Only slightly changed to reflect the above, but.)

    On a lighter note, you may notice the note on our slight preference for script format over prose due to Grandfather Clause is gone; as of today, this RP has no preference, so just go with whatever you're comfortable with. So I know what you're thinking, does this RP now work for prose? YES IT WORKS FOR PROSE! FEEL YOUR PROSE'S HEARTBEAT RIGHT NEXT TO YOURS! IT WORKS FOR PROOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOSE!!!!
