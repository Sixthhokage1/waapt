    Here it is.

    Shaun Sapphire

    Age: 17

    Gender: Cis male

    Ethnicity: White

    Appearance: Tall and lanky; soft, freckled face; thick, messy black hair; blue-grey eyes; aquamarine t-shirt; light grey trench coat; dark blue jeans; black sneakers; black backpack; gold-rimmed aviators; black belt w/ Pokeball holders.

    Former ties: Ex-apprentice breeder: Pokedex holder.

    Region of origin: Sunnyshore City, Sinnoh.

    Backstory: An average kid from Sunnyshore, Shaun didn't get his first Pokmon until his 13th birthday. As a gift from his teacher, a breeder, he got an egg and a Pokeball. After his egg hatched into a Piplup, Shaun made it his goal to train her and win every Sinnoh gym badge. Now, he has left for Alola, where he seeks new adventures and opponents. Shaun is witty, intelligent, and kind, yet also proud, snarky, argumentative and hot-tempered.

    Team

    Species: Empoleon (Starter)

    Nickname: Tsunami

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Torrent

    Bio: Tsunami has been with Shaun since she was an egg, and it shows. Originally, she was naive and carefree, but time has caused her to become knowledgeable and serious. Like her trainer, she is proud, snarky, and quick to anger, but she's also rash and hides a loving heart under her callous exterior. In battle, she focuses on dealing as much damage as she can - no matter the opponent.

    Species: Staraptor

    Nickname: Ace

    Gender: Male

    Ability: Intimidate

    Bio: The first Pokemon Shaun ever caught, Ace was seperated from his flock. When Shaun took him in, he was apprehensive, but realized he had a new flock - Shaun and Tsunami. He is somewhat of a Foil to the Empoleon, as both are proud of themselves and caring, yet he is calm, collected, and wise where she is rash and hot-tempered. In battle, he observes his opponent before deciding whether to attack or retreat.

    Species: Pikachu

    Nickname: Lightning

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Static

    Bio: A wild Pikachu who met Shaun in Sinnoh's Trophy Garden. She was drawn to him after seeing how kind he was to his other Pokmon. Shaun took her in simply because she looked different. She was orange rather than yellow and was also missing a chunk of her left ear. Lightning is somewhat shy and quiet, but is very protective of her loved ones.

    Species: Roselia

    Nickname: Ruby

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Natural Cure

    Bio: Ruby met Shaun in the Eterna Forest when she was still a Budew. She followed him through the forest since he seemed interesting to the young and carefree Grass/Poison Pokmon. Shaun noticed her and invited her to come with him. They formwd a bond, and she reached her true potential and evolved into a Roselia. She is a rather elegant and fancy Pokmon who prefers to relax rather than fight, but when she does, she prefers to put on a show.

    Species: Vulpix

    Nickname: Jessica

    Gender: Female

    Ability: Flash Fire

    Bio: Jessica met Shaun at the Sinnoh Day-Care. After hatching, her Trainer left her with the Day-Care couple. He never came back. Shaun noticed Jessica and immediately saw how unique she was - she only had 3 tails instead of 6. After a quick chat with the couple, Shaun adopted her, and the Genki Girl has been with him ever since. She is hyper, enthusiastic, and an optimist. In battle, she focuses on being a Glass Cannon and a Fragile Speedster.

    Species: Eevee

    Nickname: Jasper

    Gender: Male

    Ability: Run Away

    Bio: Jasper was a gift from Shaun's father before he left for Kanto. Unlike the other Pokmon, he dislikes Shaun. Jasper is rather haughty and stuck-up; he is distrusting of others and believes he should put himself first and foremost. Shaun is trying to win him over, but Shaun's not sure if he'll come around...
