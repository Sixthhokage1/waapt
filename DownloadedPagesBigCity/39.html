    Jet took a combat stance, staring Liepard down. "Let's."

    He spun up an Aqua Jet and flew at Liepard, who jumped into the air and used Quick Attack. Both Pokemon met in the air, clashing repeatedly and managing to somehow send up sparks with each hit. Finally a hit made Jet come out of his Aqua Jet and send two Razor Wind slashes at Liepard, who dodged them nimbly. The attack slashed through part of a rafter, causing it to fall and hit the ground with a loud crash.

    Jet and Liepard both came to ground, the Dark-type releasing his claws as Jet prepared Ice Punches on both of his fists.

    Both of them then lunged forward, sparks once again skating off of their attacks as they slashed and punched at one another, their blows meeting between them with neither giving any ground. Liepard swept an Iron Tail at Jet's feet, but the Buizel jumped into the air to avoid it. He swept down with an Ice Punch as Liepard swung another Iron Tail, the pair of them now beginning to resemble an orange-and-purple blur that constantly showered sparks and were swirling up dust.
