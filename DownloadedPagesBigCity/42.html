    Useless fools. All of them, Razor thought as he ran through the mist that covered the cliffs. Next time I'll get better help. More Pokemon like Liepard. They will understand.

    Finally, Razor came to a circular area on a small outcropping. Light streamed from the opposite side of the gulch, and a deep voice spoke to him.

    "Razor. Why have you returned?"

    Razor bowed respectfully, keeping his head pointed towards the ground. "Forgive me, my lord. I failed in my mission."

    There was a moment of silence before a younger voice than the first spoke up. "After all we gave you, you dare to return to us with this news?!"

    "Peace," the first voice said. "This news is regrettable, Razor. Fortunately, your Rare Candy trade will destabilize the balance of power in the city. You did do some good work."

    Razor smirked. "Thank you, my lord."

    "Yet, your failure outweighs your successes. I am afraid, Razor, that You Have Outlived Your Usefulness."

    "What?!" Razor cried, "But...but..." Screw it, he thought. He opened his mouth and fired a Shadow Ball into the cloud of mist, which split before it. Razor caught a green barrier popping into place for the ball to harmlessly splash over. He had time to widen his eyes before a Hyper Beam shot back out at him, hitting the outcropping. He only had time to scream before the cliff was obscured in smoke.

    The mist dispelled itself, the Dragon-types gathered there fully revealed. The Flygon that had spoken looked at Razor's killer. Their leader. Dragonite.

    "A pity," Dragonite said. "But no matter. We will continue with our plans and build upon what Razor succeeded in."

    "Who do we send next?" Flygon asked.

    "The Blades," Dragonite said, as if the answer was obvious. He turned to another dragon. "Salamence. Tell them."

    Salamence bowed his head and took off, flying out of the canyon through the mist, towards the city.
    That is the face of a man who just ate a kitten. Raw.
