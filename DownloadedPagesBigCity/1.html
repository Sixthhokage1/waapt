    The lake in the park felt amazing after a night of sleeping in a cramped pipe. Jet spun and twirled, and ocassionally breached the surface of the water. He snapped up Remoraid and other fishes, before finally coming out of the water and onto the shore. Jet went over to where he'd laid his clothes and began putting them on, drawing odd looks from a jogging couple.

    "Is that Buizel putting on pants?" asked one.

    "Looks like it," said the other. "Weird."

    Jet rolled his eyes and started running, going into the woods and across a street (staying on the crosswalk, drawing even more weird looks) into an alley. He kept going, eventually shimmying his way up a pipe to the roof of a store. He then laid on his back, closing his eyes and allowing the sun to dry him.

    "Can't catch me!" Fingers called as he scurried up the side of building. At the roof he looked back to blow a raspberry at the barking Poochyena in the alley below.

    SLAM!

    His taunting was interrupted by the fire escape behind him being forced open. Out of the dimly lit staircase slinked a battle-scarred Mightyena. Fingers gulped.

    "Did you really think you could steal from my pack?" growled Blackfang, head of the Fang Gang.

    "Yes," Fingers nodded confidently, show no weakness was a good rule of thumb. He swiped his tail in the direction of Blackfang. Stars erupted from it and homed in on the wolf but Fingers was already running in the other direction.

    A leap over the alley where the poochies were still barking, then a frantic dash. These roofs were too close to escape the pack, Fingers spotted his escape route. A hard turn right (and a pooch crashing into an air vent) and a leap off the roof. Into nothing.

    Well not nothing, Fingers' tail snapped out grabbing a streetlight and swinging him across the wide street. And safety.

    "Ha ha!" with a wave Fingers turned and continued going at a more manageable pace.

    Li'l Punk
    Kori was dozing on a wide window ledge, paws idly playing with her scarf, when she heard the commotion. Leaping to her feet, she looked around and scrambled up the side of the building, claws digging into the porous brick. On the roof, she straightened up and saw members of the Fang Gang chasing an Aipom.

    The Sneasel chuckled to herself, then ran across the roof and leaped out, landing on a lightpost and grabbing the pole extending over the street with one paw. She deftly leaned down and grabbed the main support pole and swung herself down. Ducking into an alley, she ran towards the sounds of barking. Leaping up onto a fence, she waited until the gang was passing underneath.

    A Water Gun knocked over several of the Poochyenas aside, but the leader, a Mightyena continued on. Kori dropped down in front of him and used Quick Attack to knock him into the side of the building, then Icy Wind to slow him up. She walked over to the Pokemon the gang had been chasing, a Buizel and an Aipom.

    "Hi," she said shortly. "I'm Kori, and the Fang Gang will be back in a few seconds. Maybe we should go and chat in a safer time and place?"
