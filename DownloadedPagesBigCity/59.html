    That all you got?
    Absol walked along a back street, heading into the city's east quarter. This was Fight Gang territory, but he didn't worry about attracting any undue attention. He knew the leader.

    There was a great crash from down the street and a Machoke flew past Absol, sliding across the ground and stopping after running into a pile of garbage bags. The Fighting-type moaned as Absol came out further and looked down the street from where the Machoke had come from. A Snorlax was on the other end of the street, being faced down by a Primeape and Breloom. The two were quaking in fear.

    "Now that was a punch," the Snorlax said smugly. The two Fight Gang members began to slowly back away, then turned and ran completely. The Snorlax started following them, laughing.

    Eventually, the two were cornered. The Snorlax raised another fist, energy building up around it. Absol recognized Mega Punch and shook his head in sympathy for the two Fight Gang members. The Snorlax threw the punch forward...

    Only to have it caught.

    Seemingly out of nowhere, a Lucario had appeared and caught the punch with one hand. He then grabbed the other hand and, muscles straining, roared with effort as he spun once and threw the Snorlax through the air, halfway down the street once more. The massive Normal-type hit the ground with an earth-shaking thud, and Lucario pointed down a side street. Breloom and Primeape dragged Machoke down it, and Lucario faced Snorlax down as they left.

    Snorlax got up and began charging a Hyper Beam. He fired it at Lucario, who once again lifted a hand and blocked it without any sign of effort. The Fighting/Steel type then charged forward, one fist glowing in a Focus Punch that smashed into Snorlax's cheek, rolling the skin up and sending the Normal-type flying. After he hit the ground, the Snorlax got up quickly and moved as fast as his bulk could carry him.

    Lucario then turned to Absol and grinned. "I didn't expect you to be there. How do you think I handled it?"

    "Considering it only took one blow from you to make him back down, very well." Absol replied, walking out of the alley towards his friend. "I have a favor to ask of you."

    "Truly?" Lucario asked. "What might that be?"

    "There is a group of young Pokemon that I've met up with that are in need of a safe haven. Something has its eyes set on the city and their old home isn't safe anymore. Take them in, train them if necessary. You take in orphans all the time."

    Lucario nodded. "It's not really a favor if I do it all the time, but very well. I'll take them in. Just lead them to my base."

    Absol nodded his thanks to Lucario, then took off into the back streets again.
