    Floaroma City

    "It is DONE!"

    Sakura, Lyuri and Hikari were all inside the old Riese household, which was now completely reformed into a new house full of life mostly due to all the Pokémon walking around doing stuff. One of those being Daisuke and Zack finding an old book covered in dust, with the former accidentally blowing all of the dust right in the face of the latter.

    "Man! How long have we been at this?" asked Hikari, energetically

    "Months, I think," said Sakura, sounding more neutral.

    "Why did we do all of this by ourselves again?" asked Lyuri, exasperated.

    "Who needs professional workers when you have a bunch of Pokémon?" asked Hikari, giggling

    "I don't know yours but I think most Pokémon aren't arquitets or stuff like that." Lyuri sighed.

    While they talked, Azure was going around carrying a big plate of Poképuffs upstairs where the Greninja Squad was waiting for her.

    "I wonder what everyone else has been up to, we haven't really been keeping in touch," said Sakura, tilting her head curiously, but also sounding a little bit concerned.

    "Well we would've been able to keep in touch with them if it wasn't for the fact that Lyuri accidentally threw our phones inside the garbage bag and throwed that into the incinerator!" said Hikari, still not letting go of her enthusiasm

    "<I'm still not convinced that was an accident.>" said Seth from a nearby room.

    While Lyuri proceeded to look away from everyone in shame, Duke and Grant, both in human form, then walked inside carrying painting equipment and both of them being covered in paint.

    "Well, the outside paint job is finished," said Duke, scratching his nose a little bit.

    "Man I look like a rainbow or something," said Grant, pulling his shirt to see just how much of it was now covered in paint, "it reminds me of the day Sakura tried to introduce me to make-up, that wasn't a fun day for anyone involved," he chuckled.

    This time it was Sakura's turn to get a little red and look away from everybody, with Hikari giving her and her sister a few pats on the head.

    "So, now that this is done, what are you all going to do?" asked Grant.

    "Well, the house is back in shape, so I guess I'll be staying here for a while, going back to the peaceful Floaroma life," said Sakura, getting a little less red and managing a small smile

    "I think Duke and I will be heading back to Unova, last time we were there was pretty fun so why not, huh?" asked Lyuri, stretching herself a little and smiling at Duke, who just smiled back.

    "And I'll go back to my work, it feels like a whole year passed where I did no work at all!" said Hikari.

    "So I guess we'll be parting ways now, huh?" asked Duke, looking a little bit saddened.

    The girls looked at each other, all pondering about it for a while thinking if there wasn't anything else they wanted to do together before they all went their separate ways, but there wasn't really anything that came to mind at the time. So with that, the trio nodded solemnly and gave each other goodby-

    -Record Needle Scratch-

    "Hold the phone," said Grant, "that's how the expression goes right?" he asked himself before shaking his head and focusing back on the more important matters, "Aren't you supposed to be getting married soon?" He asked, pointing at Hikari. "And aren't you two supposed to be getting married at the same time as her?" He pointed at Lyuri and Duke. "And aren't you supposed to be a superhero and have a hero sidekick who is your boyfriend who's off doing who knows what crazy stuff those people have gotten themselves into?" He finished, pointing at Sakura

    There was complete silence between the four, even the Pokémon nearby stayed silent waiting for their replies. Eventually the silence ended up broken by Sakura, who let out a sigh.

    "Well, I guess we have a lot of loose ends to tie up." She shrugged.
