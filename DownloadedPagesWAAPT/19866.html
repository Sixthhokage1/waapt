    Jublife, around midnight last night

    It slithered.

    Somewhat like a snake, somewhat like a lizard, even more wrong, it made its way through the cracks and gaps of Jublife City's streets, spewing blue and orange flames intermittently but keeping quiet.

    It didn't want to give itself away to its first victim.

    Umbra was having a restless night and had decided to go on a late-night walk. It wasn't just that the Glitch threat had postponed his and Ammy's wedding indefinitely; it was all the debacle entailed for Team Umbra, the J-Team s a whole, and them moving twoards their futures.

    A thought crossed his mind that it was almost Halloween and his worry may be because there were Ghosts about; thinking back to the Halloween his entire Ghost army from Ransei came back he couldn't help but chuckle.

    As much as it otherwise burdened him he was once the Ghost Lord. Ghosts were never more than an annoyance to him anymore.

    Then out of the corner of his eye he saw something approach. He turned and to his horror saw a thing vaugely resembling a flickering orange, black, and blue Charmander slithering on its belly. It warped and twisted into a standing Charmeleon and Charizard within seconds of Umbra making eye contact with it.

    Umbra's scream was quickly cut off as the thing collided with him.

    Ammy was sitting in her room in the Ark of Yamato, playing a round of Brigoo on a Wii U. She pumped her fist in the air.

    "Yes! Victory! Woo-hoo!"

    She put down the controller and looked around, confused.

    "Umbra said he'd be back by now... Hope he's feeling better enough for a round of-"

    Suddenly, a Mega Charizard X burst into the room, roaring as its eyes glowed blood red and glitchy patterns flickered across its body.

    Ammy screamed as it rushed her.
