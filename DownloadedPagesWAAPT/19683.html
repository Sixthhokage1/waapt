     New Disorder Base

    -Ammy slowly comes to and finds she's strapped to a metal chair with an Ampharos Trooper pointing a Pokesav jammer at her head-

    Human!Ammy: Erm, wh-where am I?

    Ampharos: <You're in a First Disorder containment unit, ma'am.>

    Human!Ammy: Who are you?

    Ampharos: <Unit WN-3411, ma'am. I'm Kylo Robin's personal assistant trooper.>

    Human!Ammy: ...I am assuming Kylo Robin himself is behind me.

    WN-3411: <Actually in front of you, ma'am.>

    -Ammy yelps as Kylo Robin emerges from the darkness and removes his hood, leaving only his mask.-

    Human!Ammy: W-what am I doing here?

    Kylo Robin:    Simple. You're my guest.   

    Human!Ammy: -grins nervously- Hehe, maybe we could have a cup of tea or a something, then?

    -gulps when he doesn't respond to her joke, then looks around-

    ...Where's everybody else?

    Kylo Robin:    You mean those thieves, murderers, and traitors you call friends? Sorry, don't know, don't care.   

    -At this Ammy scowls and Kylo Robin chuckles-

       That one got to you, didn't it?   

    Human!Ammy: More like some glitch freak is keeping someone I cared about behind your mask.

    Kylo Robin:    ...All right then. I'll show you...   

    -He takes off his helmet, revealing he's practically unchanged... Save for the crackling glitch energy streaming through his irises-

       ...I have nothing to hide.   

    Winston: -blinks- <They and Hux are the same species? These two knew each other...?>

    Human!Ammy: It... It is you...

    Kylo Robin:    Glad that's cleared up, then.   

    -he stands up and walks over towards her, eyeing her up and down-

       What do you know about the map?   

    Human!Ammy: You're wasting your time... We only have a fragment, one we can't even read, at that...

    Kylo Robin:    On the contrary; we salvaged what we needed from the Republic's records... And you, an insect, have the last piece.   

    -he smiles coyly, reaches down to stroke her hair and face, but she shudders as glitchy energy emerges from his fingers and digs into her head-

       Though, for the record, you really are quite pretty as what you are now... Yet I sense loneliness, doubt, tied to the one we both seek...   

    -he places both hands on her face and starts stroking with the other; she starts panting heavily-

       Oh, now we've got something, dear... There's an ocean... a mountain like a pillar...   

    -He moves his hand to the back of her neck as more glitch-tendrils dig in; she gasps-

       Oh, but you're scared... Scared you can never be a good mate, good friend, good anything for him or any other of your so-called friends...   

    Human!Ammy: R-Robin... Get out of my HEAD!

    -She jerks forward violently, he steps back and scowls-

    Kylo Robin:    I know you have that map in you; and if I want it, I'll get it!   

    -he pins her to the chair further and leans his face in close to hers as glitch tendrils emerge from his fingers and attempt to worm their way into her head-

    -WN-3411 staggers back, decidedly unnerved-

    Kylo Robin:    Don't be afraid. Don't resist. I feel it as much as you do.   

    Human!Ammy: You... You can't get it from me...

    Kylo Robin:    Oh, I will, don't you worry.   

    -The glitch tendrils dig deeper; Ammy lets out a scream but something in her flares at the Glitch's presence, causes red markings to appear on her body and her eyes to glow as energy courses up the tendrils, her expression shifting to a scowl-

    Human!Ammy: You forgot that leftover from when I was semi-divine, didn't you? Now I sense your own fear; that you can never re-achieve your own divine heights, that you will never become great within this Glitch army!

    -Kylo Robin screams himself as he retracts his tendrils back; just as quickly he yells in anger generating glitch claw constructs that he lashes out with to slam Ammy's head into the side of her chair and leave large bloody gashes in the side of her cheek-

    -He then sneers and heads out of the room. WN-3411 looks on horrified as Ammy recovers enough from the blow to start sobbing uncontrollably-

    Hux!Lina: -strides in the room smugly as Kylo Robin glowers at her- Kylo assumed it would be useless with the girl in hand; it's likely currently in Resistance hands with the map. Thankfully, we've tracked them to the Indiana system.

    Prometheus: ~Excellent. Now is the time to crush them; prepare the weapon for an even greater strike.~

     Interrogation Room

    -Ammy is still sobbing when she senses something approaching-

    Human!Ammy: -panicking- St-Stay AWAY from me!

    WN-3411: <Woah, woah, it's OK... Ammy, was it?>

    Human!Ammy: D-Don't play nice to me! You're loyal to that horrible thing that turned my f-friend into a monster!

    WN-4311: <Kylo was your... Friend?>

    Human!Ammy: ...

    ...Y-Yes... His name used to be Robin, Robin Loptyr... He was stranded helpless in our world by forces beyond his control, among them the Glitch... We helped him survive, adapt, find a new way to belong... But now the Glitch turned him into a horrible creature that just... just...

    -She closes her eyes and clenches her teeth to prevent more tears coming, but they do, stinging the bloody gashes on her face-

    Please... I want away... I just-

    -stops and opens her eyes in shock as her restraints are removed-

    -she gets up in turn to see WN-3411 had done the job-

    What are-

    WN-3411: <You said it yourself. You want away. I'm getting you out of here.>

    Human!Ammy: But... But you said you were loyal to-

    WN-3411: <I am. But you've changed my idea of what that means.>

    -he removes his helmet to reveal his Space Ampharos face and looks at her forlornly-

    <I was hatched and raised to serve him and him alone, cater to his every need. I thought I knew everything there was to know about him. But I never saw him act the way he did to you, it terrified me, gave me doubt. And if you're saying I've never seen the real him... I'm thinking you're on to something. And so I want you to help me find the real him.>

    -Ammy stares at him a while before nodding and removing herself from the chair-

    Human!Ammy: ...All right. I will.

    -WN-3411 nods back and tosses her a blaster-

    WN-3411: <He'll be back any minute. Follow me.>
