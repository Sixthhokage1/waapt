    Trans Terminus Homo 
    Outside Hearthome, Sergey's Camp - Nighttime

    Currently the whole camp was sleeping. Magnus and Sergey were sitting back to back while sleeping, both having a smile on their faces... likely dreaming of the contest a few days in the past. Aicha currently was used by Loran as a cushion. It was outside the camp though were the interesting things were happening right now... a bit away a certain Absol stood together with a certain Houndoom

    Ammon Ra: <What are you doing here?>

    Absol: <...>

    A small growl courtesy of Ammon

    Ammon Ra: <Hmpf, whatever, as long as you don't cause trouble for them I don't care anyway.>

    Absol: <...>

    Ammon Ra: <Not a big talker eh? Hmpf, all the better.>

    He lied down... suddenly his ears twitched

    Ammon Ra: <What was that?>

    He looked besides him but the Absol was gone. Instead he spotted two Kricketune sneaking into the camp.

    Ammon Ra: <Hey, you! Get away from there.>

    The two Pokémon turned around

    Kricketune: <Seems we've been spotted.>

    Kricketune: <What we're doing is none of your business.>

    Ammon Ra: <You've got to be kidding me, I'm giving you one cha->

    He didn't get farther before being hit by a Fell Stinger by one of the Kricketune

    Kricketune: <Quick, you steal the food, I'll stall that one.>

    Kricketune: <Gotcha.>

    So while one of them ran past him into the direction of the camp the other stayed attacking Ammon again.

    Ammon Ra: <Hmpf fine, you're in trouble now, big time.>

    He used an Inferno on the Kricketune

    Ammon Ra: <Hell no.>

    At that time he was being hit by a surprisingly powerful Night Slash that send him flying into the air towards the camp... and with that towards the other Kricketune

    Ammon Ra: <Big Mistake.>

    Instead of tumbling through the air Ammon managed to change his motion around to make a few loops, landing directly before the second Kricketune.

    Ammon Ra: <You're going down!>

    He used Overheat on the Pokémon which was KO'd by the hit.

    He looked at the other one that now hid a bit in the distance

    Ammon Ra: <There is a reason I was the strongest for a long time. Back off if you don't want that to happen to you... hmpf, or come at me.>

    Eventhough the Kricketune seemed stronger than it looked judging by the Night Slash it delivered seeing its partner go down in one hit was enough to convince it to turn and run away.

    Ammon Ra just trotted to his old position on a rock... only to find the Absol there

    Absol: <...>

    Ammon Ra: <Hmpf... fine.>

    He lied down besides the Absol, silently watching over the camp

    Achievement Obtained: "Rocket jumps"
