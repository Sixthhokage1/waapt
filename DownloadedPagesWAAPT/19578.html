The beings she has chosen to model herself after are Keres and Bellona.

    Relations:

    Save is the twin sister of Reset. They are very close, with each others strengths and weaknesses complimenting each other.     Keres is the one who brought Save an Reset to the Chateau.

Save is a terrible influence on Huet. Hes become much more rebellious and disagreeable from the ideals instilled into his young influence able mind.

    Save has a rocky relationship with Bellona. She likes Bellona as a being, but not in too large doses. Save highly dislikes how Bellona tends to pick on Reset and has tried to get her to stop in the past.

    Most Common Location:

    The Tv room: R2

    Notes for Handling:

    Save is easily angered and hard to reason with. If anything is wanted of her, it is best to use Reset as a mouthpiece, so to speak. Do not bring up the Valley Windworks around her. Do not mess with Reset. Do not insinuate that she is a bad sister. Do appeal to her ego, praise gets you places with her, albeit in a slightly morally questionable way.
