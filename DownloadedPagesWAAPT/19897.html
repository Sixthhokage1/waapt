    Henshin Hopeful
    Route 209, night

    EJ stood in the crater, face illuminated by the weakening fire. Above and around him stood a veritable night parade of Ghost Pokemon. Duskulls and Gastly flanked Dusclops, Haunters, and even a few Gengar, all stared at him balefully.

    "Wonderfu-URGH!" He clutched his head, reeling in pain as a monstrous voice spoke through his very mind- though he doubted the message was for him specifically.

       ~TAKE HIM, MY CHILDREN. TAKE HIM, THAT HE MAY SUFFER FOR OUR GRIEVANCES.~   

    He opened his eyes wide, a small mercy that allowed him to see that the various ghosts had closed the gap in the mere moments and were upon him. A particularly zealous Gastly came at him first, slavering tongue hanging out... and was promptly grabbed by the face. The man was lit in silhouette by the fire, but his eyes burned like the coals behind him. He squeezed, causing the Gastly to cry out in pain, before flinging it into a Haunter, sending them both into the wall of the crater.

    "You're like that Snover, huh?" He took a step forward, causing the ghosts in front of him to take a step back- how had he even managed to grab them like that? "More Pokemon that hate me for some reason." A black haze began to flow from around him, extinguishing the fire. His eyes were still burning in the darkness.

       "Perhaps the feeling is mutual."    He leapt forward with a snarl as the Ghosts steeled themselves and pounced on him.
