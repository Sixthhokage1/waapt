    Coast, North of Snowpoint

    Rill: <It's cold.>

    Dune: Tell me about it.

    -Dune squints his eyes as he attempts to read the message on his P*DA over again.-

    Hey, champ. Heard you were in Sinnoh, so I sent this message ahead of time since I figured someone would drag you up to Snowpoint. No other reason you'd be up in the cold.

    Anywho, word on the Sylph Co. grapevine tells me that something weird is going on up near one of our old facilities. And by old, I don't just mean "before the internet". I mean "before you were born" old. North of Snowpoint, up along the coast, is an island that I don't ever recall being officially named. It's listed in the Sylph Co. database as Site 022A, and it used to be a seed bank.

    Yes, a seed bank. As in, a place people would place berries and seeds and other reproduction-necessitating biology. It got shut down about fifteen years ago when the advent of databoxing started picking up steam, making an actual physical location up in Arceus-knows-where kinda redundant and not very cost effective (unless there's a massive planet-wide EMP, but nobody at the board meeting really payed attention to that part). Anywho, we shut that place down back at the turn of the millennia, and apparently scavengers still like picking through the place for some trophy to take home. At least, until a few months ago.

    The first report that came my way was back in January, but there might have been more incidents beforehand. People coming back showing signs of Post Traumatic Stress Disorder - like they'd been through a warzone. Twitching, paranoia, flashbacks, panic attacks - all from people that had acquaintances that swore up and down that they were in perfectly sound mental health before visiting Site 022A.

    Now, I know you hate the cold with the passion of a solar corona, but you're the only person I know with any experience with crazy nonsense like this. We issued a "Deadly" level travel warning for the island, but that's obviously not gonna stop people from going there or whatever's in there from getting nastier. I'd owe you big time if you could go up there and see what's what.

    -Dr. Mesquite

    -Dune sighs as he feels Rill give a solid heave as he puts a foot on solid ground.-

    Rill: <We're here. Any idea where the entrance to this food vault is?>

    -Dune dismounts, careful not to get any of the hellishly cold water on himself.-

    Dune: Seed vault, Rill. And yeah, there should be waymarkers showing the way up.

    -Dune scans the rocky terrain, evergreens dotting the steep slopes. He sees a yellow flash. Dune stops there for a second and starts counting in his head. One. Two. Three. Blink. That's the beacon.-

    Dune: That way.

    Rill: <Can I go back into the ball now?>
