    Erin: Great! Let's continue. Swampy, use Mud Slap!

    Swampy does. The Marill cups its hands over it's face, their vision impaired. After a moment, they attempt to use Rollout... And sadly miss Swampy, who had taken the opportunity to dodge. They went flying into a tree.

    The Marill quickly gets back up.

    Marill: <...Pretend that never happened.>

    Instead of making another suicidal charge, said Marill simply runs up to Swampy.

    Erin: Swampy, use Bide!

    ...As soon as Erin said those words, the Marill halted it's tracks and used Defense Curl. It's vision seemed to be clearing. After a moment or two, Swampy had finished using Bide.

    Swampy: <...Of course.>

    The Marill, now able to see, used Tackle.

    Erin: Swampy, use Protect!

    Swampy successfully protected against the Marill's Tackle before following it up with their own.

    Satisfied, Erin took out one of her umbrellas. Thankfully, the Poke Ball inside fell straight into her hand, instead of hitting Swampy or something. She threw it. The Pokemon was caught without much resistance.

    Immediately afterwards, Erin sent out the Pokemon.

    Erin: I'm sorry about that...

    Marill: <Is she always like this?>

    Swampy: <She's still getting used to the idea that Pokemon are sapient. Not that she did not talk to us before...>

    Erin: Hmm, what should I call you?
