    Trans Terminus Homo

    Hearthome, Contest Hall

    Sergey: "Okay, this is it, our big day."

    Sergey adjusted the grey Tailcoat Mireille gave him for a last time while Magnus still was shiny - in more than one meaning of the word. Currently the lights on the stage were off, soon they would light up and their performance would start. Sergey was even more nervous than back-stage now, they both had trained more than hard, but there were so many other contestants and their performances were all really good.

    As if on cue the lights went on and Sergey could see the audience, his heart was beating like crazy and he was sure that it must have been so loud that Magnus could hear it, eventhough he was standing one or two meters before him. But still he managed to collect himself in time. Both he and Magnus bowed deeply.

    Sergey: "For this I need a volunteer on the stage, would anyone be willing to do this?"

    After a bit a girl raised her arm

    Girl: "I would be willing."

    The spotlights were on her as she made her way to the stage.

    Sergey: "Thank you..."

    Girl: "Alyesha."

    Sergey: "Okay, thank you Alyesha then. Please, just stand here and make a good pose for Magnus."

    Alyesha nodded, not really sure what would happen now nonetheless not questioning Sergey and striking a good pose.

    Sergey gave a last sigh, now it would begin. He gave Magnus a last reassuring smile and was met with a steady gaze speaking of his confidence. With a last nod he let the show begin.

    Sergey: "Go!"

    With that the music he brought started playing

    Magnus lost no time as he brought down a man-sized rock with Rock Slide. The moment the stone made ground contact Magnus already rushed past it with his blades extended, making his first cut.

    He had trained long and hard for this and it showed, gracefully - Leaf Blade after Leaf Blade, Psycho Cut after Psycho Cut he managed to chip away piece after piece of the boulder, his performance looking half like some sort of dance. The at first kinda unshapely rock looked more and more like a human and then from a generic humanoid figure it was transformed into the shape of Alyesha who stood a bit away from the thing. While he did this Sergey did not have to utter many commands, Magnus knew what he was doing.

    Magnus: Leaf Blade now... land, 98° turn right, jump back with another Leaf Blade... spin. Jump above... Psycho Cut... now! Gently at the face... Leaf Blade. Behind the statue... 180° and jump! Psycho Cut for the arm... done. Another one, and another one! Spin and Jump! Turn clockwise while spinning. Leaf Blade!

    And so it went, the statue looking more and more like Alyesha.

    After the statue had more or less its final form Magnus used Psychic to make the whole dust that was created from the rock levitate. It formed what looked like a silvery band in the air - said band was used by Magnus to file away last irregularities on the surface, to the audience it looked like the band was barely touching the statue as it was rushing past it, forming ornate forms in the air. In the end he made a last jump directly over the statue as he used the Psychic to create what looked like a silvery explosion of dust behind him, after that he made a nearly perfect Three-Point Landing directly before the statue.

    Magnus: <YOUR HERO HAS RETURNED! THREE CHEERS FOR THE BARD!>
    One may feel fear in the face of danger, so long as one banishes fear when danger actually arrives.
