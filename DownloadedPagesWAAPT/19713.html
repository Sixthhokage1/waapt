    Pastoria City, Sea Landing

    A speedboat, with four occupants, pulls up to the landing. Three of its passengers disembark, the pilot speeding away as soon as they're off.

    One of them, the apparent leader, is a man in a tailored, midnight purple suit. He wears a white eyepatch over his left eye, and immediately pulls out a cigarette as soon as his feet touch the dock. His icy gaze sweeps over the town, as if assessing its intrinsic value.

    Over his right shoulder, his subordinate reaches forward with a lighter to light the smoke. He's dressed in an off the rack suit, black, and slightly bulked out by the body armor underneath. He has a hard face, harder than his boss.

    Over his other shoulder, his other subordinate hangs up her cellphone. Sighing, she slips it back into her pocket. She's in jeans and a concert t-shirt, for some death metal band.

    She sighs at her boss smoking.

    Subordinate F: Sir, those are going to kill you, eventually.

    The man in the nice suit chuckles.

    Boss: You know, Suzume, if the cigarettes are what get me, I'll count myself lucky.

    Suzume: Fair enough, I suppose.

    Their third member grunts.

    Subordinate M: Are you sure its here, boss? I mean, sure the town has a gym but... this is still practically a swamp.

    Boss: Reginald, friend, if I wasn't sure, I wouldn't have come myself.

    Concern assuaged, the man grunts in acknowledgement.

    Kyle Warden: At the moment, my biggest concern is not running into my son. I hear he's back from the dead, again. Either way, I don't think we're ready yet to meet face to face. In any case, the artifact we're after is supposedly kept under lock and key at the gym. We'll check it out tomorrow, see what we can dig up.

    Suzume: Very well, sir. We are lodged at the local Pokemon Center. The paid rooms, not the free ones, of course.

    Kyle raises an eyebrow.

    Suzume: I know, I know, not my first choice either, but I couldn't find a hotel that wouldn't draw attention. We're hiding in plain sight, here.

    Kyle: Oh, I don't doubt the validity of your decision, dear. I'm only wondering... staying in a Pokemon Center is the easiest way I know to bump into a member of the J-team. Ah well, so be it.

    The three make their way to the Pokemon Center for the night.

    With that, Munn's dad enters the scene! What is he after? Who are his subordinates? What is he planning? FIND OUT NEXT TIME... that I feel like posting. Probably tomorrow, who knows.
