    Duniree's Suite

    *Pop!*

    -Dune smiles as the toaster shoots a pair of golden brown slices of wheat toast up out of its slots. He takes them out of the toaster - quickly, it's still really hot - and drops them onto a plate. He drops a slice of butter on each.-

    Dune: Oh, this is gonna make her morning.

    Deziree: Why toast?

    -Dune shoots Deziree a smile.-

    Dune: It's finger food. You can't expect her to respect food utensils when she's as emaciated as she is. Plus, it's simple - we won't need to worry about overloading her system with a load of nutrients.

    -He takes a glass out of the cupboard and pours a glass of milk into it before slapping... a sippy top on it?-

    Deziree: ... Huh?

    Dune: Just watch.

    -Dune takes the plate and cup and heads into the side bedroom. The second he opens the door, a head pops out from the bed fort in the corner. Purple eyes track the plate in Dune's hands like a Mandibuzz.-

    Dune: Breakfast.

    -The girl gives Dune a quizzical look.-

    Dune: Break. Fast.

    -The girl still doesn't seem to get it as Dune sets the plate on the ground and sets the cup next to it.-

    Dune: Yours.

    -He steps back a bit before sitting down on the floor. The girl looks around before slowly pulling herself out of the pile of sheets and over the mattress. She crawls over to the plate, smells the toast, and proceeds to stuff it into her mouth as fast as she can.-

    Dune: I'd tell you to slow down, but...

    -She eventually does, though not willingly - a chunk of bread gets stuck in her chest, and she gives a chesty cough. Dune picks up the bottle of milk and hands it to her.-

    Girl: ...?

    -She looks at the cup and cap in confusion. She looks at Dune, who gives a drinking motion. After a moment, she puts her lips to the sippy cap, and proceeds to fall into bliss.-

    Dune: *snerk*

    -Dune can't help but chuckle as the little girl's eyes roll up in ecstasy and she falls back onto her back, sipping on the cup as fast as she can. She only leaves a few moments to breath between mouthfuls of milk.-

    Dune: I'll just leave you to it, then.

    -Dune gets up and heads out, closing the door as quietly as he can behind him.-

    Deziree: ... How are you doing this?

    -Dune blinks and turns. Deziree's befuddled face meets him.-

    Dune: What?

    Deziree: How are you accounting for all of that? The toast, the sippy cup so she wouldn't drown herself in milk or spill it everywhere. How?

    -Dune shrugs.-

    Dune: I dunno. Just seems like common sense.

    -Deziree almost looks insulted.-
    A single dish, providing a balance of nature's blessing: meat, grains, and vegetables. How could anyone hate such a thing?
