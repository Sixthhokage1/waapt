    Finally, Glendower went head to head with Enigma in a contest of psychic power - which ended up a draw. Since that was the final match, the game was decided as being won by the J-Team!

    Then, the confrontation with Lazarus! Going one on one, Munn took on his former aspect in a battle of first Pokemon, and then fists. Mid-battle, Munn renounced his current "patrons" saying:

    For too long have I been a sword in someone else's hand! For too long have I been a blade bared violent to the world! No more. Today, I take back my freedom.

    Having done that, he fraught Lazarus to a stand-still. In the end, Lazarus' robot body blew up from overstress. However, the rest of the Seven Swords escaped, Glendower being sure to retrieve the Eclipse Robe.

    Once the Seven Swords left, Munn was captured by agents of his former "patrons", saying that having renounced their protection, his existence on this plane is now forfeit. They then warped away with him, presumably to some prison-subdimension.
