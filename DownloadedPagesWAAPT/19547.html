    EJ stopped at a riverbank, a somewhat uncomfortable look on his face.

Pulling his fleece out of his backpack, he put it on and flipped the hood over his eyes, ready to drift away... Until a jet of water soaked his hood and face, sending him up with a yelp! Yanking it out of his eyes, he looked around furiously for the culprit, quickly spotting an angry-looking navy blue fish Pokemon.

    "Aw geez, did I clean my socks in your water?" he asked aloud, getting a frenzied volley of "Finii!"s and "Eon!"s as a response. He winced, pulling out Filbert's Pokeball. "Guess there's nothing for it... Go, Filbert!" He opened the ball, and the Chespin jumped out, ready to fight... And then immediately shocked and disoriented, chattering in confusion and looking around with a half-panicked look on his face- which isn't helped by the water jet he takes to the face immediately after.

    "Come on Filbert, focus! Use Tackle!" EJ called out, trying to get his partner's head back in the game, but it was no good- the Chespin was hopelessly disoriented by the completely alien sights and smells on what should have been a well-traveled road he was more than used to, and he wasn't in the right headspace to receive any commands, much less fight.
