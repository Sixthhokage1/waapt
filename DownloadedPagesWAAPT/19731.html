    Knocked down twice but she Rose again 
    Months ago, a short time after the events of the Delta Episode

    A hopeless fight. A sneak attack. A blur.

    The events leading up to Kieran's — no, Eskay's, at the time — fall were already a blur. He'd passed out from overexertion immediately after losing the Purple Sword, and by the time he woke up, that was all that was on his mind.

    As it turns out, being drunk on power has its own withdrawal symptoms, which certainly didn't help his recovery. After the Sword was taken from him, he felt empty. Like he lost the one thing that gave him any strength. And he didn't get a glimpse of who took the ancient weapon from him, but he had his theories. Naturally, all those theories pointed to his twin sister, Sarah. And naturally, he came up with this conclusion in mere seconds. So the moment he was conscious again, he bolted upright and glared at his already-awake twin.

    "Where is it?" he demanded. Sarah sighs as she hears his voice, and turns to face him.

    "It doesn't matter anymore," she says as calm as she can manage, although the quiver in her voice betrays her and shows how utterly scared she is. Eskay frowns, and stands up.

    "Tell me where it is, Sarah," he says, his tone getting more bitter. "Tell me what you did with it."

    "I didn't do anything," Sarah insists. "I never had it— "

    "DON'T LIE TO ME!" Eskay growls and grabs Sarah by the coat collar, prompting her to force him away. Unfortunately, it doesn't take much for things to escalate, and in moments, they're at each other's throats, tumbling out of the window. Many of their Poke Balls fall outside as well, and open upon hitting the ground, releasing Spectrum, Holly, and Jazz. They all look around,

    It takes a few moments for either of them to regain their senses from the fall, but when they do, they notice Eskay is gripping his Rainbow Wing tightly.

    "...Eskay, don't you dare," Sarah pleads. In defiance, he indeed dares. Out of sheer desperation, he holds the feather in the air, and it glints in the sunlight before shining brightly.

    In mere moments, a massive rainbow bird descends upon them through a brilliant beam of light, shining in every color of the rainbow.

    Knocked down twice but she Rose again 
    Months ago...

    "What is the meaning of this?!" Ho-oh demands. He looks at the Rainbow Wing, then at the two feuding twins. Sarah immediately kneels before him.

    "Ho-oh, s-sir," Sarah says, her voice shaking, "I-I had no idea he would do anything this d-drastic"

    Ho-oh shakes his head. "I had hoped Xerneas was exaggerating. It's quite clear, however, that he was not."

    Eskay scowls. "Ho-oh, I summoned you to—"

    "To do what, exactly?" Ho-oh interrupts Eskay, looming over him, wings burning. "To take revenge on your sister? As if you believe you ever had any right ordering me what to do? In what possible universe do you think you're above me in any way?"

    At this point, Eskay is trembling. "I-I thought, with the Rainbow Wing— "

    "The Rainbow Wing is to be used to call me when absolutely necessary! It is a sacred gift that should only be used for guidance, or for incidents that defy mortal ability, and I should have you incinerated on the spot for attempting to use it for petty revenge on your own family!"

    The twins both fall silent. Ho-oh sighs and shakes his head.

    "However, I am aware that, until recently, you have been under the influence of an ancient artifact notorious for corrupting those with the power to use it. So I will not destroy you like I am usually inclined to do but I will strip you of your Heart of Gold. Your sister is a better candidate for it anyway, and I'm not quite sure why she didn't receive it in your split"

    He raises his wings, and both twins become surrounded by columns of rainbow flames reaching seemingly endlessly up through the sky. After several seconds, the flames disappear. Eskay falls to his knees, a look of shock on his face.

    Ho-oh sighs and turns away. "Ugh, maybe I should take a page out of Mew Prime's book and take a vacation, Xerneas did mention a few nice spots Spectrum?" He turns toward the Flygon, who buzzes in curiosity.

    "Spectrum, would you be willing to fill in for my duties while I'm away? I'm sure Nix will be happy to have you around."

    Spectrum blinks, then beams. "Oh! I missed lil' Nixxy, I'd love to! Oh, I have so much to tell her about, so many stories!"

    Ho-oh nods. "I'm sure she'll love them. I promise, I will reward you for your service." He turns around and faces the twins again. "Sarah, I am leaving you with the Rainbow Wing, as long as you keep it out of your brother's reach. I believe I can at least trust you, correct?"

    Sarah nods, stammering. "I-I promise I won't let a-anything like this happen a-again"

    "Good," Ho-oh says, taking flight. "Spectrum, would you follow me, please?"

    "Okay!" Spectrum replies happily, taking flight as well. The twins, as well as Jazz and Holly, watch as Ho-oh and Spectrum fly toward Johto.

    "...And she's gone again," Jazz says flatly, breaking a long, long silence. Sarah sighs, and pulls Eskay back toward the Pokemon Center.
