    493600 Herbert40k18th Aug 2016 03:51:58 PM from Merry Olde England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I-It's not like I like you, or anything!
    The Valkyrie 
    Route 212

    Lucius, still slightly shellshocked after his Legendary encounter from yesterday, is currently trying to clear his head by strolling through the routes near Pastoria with Bishop by his side.

    Lucius: I don't know what's more unbelievable. That I actually spoke to Palkia yesterday, or that she's about as mature as a middle schooler who's just learnt their first swear word.

    Bishop: <You were a lot more awestruck at the time.>

    Lucius: Yeah, because it's hard to be honest when talking to a Pokemon that could erase you from reality with barely a second thought. Think about it. Isn't it kind of messed up that our lives are effectively ruled over by a race of omnipotent beings with limitless power who can mess with our lives however they please? I mean, I know I'm only here because of Yveltal's intervention, but it's not like I was given much choice in the matter. And if I remember rightly, Dialga gave Tagg the powers he used to... to...

    Bishop: <...I remember. We all remember.>

    Lucius: Right. So... Dialga's still responsible for a good share of the bloodshed in Orre, even if they didn't explicitly give Tagg the A-OK. Call me crazy, but I don't like the idea that when it comes to human life, the powers that be give less of a shit than a cork suppository. That seems kinda-

    He stops as he sees movement ahead. Further along the path, two trainers - one male and one female, both wearing red jackets with a Moltres mural emblazoned on the back - are wandering through the tall grass.

    Valor Member 1: Anything nearby?

    Valor Member A: I'm afraid not. Nothing but more Pidgey.

    Valor Member 1: How come there's so many Pidgey everywhere? I swear they aren't even native to Sinnoh!

    Lucius: Uh, I hate to ask a question to which I will immediately regret knowing the answer, but what are you two doing?

    The two Valor Members spin around to face Lucius.

    Valor Member 1: Why, we're liberating this area from the evil clutches of Team Mystic, of course!

    Valor Member A: ...Actually, we're carrying out research on behalf of Professor Willow.

    Valor Member 1: But mostly the "kicking Mystic's ass" thing.

    Lucius tilts his head.

    Lucius: ...I've never heard of a Team Mystic. They're not some new evil team going around stealing people's Pokemon, are they? Because Sinnoh's had enough of those already.

    Valor Member A: Nothing of the sort. They, along with Team Instinct, are also working for Professor Willow in a research capacity.

    Valor Member 1: Except they totally suck at it.

    Valor Member A sighs at her partner.

    Valor Member A: Our mission is to capture as many wild Pokemon as possible and send them back to Willow's lab. The rarer the Pokemon, the better.

    Lucius: ...Sounds shady.

    Valor Member 1: Oh no, it's totally legit! Professor Willow just needs a lot of wild Pokemon for his research.

    Lucius: What kind of research?

    Valor Member 1: Y'know. Research research.

    Lucius: ...Uh-huh.

    Valor Member 1: Hey, I'm not a scientist. I just catch the Pokemon.

    Lucius: So if all the teams are working for the same Professor, why do you hate each other?

    Valor Member 1: Because they're stupid and dumb and have stupid dumb faces.

    Valor Member A: We differ on how best to unlock the potential of Pokemon. We believe that a Pokemon's true potential can only be unlocked by bolstering its strength, whereas Mystic believe that logic is the key to understanding them.

    Lucius: And Instinct?

    Valor Member A: Instinct believe that heelys are still cool.

    Valor Member 1: Seriously. Have you even seen their leader?

    Lucius: I haven't even seen your leader.

    Valor Member 1: Oh, right. In that case, I'm pretty sure I have a photo of her on me somewhere...

    He rummages around in his bag for a moment, then pulls out a picture of Candela and hands it to Lucius.

    Lucius: ...Whoa.

    Valor Member 1: That's what I said!

    Lucius: See, you should've led with that. That would've made for a way better recruitment pitch.

    Valor Member A: Now that you mention it, it probably would've been a better idea to introduce you to our leader right out of the gate rather than keeping them shadowy and mysterious until later.

    Valor Member 1: So, are you ready to join the awesomeness that is Team Valor?

    Lucius: Uh, no. No matter how disturbingly attractive your leader is, this whole thing still sounds sketchy as hell.

    Valor Member 1 gasps and folds his arms.

    Valor Member 1: You're still calling us sketchy? After we made every effort to extend the branch of friendship to you? Team Valor will not forgive this insult!

    Lucius chuckles wryly.

    Lucius: What, are you going to sic your horde of Pidgey on me?

    Valor Member 1: Of course not. We're nowhere near a gym.

    Lucius: You can have battles outside gyms, you know.

    Valor Member 1: No you can't.

    Lucius: ...Except you can, though.

    Valor Member 1: Don't be silly. What kind of crazy lawless region do you come from?

    Lucius: This one, and it's definitely not as crazy and lawless as some-

    He pauses, then narrows his eyes at the pair.

    Lucius: ...Hang on. Don't I know you two from somewhere?

    The two Valor Members blink, glance at one another, then look back at Lucius.

    Valor Member 1 Mook: ...Uh, nope. Definitely not. Never seen you before in our lives. And even if we had-

    Valor Member A Val: Mook.

    Mook: -we'd want you to know that we've turned over a new leaf, and that any group you might associate us with is all in the past since we realised-

    Val: Mook, we're leaving.

    She grabs Mook by the wrist and drags him away.

    Mook: Hey! What are you doing? My PokeRadar says a Charmeleon just popped up somewhere around here!

    Val: I don't care. I'm in no hurry to get on that kid's bad side again.

    Lucius watches the pair go.

    Lucius: ...As far as strange encounters go, that seemed pretty mundane compared to yesterday.

    Bishop: <You're not going to go after them?>

    Lucius: Nah. Even if they are full of crap, digging up the past never ends well for me.

    He carries on walking.
