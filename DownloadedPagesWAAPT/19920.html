    Well not exactly Christmas-y, unless you count the gift of attempting to resolve plots in a timely manner, but here's another condensed solo plot post! Hopefully it's not too huge of a wall of text for people

    Unknown series of caverns - (previously)

    Once the group from the grasslands has returned to the village, they find out that Ignia has been busy talking to King and Jack, her sort of jailers, and explained what she knows of the Doctor's plans. "Blitz" immediately sets on making plans to hatch the viable 'Sky dragon' egg in the Egg Corridor so the group can escape to the surface, but Dr Booster reveals that the Doctor has already located the seeds to a breed of red berries unique to these caverns and plans to use them to power up the members of the Mimiga tribe he's already captured to use as soldiers, at the cost of the Mimiga's sanity. "Blitz's" immediate concern turns toward whether the human turned Darumaka patterned Mimiga, Ignia, would also be affected by the berries. Doctor Booster then addresses the fact that nobody in the group of scientists and children is cut out for combat, and with the Mimiga apparently preferring to stay as far away from the Doctor as possible, that leaves only one option...

    He points out the gear Tori carriesnote (Especially a metal brick with a heart-shaped stamp on it that she'd been using as a belt buckle, but is in fact a personal force field generator) and his belief that she is, in fact, a soldier sent into the caves some time in the past and now suffering amnesia. Despite Tori's claims to the contrary based on her memories of home, Dr Booster insists that she's the only one there that might have a chance of going through the Sand Zone to get to the berry seeds before and destroy them before the Doctor can act on his plans for war and domination. She concedes on that point and sets out alone through the teleporter.

    Once arriving Tori finds that the Sand Zone is aptly named, being a large subterranean desert with few immediate signs of life beyond some annoying Flygon. And a human figure the retreats as soon as she rounds a corner. Curious she explores in that direction to find a door built into the sandstone-like wall of the cavern

    -Inside the room that looks something like a bar in the desert is a red haired young man with glasses, and 3 young Mimiga tribe Buneary. All 4 of them turn as Tori enters-

    Lane(?): "....You... Look familiar"

    Tori: -!-

    "Lane! You recognize me? What are you doing here?"

    Lane(?): "Yeah..."

    -Pulls out a red gun-

    "You're another butcher from the surface, here to kill these Mimiga, aren't you?!"

    Tori: "Ah- wait what?!"

    Lane(?): "I won't let you hurt these kids!"

    .........

    After a brief duel between a girl with a zappy pistol, and a boy with a zappy machine-gun, and a few attempted assists by the kids, Tori is able to wear them out enough to explain that she DIDN'T come to kill anyone. Though "Lane" doesn't quite believe her story about how she got to the caves, he concedes that soldier or not, Tori is a friend. Unfortunately "Lane" has no hints about berry seeds, but suggests Tori look for an old woman named Jenka, something of a witch in the Sand Zone who keeps to herself.

    Tori decides to do so. And after another encounter with "Maria" resulting in a battle with a mechanical Golem, she opens the way and finds a small house sitting in a secluded corner of the desert-cavern. Jenka nearly refuses to talk to Tori at all once she mentions looking for the berry seeds, and instead complains about her arthritis preventing her from searching for her missing puppies.

    With the old woman refusing to cooperate, Tori explores some more before returning to visit "Lane", who mentions a dog that kept visiting and was currently hanging out in the bedroom and might belong to Jenka. Another walk through the desert reveals that yes, she recognizes the Lilipup. And while feeding the dog starts talking to it about a time in the past when other soldiers came from the surface... and how there were 4 other puppies missing. One other was hiding in a chest in a hidden passageway, another lilipup insisted on riding on Tori's head was found in an extremely dark abandoned house with a Trapinch infestation, a third Tori on a merry chase through the desert until she found a way to corner it, and the last was simply napping on a secluded sand dune.

    With each returned Lilipup Jenka revealed a bit more. Some time ago, humans seeking an object known as the Demon Crown came and attacked, some out of a desire for power, some in fear of what others would use that power for, but both saw the Mimiga as a threat. In response, the normally peacful mon used the red berries to strengthen themselves and fight back, even at the cost of their sanity and lives. The outsiders were driven back at a terrible cost, but the caves once again knew peace. With four dogs returned and the story completed, Jenka addressed Tori directly and insisted she could not let anyone have the key to the seed storage shed for fear of their misuse, and fear that one day the Mimiga may need to protect themselves again. Jenka then identifies Tori as not being from the caves or a soldier from the surface as others believed, but a visitor to this world.

    Tori returned again with the fifth and final lilipup only to find "Stark" fleeing Jenka's house. Jenka told Tori that he'd taken the key and, barely pausing long enough for the old woman to give her something, Tori chased the mon.

    Inside a shed a blue haired man in a lab coat wearing a peculiar crown inspects the contents of the shed, and on discovering some of the preserved seeds took root in a cracked portion of the floor and were already bearing red berries that seemed to static slightly out of the corner of one's eye, "Maria" suggested using a captive Mimiga to test them out. The Doctor agrees, and Maria teleports Tokoro into the shed, who immediately runs before she's restrained by "Stark" who then tries to force feed her. King arrives to drive Stark off with a sword, and enraged charges after the Doctor, but is blasted into a wall by a thunderbolt before he can close the distance.

    Tori arrives in time to watch the Mimiga's landing, and for the Doctor to tell her to enjoy his gift just before disappearing himself. Suddenly, Toroko is transformed into Mega* Mimiga-Lopunny!Toroko, and attacks Tori in a blind rage. Tori fights back, and after a fierce battle Toroko reverts to her normal Mimiga-buneary state, collapses... And won't wake back up.

    The critically injured King curses the Doctor and asks Tori to take up his sword before he passes. Tori refuses and insists King can help her stop the Doctor himself, if he'll let her help him. King hesitantly agrees, and Tori uses the Heal Ball Jenka gave her on King. With the now occupied ball in tow, Tori leaves the shed to report to the others... Only to be ambushed by "Stark" as she leaves and teleported away by "Maria"
