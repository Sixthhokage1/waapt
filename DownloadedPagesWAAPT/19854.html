    What can be determined for certain is that the Glitch struck without warning sometime in mid-afternoon, 23rd October.     Due to these opposing first-hand reports, as well as the thick glitch-coloured "fog" that has surrounded the city since roughly 4pm on the 23rd, no details on the current state of Sunyshore can be provided.     The city has been evacuated and is currently declared uninhabitable by both humans and Pokemon. In an official statement, however, Leader Volkner has given his blessing to any "heroic groups" who wish to enter the city and attempt to reclaim it.
