    Hearthome City, Hotel

    -Lucius returns to his hotel room to find Maul floating in the corner and a package sitting on his bed-

    Lucius,

    I took the liberty of purchasing a new phone for you whilst I was in town today and adding as many of your old contacts as I could remember. You can thank me later.

    Alice

    -he tears open the package to find a brand-new Holo Caster-

    Lucius: ...Huh. I appreciate Alice going through all the trouble of picking this up for me, but I'm not sure I'm sold on the whole Holo Caster idea. Do you have to use the hologram function? What happens if I get a call whilst I'm sitting here in my pants?

    Maul: ~Shame and humiliation.~

    Lucius: Hrm. Maybe I'll have to make some modifications.

    -there is suddenly a tap on his window, and then it's sliding open and there is a young woman with a shit-eating grin perched on his windowsill-

    Crewe: Yo, Fruitcake!

    -Lucius blinks in surprise-

    Lucius: ...Wait a sec, Crewe?! Is that you? Where the hell did you come from?

    -Crewe hops down off the sill, still grinning-

    Crewe: Damn straight it's me. Unless you know of any other girls likely to come crawling through your window, and if you do, please introduce me 'cause I wanna meet them. Anyways, get the hell over here so I can hug you and then kick your ass, Fruitcake, 'cause I got a bone to pick with you!

    -the smug grin has become a warning glare. Lucius rolls his eyes, though there's a hint of a smile on his lips-

    Lucius: See, the last time a girl came crawling through my hotel room window, she had a very different bone to pick.

    -he preemptively takes a step back-

    Lucius: I'll take a rain check on the hug and the beating, if you don't mind. How about we trade disappearance stories? You first.

    -Crewe wrinkles her nose-

    Crewe: Ew, no. Also, there is not a single solitary chance in the universe you have a girlfriend. You are way too much of a broody dweeb. And a fruitcake.

    -she narrows her eyes and looks him up and down, then shrugs and leans against the wall, tugging on the emerald kerchief tied around her neck-

    Crewe: Been doing Corps stuff, you know? Traveling the world, trying to convince more 'mons it's a good idea, helping put out fires and stuff. Y'know, the usual. Kept tabs on you guys, though, so I'd know if you were in the area and also to make sure you weren't dead so I didn't have to come murder somebody for vengeance and shit, but then you—

    -she jabs an accusing finger at him and glares-

    Crewe: You completely dropped off the map. When Chad told me, I had a whole bunch of the Natu on "Find the Fruitcake" duty, and I just got word back that not only are you suddenly back on the radar, but y'all were in Sinnoh! So I came to ask you what the hell happened, and kick your ass so it doesn't happen again. So, what the hell happened?

    -she narrows her eyes again-

    Crewe: And don't think you're getting out of anything so easy, Fruitcake. I come all this way, I'm gonna hug my damn brother. And beat him up for being an idiot.

    Lucius: Why does everyone want to beat me up for something that wasn't my fault? There's probably much better reasons to beat me up.

    -he sighs-

    Lucius: ...I got kidnapped. Some girl in a silver suit and weird visor knocked me out and dragged me to this evil corporate-lab-type place up north. This was about... five or six months back, I think? The days started blurring together after a while. Turns out her boss just wanted me to stick me in his office and use me as a giant Obscura battery. It was just as much fun as it sounds. Alice and Titan broke me out eventually, but not before I'd reaped the benefits of their super-slimming gruel-only diet.

    -he pulls down the sleeve of his sweater to reveal a bone-thin arm-

    Lucius: See? My finely-toned physique is in ruins. It's totally unfair. I'm a zombie, I shouldn't even have a metabolism anymore.

    -Crewe's expression quickly goes from annoyed to horrified to utterly pissed off. She marches over to Lucius, making an odd low noise like a growl in the back of her throat, and grabs his arm, pulling it up towards her face to study it and furrowing her brow. She suddenly throws it down and draws a dagger made from a Razor Fang from a small sheath at her hip-

    Crewe: I'll cut their faces off, you watch me. Where are they?

    -Lucius pulls away, looking disturbed-

    Lucius: Whoa, whoa, chill out. You think I'd be lounging around here playing with my new phone if I knew that? I'm still working on finding them. In the meantime, I don't think any face-cutting will be necessary.

    -Crewe scowls, but reluctantly lowers the knife-

    Crewe: It is absolutely necessary. And you, Fruitcake, are in no shape to do it.

    -she glowers at him for a few moments, then abruptly grabs him and drags him into a tight hug-

    Crewe: I missed you. Stupid.

    -Lucius squrims, but eventually submits and returns the hug-

    Lucius: I missed you too. Jerkface.

    -Crewe pulls back after a minute and punches his shoulder-

    Crewe: Now don't go and get yourself into life-threatening situations when I'm not around to bail you out, huh, Fruitcake?

    -she pauses and bites her lip-

    Crewe: By the way, you, uh... you said Titan got you out? Is he... around?

    -Lucius's expression quickly darkens-

    Lucius: ...I dunno. Presumably. He just kinda up and vanished after we finished talking.

    -Crewe frowns and rubs her arms-

    Crewe: Oh. Well, uh... okay. I just thought maybe I'd see him. It's been a while.

    Lucius: ...Yeah. It sure has.

    -he seems to spend several seconds in intense internal debate before speaking again-

    Lucius: He, uh... he hasn't tried to get in touch recently, has he?

    -Crewe shakes her head, shrugging-

    Crewe: Nope. I mean, I did kinda drop off the map there for a while, but... no, I haven't talked to him for... well, months. Hell, I haven't talked to anyone who isn't a Pokemon for months.

    Lucius: Right. Gotta keep up the whole "feral" image.

    -she rolls her eyes and punches him again-

    Crewe: Like you're keeping up your stupid image?

    Lucius: Hey, I haven't had the chance! My kidnappers didn't exactly provide me with my own personal stylist.

    Crewe: Well I mean, you're ugly too, yeah, but you had the stupid thing going long before you got kidnapped, Fruitcake.

    Lucius: At least I'm not projecting hard enough to display a Powerpoint presentation.

    -she scoffs and presses a hand to her heart-

    Crewe: Rude! I am offended, Fruitcake. I'm gonna kick your ass for that.

    -she tackles him, and he swats helplessly at her in response-

    Lucius: Gah! Gerrof! You know I can't fight back! This is exploitation! Maul, help me out here!

    Maul: ~Okay.~

    -he telekinetically hurls a pillow, which hits Lucius squarely on the nose-

    Lucius: Ow!

    Maul: ~Whoops. Missed.~

    -Crewe grins-

    Crewe: HaHA! I can always count on Maul to take my side against you!

    -she smushes the pillow against his face, to which he responds by grabbing another off the bed and whapping her in the face with it. Crewe gives an undignified squawk and tries to tug the pillow out of his hands, but Lucius seizes the opportunity to disarm her of the first pillow as she does-

    Lucius: Ha! Reversal!

    -he proceeds to counterattack with a precision strike. Crewe squeaks in alarm and ducks under the pillow. She conjures an Obscura shield over his eyes, and uses the opportunity to scramble around and get him in a headlock-

    Crewe: Surrender!

    Lucius: Never! You cheated! I refuse to recognise your illegitimate victory!

    -he flails ineffectually-

    Crewe: There are no rules! Only my sheer awesomeness.

    -she proceeds to noogie him viciously-

    Lucius: More like sheer awfulness! Maul! Attack Pattern Alpha!

    -Maul blips Lucius away to a point about two feet above Crewe's head, and gravity takes care of the rest of the attack. Crewe yelps and dives out of the way just in time for him to land on her legs instead of her head. Her outline goes hazy for a moment, and then there's a Sneasel on the ground about where her head was, which promptly latches on to Lucius's back with her claws, as gently as an excited Sneasel can-

    Lucius: OW! Oh come on, quit cheating! First magic, now shapeshifting. I call foul!

    Crewe: <Surrender and I'll stop!>

    -she scrambles up to his shoulder, prompting Lucius to grab a pillow and start swatting at her. She hisses and claws at the pillow with one paw, wrapping the other arm around his neck to stay in place. Lucius continues swatting until the pillow is reduced to a cloud of stuffing, then reaches for another... only to topple over and collapse onto the bed-

    Lucius: Urgh... I feel real dizzy all of a sudden...

    -Crewe yelps and dives off his shoulder as he collapses. A moment later she's a human again, looking concerned and a little alarmed-

    Crewe: Hey, whoa, you okay there, Fruitcake? D'you need... like... I dunno, a berry or something?

    Lucius: Nah... a lie down, a glass of water and some real food is probably enough. I just need to take it easy for a while... or maybe more than a while

    Crewe: Yeah. Okay. Um. You lay down and don't do shit for the rest of the night, I'll... go get some food from somewhere and bring it back. If you die before I get back, I'll kick your ass, and I mean it.

    Lucius: I'll try not to. Dying once was bad enough.

    -he looks over at her, light-headed and slightly unfocused-

    Lucius: ...Thanks, Crewe.

    -she grins and ruffles his hair-

    Crewe: Hey, no worries, Fruitcake. What kind of adoptive sister would I be if I didn't get my little bro dinner after I kicked his ass at a pillow fight?

    -she climbs back up onto the window sill and is gone. Lucius stares silently at the ceiling for about a minute-

    Lucius: ...Ugh. I have to tell him, don't I...?

    Maul: ~Yep.~

    -Lucius groans, then reaches for his new Holo Caster-

    Thanks to Crewe for spagging this with me.
