    Sholto: <...What do you mean?>

    Lisa: <Well... have you heard much about the trips we've gone on to Pokesona?>

    Sholto: <Bits and pieces, but not much. Why?>

    Lisa: <Well... we've been to my dungeon too and... I still haven't told you what was there.>

    Sholto: <Oh?>

    Lisa: <Well... I'm honestly not certain. Frankly, I don't come off like I like to rely on others much, but I also feel like I'm somewhat dependent on them. Regardless, though, I often don't talk about my problems and I thought I should come clean.>

    Sholto: <Ah... Well, I understand why you might feel weird about that stuff. I can understand keeping secrets about some stuff. Though... I'm glad you're willing to talk about that stuff.>

    Lisa: <Well... that's not all. Someone resembling you was there.>

    Sholto: <...Oh?>

    -Lisa opens her mouth to speak, but ends up whispering in Sholto's ear-

    -As she does so, his expression shifts to one of hurt-

    Sholto: <...That's what you think of me? You were with me at my worst with Lawrence, in what world would I not be loyal just because->

    Lisa: <...Sorry. I want to be clear that that is not what I think of you. I just thought I should note what happened with that...>

    Sholto: <Hm, well... Alright... I guess I'm glad you told me...>

    <Though... Is that what makes you uncertain about us?>

    Lisa: <It's not just that. Frankly, it feels like our relationship has an unbelievable number of hang ups that make me not sure if it's totally healthy, but simultaneously multiple reasons I don't think we should let it go. I'm frankly unsure at this point whether we shouldn't continue to not deal with the problems or just blaze forth and say to heck with them.>

    Sholto: <I... know what you mean, yeah... This does feel kind of messy at times, but ultimately it does feel worth it in the end.>

    <And, I mean... there's definitely chances to end this before, but we haven't. Even with that egg thing we eventually bounced back.>

    Lisa: <That... is true. I'm overall happy to have met you and love being with you.>

    <So... I suppose... would you like to make it official?>

    Sholto: <...Official?>

    Lisa: <Yes.>

    <Sholto, will you marry me?>

    Sholto: <I... yes! Yes I will!>

    <I... never thought I'd say that in the same conversation where someone confessed some panphobic thoughts to me, but->

    Lisa: <I know, this is awkward, but I feel that's a testament to how we've made this work.>

    Sholto: <...Okay, fair.>

    <...Though, wait, shouldn't we have rings if we're doing this?>

    Lisa: <Perhaps, though we could always forgo them or just get them later.>

    Sholto: <True, but I feel like we should have a ring...>

    -He looks at his ankle bracelet-

    Sholto: <Oh, how about->
