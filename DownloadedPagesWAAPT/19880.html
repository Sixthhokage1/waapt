    Bedman: ~Boss, we got something.~

    -Cid snaps from his reverie.-

    Cid: Police?

    Bedman: ~Nope, but it's moving FAST, and it feels really really hungry.~

    Cid: A Snorlax on a binge?

    Bedman: ~Unless Snorlax can FLY, then no.~

    -Cid and his Alakazam duck behind a copse of trees and wait as the Alakazam counts down on his fingers until the thing he feels comes into view.-

    -Three...-

    -Two...-

    -One...-

    -A Pidgeot, to Cid's incredulity, screams through the trees, breaking off branches and diving over rocks. It quickly speeds past Cid's hiding spot and starts to wheel around.-

    Bedman: ~... Oh I think he saw us.~

    -The Pidgeot begins to gain altitude.-

    Cid: He's preparing to dive. Watch yourself.

    -Before Cid can even finish, the Pidgeot divebombs the Alakazam with a Brave Bird. His speed and ferocity give Cid pause, and Bedman a very close shave as he Teleports out of the way. The Pidgeot wheels around for a second dive with surprising agility.-

    Cid: Shock Wave. Bring that thing down.

    -The Alakazam pulses lightning, zapping the bird on his descent, but not dissuading him from his target. The Pidgeot impacts Bedman and the Alakazam crumples under the impact.-

    Cid: What...?!

    -The Pidgeot, ignoring its wounds, once again dives at the Alakazam, his talons out and hunger in its eyes. Cid finally identifies the source of the Pidgeot's power - desperation.-

    Cid: Oh no you don't.

    -Cid snaps a wrist out, flinging an Ultra Ball that releases a monster of a Slaking.-

    Cid: Gonzales! Restrain!

    -The Pidgeot ducks and weaves around the Slaking's mass, his eyes still intent on the meal he'd made for himself, until the hulking humanoid's hand whips around and nearly crushes the life from between the bird's relatively frail ribs.-

    -Gonzales grunts as the bird furiously flails in his grasp. Annoying bird. Stupid bird. Be less stupid.-

    -Gonzales then flicks the bird on the side of the head, and it goes limp in his hand.-

    -Much better. Stupid bird now asleep.-

    Cid: Interesting...

    -Cid recalls Bedman - he can always use Hector for teleports - and beckons to his Slaking.-

    Cid: Gonzales. Bring him here.

    -The Slaking yawns and drops the bird at Cid's feet. The scientist looks the bird over - scars covered the Pidgeot's wings and chest, and the distinct knots of broken and healed bones were a common site on both the legs and wings. This Pidgeot, for whatever reason, had made itself many enemies over the years.-
