    A boy and a girl, accompanied by a Greninja and a Delphox, were currently making their way into the city. The girl holding a map while the boy was looking at it.

    "So, we're back in Jubilife! Lovely!" said Diana, smiling happily, "We're almost done!"

    "Uh... Di... I think we're anywhere but close to done," said Samson, blinking as he looked at the map

    "What makes you think that?" asked Diana, genuinely curious

    Samson then took hold of the map and, after getting a marker from his backpack, proceed to start marking things on the map for a bit before handing Diana the finished product

    "Oh," said Diana, getting tired just from looking at it.

    "We missed three cities, two major cities even," said Samson, sighing

    "Well, one. I'm not going anywhere close to Snowpoint. I hate cold places." Diana frowned

    Samson blinked, baffled. "... You realize you're in Sinnoh right?"

    Diana then placed her hands on her hips, narrowing her eyes at Samson "Well! It's tolerable levels of cold right now! Why do you think I have a fire type?!"

    "To help with your cooking?!"

    "<They're gonna keep arguing aren't they,>" said Aegir, rolling his eyes

    "<Yep. We'll be here for a while.>" said Soleil, smiling a little bit but letting out a sigh.

    "<Maybe they should take a vacation, from each other.>"

    "<Come on, it's fun when we're not fighting! Remember when I dared you to swallow one of those big poképuffs whole?>" Soleil giggled

    Aegir simply looked at Soleil with the most not-amused expression he could possibly make.

    "<... Yeah, that wasn't pretty for anyone involved.>" She giggled again, this time more awkwardly

    "<I think maybe I need a break from you too.>" Aegir sighed.

    "<There, there,>" Soleil patted Aegir on the head a few times, "<At least now I know you'll do anything if your pride is on the line.>"

    "<That's anything but comforting.>" Aegir then brought a hand to his forehead and shook his head a little bit.
