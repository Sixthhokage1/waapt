    So hey. It's me, Umbra, again, here to say that after that mental hospital visit and a further month of taking some chill time I've decided I may be willing to come back to this RP after all. There's just plenty of stuff I need to address first.

    For starters, I don't really feel like I can just waltz back in here and act like everything was all hunky-dory when I left because... It definitely wasn't. I was driving myself insane from my own anxiety and depression, and that led to me lashing out at people and alienating them, which just further fueled the anxiety and depression and... Yeah, I was in an extremely self-destructive spiral and am glad I got some time off and professional help (even if I didn't get the latter in the most ideal fashion, being cooped up in a mental hospital kind of sucks).

    And... I'm feeling better. My mood's been much more stable, I've been having some good developments to keep me positive, and I'm just overall a happier, less miserable person with a better grasp of his coping skills.

    Still, it took a lot of contemplation for me to decide to come back, mainly because while I really have missed you guys and this RP a lot I still feel crappy about being hurtful. I've vowed to not hold grudges anymore and use those handy-dandy coping skills I mentioned earlier, but I'm well aware actions speak louder than words, so I'll have to keep myself in gear, as well as getting your guy's advice on how to truly make up for my hurtful behavior and when to take more breaks.

    But I feel more refreshed than I have in a long, long time. I feel confident. And confidence in myself to do better is going to be vital for being part of this community again, so I feel like I'm finally ready. I just gotta keep it low-key at first and remember some respect and tolerance as much as possible.

    And... I love you guys. Without you all, my writing, my passions, my friendships, wouldn't be anything like they are now. And I'm grateful to you all for that and want this community in my life again. And if you guys are willing to have me back in turn I'll share good news from my life since I've been gone. And after that a rather unusual Best Berry character idea.

    Anyway, on to my coming-back plan, part one of which I call:

    Operation Legacy, AKA the Lockeplot

    And now, my super-elaborate plan for Locke's plot is...

    ...

    Made ya look, there is no super-elaborate plan, A bunch of loosely-related plans, yes, but I'll stitch them together as I go.

    Why? Well, I've had the suspicion for a while, but talking to Bitters, Silent, and Zeal helped me realize that stonewalling myself with countless big endgame plans, all of which I wanted to do ASAP, was decidedly awful for my psychological health, meaning picking up exactly were I left off was a no-no. So, I'm using Operation Legacy as a change of pace to try something more freeform for a while. Then, when I commence Part 2 and resume my endgame plans, they'll be much more of a slow burn - including the megaspags (Delta Serial and Scythermen Plot) that were previously huge obstacles. This way I can hopefully minimize stress for me and everyone else, and better balance the RP with the rest of my life, including my other writing. On that note, to help things along, my WAAPT spinoff fic of sorts, Heroes After All, is now no longer dependent on the endgame and now dependent on Rude Awakening finishing up, meaning it's no longer being held up by the RP itself. (Speaking of which, expect an official reveal of HAA's premise to anyone not already aware within the near future.)

    But back on the note of Operation Legacy, here's that Best Berry/Team Iron NPC that'll be part of the plot, somehow:

    Oran OS

    • Species: AI

    • Age: Unknown

    • Gender: N/A

    • Ethnicity: N/A

    • Appearance: A blue, Oran-berry shaped icon with voice lines that pops up on computer screens.

    • Organizational Ties: Best Berry, with some loose Team Iron associations.

    • Region of origin: Unknown

    • Backstory: Oran OS is part of the official software of Best Berry's computers, and is vital to managing their assets. Part of that, however, is that it is a surprisingly intelligent and ruthless AI who uses Best Berry's computer network to serve as a spy and enforcer, immediately being able to inform whoever it needs or take action itself against threats to the company. It sees Legendaries as threats to technological advances that would make it more powerful, so it has also been feeding information to Team Iron since the group's alliance with Best Berry.

    It also has a team of special Pokemon that do its bidding:

    Fortran

    • Species: Porygon Z

    • Gender: None, masculine voice

    • Ability: Download

    • Personality: Loyal to Oran OS to a fault, Fortran seems to be unable to comprehend they are capable of doing any wrong if properly serving their "trainer." They are capable of interfacing with Oran OS to receive updates and share information.

    DeLeo

    • Species: Alolan Persian

    • Gender: Male

    • Ability: Technician

    • Personality: While mostly a pampered pet, DeLeo makes a surprisingly good attack cat when the time calls for it. However, he's also prone to laying on Oran OS's keyboards.

    Ein

    • Species: Midday Lycanroc

    • Gender: Male

    • Ability: Sand Rush

    • Personality: Ruthless and predatory, Ein has special microchips installed into his body to serve as Oran OS's eyes and ears. However, he still shows his doggy instincts in not getting along with DeLeo.

    Haggar

    • Species: Oranguru

    • Gender: Female

    • Ability: Inner Focus

    • Personality: Haggar is Oran OS's interrogator, and shows no pity to anyone she's assigned to wring information out of. The one thing that seems to make her actually happy though, is twisted experiments.
