    The Masked Commissar

    The Rossum Institute Discussion

    It has been brought up in the chat that I have not properly discussed the team behind the Mole for my "Below Suspicion" plot idea. So it is time to bring the idea to the Forefront and discuss "The Rossum Institute".

    The Rossum Institute is a Technology Heavy team focused upon the idea of using technology to improve lives and make the world a easier place to live, sounds noble right? Well that's just it, they believe what they're doing to be just and noble. No matter what happens.

    The end result would be a almost Zeerust style world, with people living in clean smart homes. But only if you served Rossum, everyone else would be relegated to serving them or servicing the machinery they'd use to make this world possible. Basically, Life is pretty bad if you don't work for Rossum.

    But their power isn't full on army, it's small, their greatest powers are in the tech they use and agents they place, hence their use of a Mole, to gain access to unusual items or even unique pieces of technology. All to gain access to power, power they can use to build their Utopia and assert it over the planet.

    Now, what can be changed, what can be tweaked? And would should be removed?

    I wish to know to make this good for the RP so everyone who gets involved can have fun.

    Edit 1: Robotics

    Rossum makes use of various robotic servants, to clean their base, carry heavy objects, or to be basically robotic maids. Ideal for their future, any of their droids seen out on the field are little smarter than Bricks meant to just serve as carriers for objects too heavy for Agents when necessary. Other than that, rarely seen outside of their base moping up the floors.
