    Two things...

    One, I'm not retiring Robin. General consensus is the FE Fates debacle has little to do with him and likely won't affect him meta-wise so... Yeah, he's staying.

    Second is I should probably go into more detail about exactly what has been keeping me away from the RP for the past month or so.

    Around May 20th or so (it was right after I finished Truth, I remember) my left hand/fingers/arm started twitching and convulsing involuntarily, nonstop. Often, the level of convulsions got so bad it became extremely uncomfortable and I can't focus on other things or even sleep. And to make things worse it started actually getting painful soon after. The nature of the pain is difficult to accurately describe, but to sum it up best I can it feels like some large carnivorous animal is trying to rip the flesh off my arm and fingers. It's extremely painful and during pain episodes I'm often screaming in agony and/or begging someone to cut my arm off.

    Thankfully the pain can be quelled mostly or even entirely by some of my meds... but that's where the good news ends.

    See, the doctors have no freaking idea what's causing it. They thought it was my meds, then they thought it was something in my brain, and they've been doing all sorts of medical voodoo and nothing's really working. Any lessening of the symptoms turns out to be temporary, they prescribe new meds and it just keeps repeating itself, and worst of all the convulsions themselves never actually stop, just are lessened occasionally.

    And then two weeks ago they took me off most of my psychological meds because they thought it would improve the symptoms but that just made things worse since the twitching persisted and the withdrawal symptoms from losing the psychological meds just made the rest of my body sick. I feel drained and sluggish, and I frequently get the urge to vomit for no reason- in fact I can't eat large meals anymore because I just barf them up. Most days I have no energy to do anything but sleep (when the convulsing's at its low points) or binge Tumblr on my phone (when it's at its high points).

    So yeah, I've been extremely ill and miserable and in no state to RP- which is really frustrating because I was gonna use this part of the arc to do very important things and am very worried about you all moving on without me even though this is completely out of my control. Right now all I want more than anything is to get this cured ASAP so I can get back to doing what I swore I'd do nearly a year ago.

    (And yes, because of my arm this was incredibly difficult to type.)
