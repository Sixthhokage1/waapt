    Dune usually espouses even-handed, cooperative responses to problems, however he is not adverse to applying violence to situations that deserve it, such as when he perceives that people are being malicious or criminally stupid. He has been known to show a tired, smoldering temper from time to time, especially when attempting to reason with the unreasonable.

    Dune joined the J-Team as the team was finishing up it's second journey through Unova and quickly took to following the team after wandering the Badlands north of Unova for some time.

    He is about as "bog standard" as a human can be at birth, with no Auric or Obscuric capabilities. He also doesn't have an Attribute. That said, he does have an abnormality in that he is psychically deaf - telepathy can neither hear his thoughts nor project thoughts to him. Attempts to enter his mind through any means beside a specially designated entry point are violently rebutted by both the "scar tissue" left behind from the traumatic event that damaged Dune's mind and the self defense mechanism that arose from said event.

    Dune's parents are both divorced. Violently. Speaking to either parent is incredibly uncomfortable due to years of aggression and disagreements regarding Dune's life choices. He is amiable with most of the rest of his family, but is unfamiliar with them due to his parents being considered "black sheep" of their respective names due to their outright, fight-in-the-middle-of-the-street aggression towards one another.

    Dune's Main Team

    Galvani the Magnezone (and Human): I'll be going into more detail with her later. She is Dune's starter.

    Rill the Swampert: Joined Dune while leaving Orre for the first time. Even tempered Cloudcuckoo Landers Minder who more often than not ends up getting knocked out in combat due to not truly specializing in any form of combat.

    Garuda the Sigilyph: Caught by Dune in Orre after escaping a Cipher smuggling operation. Defending and fighting alongside his trainer is almost a biological imperative. Focuses on ranged support during combat.

    Big Dom the Shiny Golurk: Hatched from an egg Dune acquired as payment from the Daycare in Johto during his early travels. Always speaks in ALLCAPS or nocaps. Responds especially violently to "Vow Breakers".

    Selene the Clefable: A Clefable caught outside the Blacklight Caverns, a set of caves far out in the badlands. Is currently on a "roadtrip" with a Shiny Ralts named Satele.

    Ajax the Tyranitar: Caught in the Spitfire Valley, a climatological oddity where the weather can make sudden, incredibly violent shifts over the course of a matter of seconds. Taught himself how to read, he is quite erudite but is afraid of his own strength and of society's perception of how violent Tyranitar are.
