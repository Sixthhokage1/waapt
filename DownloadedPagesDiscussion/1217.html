    So, this has technically been in effect for a while now but I'd kind of like to make it official and a bit bigger in magnitude:

    I am taking a semi-indefinite hiatus from actually actively R Ping here.

    Oh, sure, meta-wise I'll be as active as I usually am and possibly more so for reasons I'll get into, but in terms of the RP itself don't expect anything other than one-off gagposts and tentative addressing of certain loose ends until I'm up for anything bigger.

    But Umbra, your endgame! Didn't you want that done as soon as possible, especially with all the delays?!

    Yes, and I still pretty much do. But I feel this is kind of a necessary evil for the following reasons:

    • My tendency to cross the line into jerkdom has gotten notably worse lately: You've all been seeing this in this very thread as recently as yesterday a few posts up, more subdued attempts to curb that have failed, and I feel I should be focusing on addressing my short fuse and how to less easily go after people when triggered or otherwise provoked. This is also why this isn't a full-on hiatus; I've figured out avoiding you guys even to cool off does nothing to actually help me learn how to deal with social situations with you guys where I inadvertently get rubbed the wrong way.

    • I've been acting like a jerk more partially because I've had a really stressful past 1.5 or so years: It doesn't excuse the previous, but it's certainly a factor in it and why I'm doing this break. Horrible illnesses, suicide attempts, coerced mental hospital stays, getting a computer broken, horrible falling out with formerly close friends and other stuff from RL too sensitive to mention here... The good news is that it's slowly been settling down over the past month or so, but there's still enough awful crap popping up that I'm still pretty stressed out most of the time and think trying to refocus will help until things better smooth over.

    • Stress has been making focus on WAAPT endgame stuff even harder: This mainly affects the next important thing to get done, the Scythemen plot - we've got pretty much everything set up to go, but wrangling everyone involved when they have varying motivation levels and availability is the killer - and as with similar roadblocks in the past it's been draining my will to get anything else done, even stuff I could theoretically get done before it goes up.

    • There's a load of fanfic stuff I need to focus on - and would RATHER focus on over WAAPT right now: I've got my entry for the Worlds Collide contest, Rude Awakening, getting the revised version of Rosencrantz And Guildenstern Are Plasbad done before the end of the year, and potentially one other thing for the holidays, along with a non-fanfic thing that's almost done but I've been procrastinating on finishing off - and I'd honestly rather focus on all that writing-wise when I feel so stuck on WAAPT, especially since I've found working on other writing projects after being stuck on one tends to REALLY help my motivation overall, including when I decide I'm ready to come back to the one I was stuck on.

    As for when I make that decision... Well, I've decided I've rather that decision not entirely be mine, but the circumstances of that are private and will be addressed privately as well.

    However, aside from the shitposts and minor loose end wrapup I know I will be temporarily returning for two things: Arika's Memory Glitch plot (which is likely to start once I finish the first draft of my contest entry or so) and Halloween, which I will bring up that silly idea I mentioned to end this on a lighter note:

    Return of the Charizard 'M Virus!

    As some of you may know, for the J-Team's first Halloween they were infected by a virusy intepretation of the Charizard 'M glitch that turned everyone into glitchy hivemind Charizard, vials of which are now quarantined at PEFE. My idea was that the Re: Glitch glitch got ahold of a vial and mutated it into a Charizard 'Megavirus not curable by the normal means... And turning everyone into a Glitch Megazard of whichever of the two suits the infected character's personality (or is ironic, your pick). However, the two types of Megazard have separate hiveminds, and they erupt into an all-out war, with those not infected scrambling to find a cure!

    But thoughts of either half of this post?
