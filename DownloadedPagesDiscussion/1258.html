    Alright, here's the big preplan post! It was mostly written by Tagg and I, but there were many other contributors and good suggestions, so please give it a good read!

    The WAAPT Sun and Moon Incorporation Post!

    Basically, this is a long list of things we're gonna need to cover before updating the RP to Generation 7 mechanics, much less starting an Alola arc. To start off...

    It takes two to tango, but four to Tapu

    • A good deal of people have volunteered to write for the Tapu, AKA Alola's four guardian land spirit/deity Legendaries. It's likely best establishing them as characters is split between people who have been in the RP a while and can be trusted writing Legendaries but haven't written for many yet.
      • So far Crow is interested in writing for any of them, while Uill is interested in Tapu Koko. Nothing here is to be finalized until after public discussion of this post opens, however.
      • Are the Kahunas the only ones who understand the Tapu of their island without an Armband or such? Or can everybody understand the Tapu when the Tapu in question desires, given Tapu Koko briefly speaks to the player in the epilogue?

    The OTHER Eclipseshipping

    • Writing/establishment duties for Solgaleo and Lunala should be figured out as well.
      • Naturally, the version followed by WAAPT should remain ambiguous, especially since we've had to do it the last two gens, though that leaves the question of what to do about the Lake of the Sunne/Moone and Altar of the Sunne/Moone. Combine them like we did for the two versions of Opelucid and White Forest and Black City? Leave it as it is in-game? (Which would mean having to determine a "canon" version for WAAPT.) Something else?
      • It is heavily implied the original Solgaleo and Lunala were lovers by in-game lore and the one of the pair that evolves from Lillie's Cosmog Nebby into the one you catch is their child, so that all needs to be addressed.

    • Necrozma, Magearna, and especially Marshadow are a bit more up in the air as to how we handle them.

    This RP's getting Ultra Beastly

    Basically, Ultra Beasts and How To Handle Them:

    • Are there any still roaming around Alola? Would they be capturable, and implicitly, do we want to give the J-Team access to Beast Balls? If not, how would they be used as/viable as NP Cs? What measure are they in terms of power compared to Legendaries?
      • If they are capturable, would it be all the Ultra Beasts, or only some? Would they be usable for Gym Battles and other such official battles or would they be semi- to fully restricted like Deltas are?

    • Due to not being like regular mons, would the Armbands still suffice for communication with them, or would something else be required? How does this affect communication with members of the Cosmog Line since they're technically Ultra Beasts?

    • If there are more roaming around than what the Sun/Moon protagonist contained, would the J-Team be enlisted to help handle them? Could this be the crux of the Alola arc plot? Would the PEFE be interested in the Ultra Beasts?

    • While Ultra Beasts can appear throughout the world, the reason they appear in Alola the most is because for whatever reason interdimensional boundaries are weaker within the region. Do we want to have Ultra Beasts appearing outside of Alola from time to time going forward?

    • Additionally, the power of Totem Pokemon to flare their Auras is said to come from channeling the Ultra Wormholes... Could this be tied to the Gates?

    • Dimensional Barriers are Falling Down:
      • Fallers are people (And probably Pokmon) that have passed through an Ultra Wormhole, either to Ultra Space itself or another universe. Are all Fallers under the custody of the International Police, or are there those who managed to slip through the cracks? Do we want to allow P Cs going forward to have "Faller" as part of their backstory? Only allow it for secondary characters?
      • Is Ultra Space Entralink-accessible? Or are Ultra Wormholes the only way in? Can the Entree generate Ultra Wormholes?

    A Meeting of Minds

    • How do we handle the reformed Aether Foundation with Gladion and Wicke as its leaders? Would an alliance with the PEFE be possible? Had the PEFE and Aether Project ever had any dealings beforehand, or perhaps even the Aether Project and Pokefutures prior to the latter's dissolution?

    (This of course is extremely determinant on whether or not we go with the J-Team doing the canon plot.)

    Type: Null in Short Supply

    • A lot of people were itching to get their hands on a Type: Null and a Sivally, but it turns out there are only three members of the artificial species in existence, two of them are in the hands of Gladion and the canon protagonist, the third is in cryogenic stasis, and they can't breed. Future games may provide workarounds, but for now, what do we do? Have PEFE humanely reverse-engineer them? Have one of the few remaining in an important NPC role and put the line under the Legendary clause? Something else entirely?

    Z-Moves, Z-Problems

    • This is more a balance issue: Z-Moves are capable of drawing from any compatible move in the Pokemon's moveset, but due to the way the four-move limit works in-RP it could allow a Pokemon to use a Z-Move off a move it otherwise doesn't use in battle. Should this be kept in, or should Z-Moves be limited to being used from a move the Pokemon's already used in-battle?

    • The good news is Z-Move items should be easier distribution-wise than Mega items, especially since mundane Trainers can use Z-Moves in-game and every Pokemon can use Z-Moves as well. But earning of Z-Rings seems to still be a big deal and currently only Kahunas can refine the stones to make them, so how should that be handled?

    • While most of the type Z-Crystals are given to you as part of the Island Challenge, there are those that aren't (Ex. Psychium-Z, Icium-Z) so how should we handle those?

    Anabel's Buneary Hole

    • In Sun and Moon, a certain Frontier Brain returns for the Ultra Beast plot... But not as we know her due to walking down a multiversal path into the International Police. Obviously, something like this never quite happened to WAAPT!Anabel, at least not to the letter, given she appeared as recently as several months ago. Some solutions to this conundrum have already been pre-emptively discussed - should this be part of the current Anabel's backstory in some convoluted fashion? Should there be two Anabels, one native, one stumbled from some 'verse no J-Teamer has ever set foot in, both handled by different players at a time? Something else entirely? For the aformentioned little bit of discussion that has already occurred on the subject, check this thread.

    The Champion Conundrum

    In Sun and Moon, while Kukui serves as the final boss in attempting to reverse Blue/Gary Oak you, you the player are the Champion. This obviously won't work for WAAPT, so we'll probably make a necessary breach from canon in that whoever served the role of Champion stepped down. But who takes their place? Kukui himself? An older WAAPT NPC making their triumphant return? Jacob getting his pending Champion title transferred to another region? Have any of the postgame title-defense opponents available per challenge due to the Alola League having a particularly contested seat? Something else entirely?

    Trials and Tribulations

    • The Trial system: The overarching structure should be simple enough to adapt, but should we stick solely to the in-game trials or have the various Trial captains come up with more devious ones when the player wishes? Do the Totem mons still have to be the same as in canon? Do they even need to be necessarily attached to a trial?

    • Given that in canon, trials are supposed to be for 11-year-olds as a sort of coming-of-age challenge, we'll inevitably have to come up for some explanation of why it's open to the mostly-teenage-to-young-adult J-Team. Question is, what will our explanation be? Are they experimenting with the trial system for visitors such as the conveniently-present J-Team as part of experimentation for a proper League? Outright making the Trials the entry fee for the Pokemon League, much like how Gyms work in other regions? Are we just abandoning that part of canon entirely for the J-Team's sake? Something else entirely?

    • Given that two of the Kahunas and one of the Trial Captains are currently part of the Alola Elite Four, how do we handle the Melemele and Akala Kahunas and the Thrifty Megamart Trial? Do we have other characters fill Hala, Olivia and Acerolas roles?? Have them multitask? Something else entirely?

    • Depending on the version you're playing, in Ilima's Trial you'll end up battling either Totem Gumshoos or Totem Alolan Raticate. Should there only be "one" canon Totem mon, or should it differ depending on the time of day that people do the Trial?

    Do you ever wonder why we're here?

    • We've gone through a couple generally plot-loose arcs over the past couple years. This is, of course, due to a number of us having to deal with things in reality, as well as a general drop in motivation near the end of the year for this past one. But for Alola, though, should we keep to the relatively structure-less trend we've had since after Orre, or should we try to do something with a bit more direction? If so, what? Should we try to build up toward a new Big Bad, whether they'll be handled in this arc or a later one? Should we attempt to follow the games' own plot, breaking the J-Team's just-too-late-to-help trend? Something else entirely?
      • Of course, if we do choose to try to follow the game plot, that would inevitably affect how many of the other things in this list are handled as well, so that idea should probably be either taken or shot down before much else is decided.

    Timeline Tangle

    • According to Grimsley's concept art, he's about two years older than he was during Black and White 2, meaning that Sun and Moon take place about two years after BW 2 and XY. How much of a gap do we want between them and WAAP Tverse!Sun and Moon? If we were to do it straight (That is, WAAP Tverse!Sun and Moon taking place in 2015 during Hoenn-2 since WAAP Tverse!BW 2 and XY took place in 2013.) that would mean the J-Team would be almost two years late to the party.

    Mega Evolution: Mega Darker And Mega Edgier

    • Many of the new Pokdex entries that mention Mega Evolution treat it as some sort of Superpowered Evil Side, even though in-story a strong bond between human and Pokmon is still required for it to work. While we often ignore the Canon!Dex, compared to earlier games Sun and Moon seem to have a greater amount of Gameplay and Story Integration. How should we handle this? Should we suddenly depict Mega Evolution in a more negative light, or should we treat what's described as just examples of what happens when Mega Evolution is done incorrectly?

    Ya Boys at Team Skull

    • Since Guzma ended up disbanding Team Skull, what do we want to do with the remnants? Completely gone? Some still hanging around but utterly listless without a leader? Is Plumeria still watching over them? Are they still hanging around Po Town? Something else entirely?

    Our Pokdexes are Haunted

    • One of Sun and Moon's newest features is the Rotom Pokdex, a Pokdex that is actively haunted by a Rotom for an onboard not so artificial AI. Canonically they're supposed to be rare by virtue of being bleeding-edge tech, so should any J-Team members be able to get them? Would having any of the J-Team's Rotom possess an Aloladex work just as well?

    Comrades, Together We Ride

    • Instead of H Ms, riding mons are used instead. In fact, according to the Sightseer in Malie City that gifts the player a Fly TM , Alolans frown upon if not outright consider illegal using non-designated mons for Flying. Does that mean we have to use the canon Ride mons in-game, that apparently all come from some ranch, or is it possible to designate mons you already own as riding Pokmon?

    Lillie White

    • We know that following the player becoming Champion and catching/defeating Tapu Koko, Lillie decides to take Lusamine with her to Kanto, partially to go on her own Pokmon journey, partially to get help from Bill removing the remainder of Nihilego's deadly neurotoxins. In future arcs taking place in Kanto presuming that she doesn't come back to Alola any time soon, do we want her and/or Lusamine to be encounterable in Kanto? Battleable?

    Bean Daddy

    • Do we deal with the existence of Pok Pelago, especially since many players make use of the PC box system? And perhaps more importantly, do we deal with the existence of Mohn and the heavy implications he is Lusamine's husband and Gladion and Lillie's father?

    Those Random Narcoleptic 11 Year Olds from Kanto

    (Probably extremely dependant on whether or not we have the J-Team actually get involved in the main plot of a game for once.)

    • How should we choose to handle the P Cs of Sun and Moon, since Gen VII seems to be going the Schrdinger's Player Character route? Should they not exist like Brendan, or exist but we don't run into them like we did with the Unovan and Kalosian protags? (Both Red and Leaf exist in the WAAP Tverse, for the record.)

    • If we do actually run into them, should we actually name one as the "canon" one, or leave it ambiguous as to which one was the one chosen by Tapu Koko, if not both? Teams? Possible chance of appearing as a Title Defense-style challenger or as a "postgame" battle?

    Gary Blue Motherfucking Oak

    • We know that Red and Blue have been asked to be the new heads of the Battle Tree on Poni Island, and with that in mind, what does that mean for the Viridian Gym back in Kanto? (Especially if we decide to go back to Kanto for an arc in the future.) Does Blue have a rule that only people with seven Kanto badges can challenge him like in the games and commutes between Kanto and Alola like the Gen IV Brains do between Johto and Sinnoh? Did Giovanni somehow take back his old spot since he can be fought at the PWT without incident? Is someone else Gym Leader now?

    You Got My Ash in your Greninja!

    • Should P Cs be able to obtain Greninja with the ability Battle Bond? If so, how? Do they necessarily have to turn into Ash-y Greninja or do they change to suit their Trainer's appearance instead given Ash is of intentionally ambiguous existence in WAAPT canon? Do the mechanics of how it works operate like the anime, the games, or some combination thereof?
    We make a great team.

    Okay, these are my initial responses to the issues presented in the Sun/Moon incorporation post. Anything I don't have an immediate response to will be skipped for now for purposes of streamlining this post.

    Solgaleo and Lunala

    • I think both of the Altars/Lakes of the Sunne/Moone coexisting seems like the best option to me.

    Ultra Beasts

    • Since they're canonically part of species that can come in multitudes in at least certain cases, are powered up by the Ultra Wormholes in initial encounters, have ridiculously minmaxed stats compared to Legendaries, and are implied at certain points in-game as not being all that different from normal Pokemon in the end, I view them as more akin to the pesudolegendaries than actual Legendaries and thus would designate them as "capturable, but with limits akin to Deltas (The Delta Clause)". Plus, the J-Team being recruited to round up errant Ultra Beasts is one potential hook for the Alola arc having an actual structured plot.

    plus i think the idea of robin having a guzzlord is hilarious

    • I think the Ultra Beasts having a dimensional "patch" that lets them speak normal Monese via the Ultra Wormhole like we tossed about a bit in the preplanning sounds solid. Having humans and Pokemon with previous extradimensional experience being the only ones who can communicate with them at first is an okay-if-not-ideal backup plan.

    • I also support some Canon Welding of Totem Boost and Beast Boost with Aura Nexuses and/or the Gates, though tentatively unsure of the nature of it.

    • "Was a Faller" is ripe for initial PC abuse, so I'd put it as "secondary NP Cs only."

    Type: Null

    • I'm torn. On one hand, it'd be kinda unfair to snatch the opportunity for people to get this Pokemon away, especially since the existing ones are kind of tied up. On the other hand, I'm not sure PEFE finding a more humane way to create them and put them under the Delta Clause rather than the Legendary Clause is feasible, given they still need a heavy metal mask strapped over their heads to avoid going feral at first. I'm legitimately pretty conflicted here, so don't have much else to say.

    Anabel's Faller Fiasco

    • I'd be in full support of two Anabels at once, (with the Interpol one coming from a 'verse no J-Teamer has gone before inb4 canon Emerald timeline) written by different writers at once, if only for the inevitable shenanigans between the dual Anabels. I wouldn't be particularly miffed if we just retconned the existing Anabel's backstory the way Bitters suggested, though, since that would open up its own shenanigans.

    Island Trials

    • I'd support making the Trials the entry fee for the League.

    Arc Plot

    • Now see here, I am all for this arc having a coherent plot, but very decidedly against making the arc the actual game plot BUT WITH THE J-TEAM for reasons related to why one of my most vital endgame plots that started with a similar premise is currently in limbo. Putting 1-3 people into a plot meant for one person is fine. Putting 4-6 is still feasible, but can be like herding cats due to variation in player motivation and investment. Putting several dozen will result in nothing but an incoherent mess where everyone is screaming "WHY AM I NOT MORE IMPORTANT" at once, and designating one person the protagonist is still messy since that leaves the question of who and the fact it leaves literally everyone else doing... Almost exactly what they'd be doing with no coherent plot, save for a few given scenes. The plot of Sun and Moon could be feasibly heavily rewritten as a thing the whole J-Team would do, but the whole point of that suggestion is "Sun and Moon's plot was really good, let's do it ourselves" and rewriting it that heavily would retcon the actual plot enough to not be worth it.

    HOWEVER! I still support this arc having an actual coherent plot with a new antagonist, so perhaps we could use a heavily bastardized version of the game's plot as a base for our own with a new villain or set of villains (perhaps based on Dole/Chiquita for satire points?) as a "Pokemon Sun and Moon 1.5" plot. It'd let us have the good stuff of the actual plot with less of the mess of cramming everyone into the actual protagonist role or something.

    Catching Ultra Beasts

    IMO, while it's true that in-game the Ultra Beasts, Buzzwole and Pheremosa in particular, were capable of going toe to toe with the Tapu, there are some mitigating factors:

    1) They're implied to be powered up by the Ultra Wormholes, so a battle without them might not be as one-sided as it seems.

    2) While the Beasts are still pretty powerful, statwise they can have some pretty glaring weaknesses because of the Minmaxing most of them have in common (Buzzwole having an absolutely pathetic 53 base Special Defense for instance), which makes me feel that storywise being able to take one out with a precise hit aimed at their weak stat wouldn't be all that out there.

    Also, there is this theme throughout the UB postgame sidequest and in Pokmon Refresh that despite their power and physical strangeness, in the end Ultra Beasts are no different than other mons. Which is why I feel that those who have been Armbanded should be able to understand Ultra Beasts just like any other mon, not to mention that Ultra Beasts (Primarily Nihilego and Guzzlord, but others have probably shown up as well.) have been sighted throughout the world for thousands of years, for all we know the civilization that created Armbands managed to encounter them as well.

    That said, I do feel that Ultra Beasts should be under the Delta Clause of case by case basis for catching, as they're something newbies can easily abuse.
