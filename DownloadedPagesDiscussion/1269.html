    31716 keys2tkingdom30th Dec 2016 01:05:30 PM from a world of my own Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Maxing my social links
    I am thou. . . Thou art I. . .
    Pokesona Part II Revamp
    After taking into account several complaints and suggestions I got during the first part of Pokesona and the fact that dungeons ran so long people lost interest, I came up with a Revamped idea for Part II. Specifically we'll be dropping the "Evolved Stage" of our PCs' Personas and Pokesonas and moving directly to their Ultimate stages. Signature Moves, however, will still only be unlocked on the Final battle against Izanami.

    I never finished my PC's Dungeon, what do I do about that?

    For simplicity's sake I must ask everyone to assume that there dungeon was completed within 18 hours of IRP time once the group left for the Velvet Room and the CU. If you actually want to finish your PC's Dungeon and show all that happened there, I think it might be best to create a Pokesona TS thread in the second forum so it doesn't take away from the action currently happening in the main thread.

    So how will Ultimate Personas and Ultimate Pokesonas work?

    I decided to take a P1 and P2 approach to Ultimate Personas - i.e. Ultimate Personas were achieved by attaining key items that had a symbolic connection to the Persona users' character development and if they returned to the Velvet Room with said Key Item, Igor would use the emotions contained in the Key item and use it a fusion with the character's Persona. And thus, their Persona would evolve into their Ultimate Persona.

    To this end, All Contractors in the RP will receive mask-shaped trinkets. These Trinkets will have the naming theme "The Mask of [X]" and will resonate with the contractor when the contractor strikes upon their Character development, which in turn will cause their Persona or Pokesona to evolve to their Ultimate Persona or Ultimate Pokesona.

    Yes, Digimon fans, the Key Items in the early Persona games and the masks IRP work basically just like the tags and crests did in Season 1. It wasn't intentional and I only realized it after I wrote it out the first time. We don't need to make a bigger deal about it other than a lampshade or two.

    So how do I get my character's mask to resonate?

    I am completely leaving it up to you! Thanks to some very helpful tips I got from Verax about GMing, I am taking a very hands off approach for this part and will only ask for an edit if a post derails the plot so completely there would be absolutely no recovery from it. So feel free to come up with creative ideas!

    So how does this affect Izanami's Guardians?

    It affects them very little. All it really affects is the amount of lead up to the battles with them.

    • Guardian #1 will still cause a tactical retreat and force a week long tutoring session.
    • Guardian #2 will still abduct a J-Team Member (this may get tweaked depending on availability and consent) and use use them as People Puppet for a P4 Secret Boss-style boss fight.
    • Guardian #3 will still be a fight followed by a moment that basically is "Enough! I'm Just Going to Shoot Them!" before some guests arrive.
    • Guardian #4 will still involve Marie
    • And the Final Guardian will still be a pseudo Boss Rush of Reapers & Thames and Magatsu Izanagi> The Final Guardian.

    Then what?

    Then we'll take a short break to set up for the Izanami Battle. And then we actually finish this thing.

    Anything else?

    I'll send PMs to those who are involved with certain aspects of the Guardian Battles and will field questions regarding this post.

    EDIT: I am also willing to give more details regarding the Guardians' motivations and boss encounters if anyone thinks that that they might be useful regarding their getting PC's masks to resonate.
