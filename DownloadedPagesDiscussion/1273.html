    Though, since main plot discussion seems to be getting to a place where if we had to start the arc today for whatever reason, we wouldn't be floundering in the water too much... going to see where we stand on the minor plot points...

    None of these are really set in stone yet, so if people have other opinions feel free to speak up.

    League Champion: I think if people have an opinion it seems to be 'Leave it up to the challenger.'

    Skull's presence seems to be 'They could still be a threat if people want to do stuff with them'. I think something similar is suggested for Aether's villainous side, but as a shootoff, maybe.

    Most people seem fine with multiple Lunala, though rather that neither is stated to be Nebby, so I think that's what we'll go with. Of course, Keys hasn't spoken up yet, so if he doesn't end up writing his Lunala it might just be Uill.

    There seems to be more support for 'catchable with limits' (at least Uill, B, Tagg, Dune, Umbra, me) than 'no catching' (at least Silent, Every, Verax [I think]), so current direction seems to be catchable, with permission both in and out of universe needed beforehand. If I misrepresented anyone or there are other opinions on the matter, again, feel free to make them known.

    UB language we seem to be pretty evenly split on whether they speak standard Monese or UBese and unlike some of the other times we've had to vote, I think I can offer another compromise on this one. That being whether a UB speaks standard Monese or UBese depends on the UB.

    How would that work? Well, sometimes even within a language, dialects can be so different from each other that two people speaking in dialects won't always understand each other. I know German's that way and I think Japanese is too, though I could be wrong. Since UBs are known for being territorial I could see dialect differences making it hard even among them. But anyway...

    Let's say someone prefers a UB who just speaks standard Monese. They can do that. I figure it would be like a noticable but still understandable accent. If someone prefers a UBese-speaking UB? That's fine too! People can decide how well they want to understand different UBese speakers. It's also entirely possible for standard Monese-speaking UBs to not be able to understand UBese or different UBs to not even understand each others' UBese, but I figure cases like that would be in the minority, if still a possibility. Plus, if UBs show up in later Mystery Dungeon games, regardless of whether they're intelligible to non-UBs or not, we can keep going assuming they're in one group or the other and keep both approaches valid.

    But anyway, yeah, would people be fine with that as a compromise?
