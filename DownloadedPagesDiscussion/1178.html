    Dogs: 4Chan has similar threads; I got this◊ pic of Robin and Sam off one of /vp/'s. Tread lightly in such places, however.

    Speaking of which... Verax and Bitters had some concerns about the Incursionplot that I felt should be addressed here FAQ-style:

    Who are these random characters showing up in your posts now your normal characters are in Glitchstuck Wars?

    They're characters that have either already appeared or are due to appear in my shared fic setting the Yangverse, which draws heavy spiritual inspiration from WAAPT. It's something I've been developing the past couple years and intended to be my primary Pokemon writing outlet when I leave WAAPT, and Heroes After All will have two versions set in it and the plot-isolated past of the WAAPTverse, respectively.

    Why are they showing up so abruptly?

    It's part of the point; an anomaly heavily tied to the metaphysical one the glitch used to trap Team Umbra and co. has similarly trapped these characters in the WAAPTverse, and they're searching for the root of the problem to get home.

    Why are they joining in on other people's plots then?

    Simple, because I was having a hard time getting what I needed done in the right timeframe trying to get other players involved myself, so felt tying their plot into others might be a better solution. Of course if I'm wrong I'm willing to adjust things.

    Why'd you give Herbert's new villain Legendaries?

    Well, I actually did clear it with Herbert first, complete with telling him the species (he can confirm I did here if he wishes); he felt it'd be good for upping the monpower in a potential boss fight, (especially since rescue team has powerful allies and his villains have... Batastrophe and co.) and the villainous Legendaries were important to the Incursionplot and thus going to show up anyway. (Note: This seems to have been main point of objection, so extra-willing to discuss more.)

    How powerful are Yangverse Legendaries compared to WAAPTverse Legendaries anyway?

    About the same on their home turf; trapped in the WAAPTverse noticeably weaker for metaphysical reasons.

    Will this plot have a lasting effect on the WAAPTverse in any way?

    Absolutely not. That'd be pretty stupid and a dick move on my part,

    Are the Yangverse characters sticking around?

    Well, I couldn't do much with them in their home canon if they did, could I? Once this plot and Glitchstuck Wars are both over they'll all be sent straight home and all but a few of them will remember this as a weird dream.

    Hopefully that clears the important stuff up; as for questions from me to you guys:

    • Any other concerns I should address?

    • Does any of this leeriness tie into anything against my out-of-RP writing? If so, why? Please be honest. (For record, Verax and Bitters both said a sound no and I suspect rest of you will too, but I'm me and still wanted to check.)
