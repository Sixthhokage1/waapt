    I thought long and hard about how Ark got to the Lostlorn forest in the first place. This is what I came up with. It is non-canon until it's approval.

    Pre-Penumbra-backstory

    Ark was Born: Arcadia Phantasmia

    Location: Nimbasa City

    Family members:

    Adoptive:

    Mother: Ashling

    Father: Abel

    Brother: Kiaran

    Blood:

    Father: Talib Phantasmia (60)

    Mother: Alcina Phantasmia (58)

    Brothers: Keenan (18) Phantasmia Notes: Currently Keenan is messing around in Unova, participating in musicals and the Pokémon league. Has completed Channeler training.

    Sister: Lilith Phantasmia (deceased at 12 years old)

    Grandparents: Miasmia Phantasmia (deceased) Arkos Phantasmia (deceased)

    Others:

    Abra(past)/(now) M.D. Kazam (Alakazam) Note: Runs the rehab ward Ark was quarantined in.

    Adam Lazarus: He is the only one who still visits the strange house. He has developed a huge one-sided grudge against Darkrai and anything resembling them. He had a childhood crush on Lilith, and was heartbroken by her passing. Is doing research into the Spirit World and Darkrai. Mentally unstable.

    History:

    The Phantasmia clan is not as well known in the modern age as they were in the ages of Ransei, but are still well known in occult and otherworldly power circles. The Phantasmia are a clan of powerful channelers, ones who can ignore the barrier between the living and the deceased. They can make contact with and channel those who have passed on to the Spirit World. One of the things that make the clan unique from others is that they're one of the few groups of Channelers in Unova and a fairly powerful one. One of the many reasons and events that the clans have fallen out of the public eye in the modern years, is the failed curing of the Eternal Nightmare on a child by Arkos Phantasmia, Ark’s grandfather. Eternal Nightmare is exactly what it sounds like, and is caused by contact with Darkrai.

    This was the reason that years later, Darkrai decided to visit the current residence of the current Phantasmia clan heads. At the time, only the youngest daughter Lilith was home due to her parents taking her Abra to the Pokemon center. Lilith was a powerful channeler who was very sensitive to otherworldly energy. Almost immediately after meeting Darkrai, Lilith fell into a seemingly eternal nightmare. Darkrai tried to help, but the family assumed it was done on purpose and chased him off.

    Lilith’s Abra was perhaps the most distraught, as he blamed himself for getting sick. The family tried everything in their power to help her, but only found out and acquired the Lunar Wing when it was too late. The distraught pair abandoned the house to ruin and moved far away to Nimbasa City to start over. Ghost Pokémon flocked to the house in droves, due to the residual spiritual energy there, and Lilith invoking the mutual aid contract out of loneliness. The house became known as the strange house over time, due to Lilith and other Ghost Pokemon haunting it. 10 years later, Arcadia and Keenan Phantasmia were born. It was only a year later that tragedy struck once again. A pair of thieves broke into the house on Halloween. They were both wearing Darkrai masks. The only one home was the now Kadabra, who completely freaked out upon seeing the masks. In a panic he teleported the thieves away… along with Arcadia. Whoops. The thieves were later chased out of the Lostlorn by the Penumbra Clan, ditching the toddler to presumably die. Kadabra left the residence in shame, and started studying for a degree in psychology. Many years later, as an Alakazam known as M.D. Kazam, he opened the Kazam Rehabilitation Center in Castelia City.

    More years later, When Ark was quarantined in his center, he never approached her out of a combination, of guilt, cowardice, and fear. He knew she was Arcadia, the DNA tests showed that much, but how was he supposed to show this feral girl to the Phantasmias?! He just… had to fix her first.

    Notes: The only real 100% for certain effects that this back-backstory will have on Ark is that she will be very vulnerable to possession by ghost types. Ark does have the potential to learn to Spirit Channel, but it’ll take an astronomical amount of character development and convincing to even get her to try to learn, so it’s very unlikely to happen. This also opens up potential future plotlines for Ark and others, such as Lilith, Adam, and the Spirit World. Assuming Lilith was conceived when Alcina and Talib were still young, then that along with the 10 year gap between Ark and Keenan being born, should be enough time for some asshats to have locked Nico in the strange house. I don’t think that making Lilith Ark’s sister will really affect things too much. Hell, Ark really wouldn’t care. They never met, she’s dead, and as far as Ark’s concerned, only happen to have been born from the same parents. Getting Ark to even interact with her birth family once again requires the astronomical amount of character development. The Darkrai masks aren't really significant in anyway. Darkrai seems like it would be a really popular costume or subject for Halloween, and would allow the thieves to blend into the crowds fairly well.

    WAAPT RFC #3338: "Summer Camp"

    So, an idle idea I came up with for this summer to flex our creative muscles:

    WAAPT Summer Camp!

    Here's the plan, and the plan may change with feedback:

    • There is a "signup fee" of a one-sentence RP prompt to seed a pool.
    • Every week (maaaaybe biweek or month), participants submit another prompt into the pool, and in turn get a random RP prompt to try and work into the week (biweek/month)'s activities.
    • If necessary, you can pass on a prompt, or do the prompt but in a non-canon fashion. Still, it's probably most fun to try.

    Some possible prompt examples:
    • "cook a meal"
    • "get lost at the mall"
    • "explore a Unovan bridge"
    • "META: illustrate a post by mashing up Google Image Search images in your editor of choice"
    • "META: include a poem or song lyrics in a post"
