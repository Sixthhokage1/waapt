    Thanks for the warm welcome back, guys. With regards to the chat summons being discussed a couple of pages back, I'm not actually sure when it's supposed to be happening, though I'd appreciate letting me know as I assume my presence is kind of required. Also I've been told that "dressing-down" was probably too strong a word for it, so apologies for that misunderstanding.

    Anyway, I've got a character bio ready for submission, which I hope is okay. It's a little vague, but that's mostly to try and stay in keeping with the character. And yes, this probably does mean that I'm coming back, though I doubt I'll be around as frequently as I was in my hayday.

    Trainer:

    • Name: Minstrel
    • Age: Somewhere in his mid-to-late teens
    • Gender: Male
    • Organisational Ties: Not a permanent member of any organisation, but has worked for or with several groups as a freelancer in the past.
    • Description: Constantly changing for the reasons described below.
    • Backstory: A wandering performance artist and Coordinator with a flair for the dramatic, Minstrel takes the adage that "all the world's a stage" a bit too literally. He's a self-proclaimed master of disguise who revels in being able to change identities as easily as other people change clothes, using nothing more than stage makeup and other perfectly mundane devices to do so. At times, he's put this skill to use in more serious contexts - working undercover for the Rangers, for instance - but he much prefers simply showing off his disguises and neatly deflects questions relating to his past. He's outgoing and bombastic, keen to meet new people and drag them into his performances. However, he has a tendency to get lost in character, and often seems to forget that reality isn't just a theatrical production being put on for his amusement.

    Pokemon:

    • Species: Liepard
    • Nickname: Bard
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Limber
    • Personality: Bard is just as good at appearing and disappearing as his trainer, if not better, and can frequently be found lurking in Minstrel's shadow. He fancies himself a gentleman and a scholar who enjoys the finer things in life, though in practice he's little more than a well-spoken thief with a penchant for particularly shiny trinkets.

    • Species: Bouffalant
    • Nickname: Troubadour
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Sap Sipper
    • Personality: Dragging an entire dressing room around with you can be rather cumbersome when you live out on the road, but thankfully Minstrel has Troubadour to take care of that problem. He quite literally carries his trainer's wardrobe on his back. Most Pokemon might find such a task tiring or tedious, but not Troubadour - he's a veritable fashionista who enjoys helping Minstrel pick out new outfits and disguises.

    • Species: Kecleon
    • Nickname: Poet
    • Gender: Female
    • Ability: Color Change
    • Personality: Poet serves as the oft-ignored voice of sanity on Minstrel's team, trying to keep her trainer grounded and making sarcastic commentary at his expense when that proves impossible. Her main role is as Minstrel's Contest partner, though whilst she enjoys the showing off and the spectacle she'd much rather be involved in a straight-up battle.

    Let me know if there's any issues or general concerns.
