    YO MOTHERFUCKERS

    As you all were hopefully told, I've been in the hospital and then recuperating at my parents' house for the last week or so due to the arm problem getting even worse, making me even more out-of-commission than usual. The good news, though, is I'm back home, I've got access to my actual computer again, and the symptoms are starting to actually improve for once and my family and the doctors have a better plan for getting them to keep improving, so I'm planning on finally jumping back into the WAAPT action! For real. Honest. No really.

    However, there are a few things I'd like to address first:

    Odinplot

    I haven't been following the thread all week, but when I checked today... Yeah, seems we've reached Sootopolis. So Odinplot will have to be T Sed. >_>

    Good news is that I had no other Mossdeep-specific plans and only one Sootopolis-specific one (That's just a gag that can also be T Sed if I even actually do it) so if I actually get my ass in gear this time there's still time to get it done. I hope to have one or two more planning sessions early this week (hopefully starting tomorrow), to work out the one thing that's still kind of a question mark, then start going from there (though I'll have to work out when to prewrite the important bits).

    Do note I won't be doing anything else in-RP aside from HTYD until the Odinplot is done, since it's one of my most important loose ends and after all these delays getting it actually done is my biggest priority.

    A Little Robin Problem

    This is all about RP!Robin and some apparent nuances to his story that flew right over my head in my experiences with his home game and need dealing with. Naturally like anything to do with RP!Robin it's rife with spoilers so I'll spoilertext it all for you newbies who want to play his game but somehow haven't gotten around to it (Fire Emblem Awakening, natch.)

    I happened to be glancing at the Video Games Section of TVT's own Alternative Character Interpretation page and learned from there AND Grima's entry on Awakening's character page that Grima implied that Bad End!Robin willingly became one with him, with varying reasons offered on both pages. Since, again, I completely missed ,anything like that in the game itself, I did some digging and while a quote by Grima on the character page implying it rather suspiciously didn't show up anywhere else on the internet, I did find this, with a few lines that do kind of imply it. Depending on if said implication is true and the exact details, this could conflict with RP!Robin's backstory and character quite a bit, since he IS Bad End!Robin. So I looked over the possible reasons and came up with some of my own to evaluate.

    *Robin was secretly a huge dick who backstabbed everyone to become a god- yeah, not doing it, completely incompatible with RP!Robin and contradicted by in-game evidence.

    *Grima was lying to get under Good End!Robin's skin- simplest, most compatible with the WAAPT versions of both characters, but feels kinda hand-wavey.

    *Willingly joined due to lesser bonds with team: Possible, since only Tiki and Chrom have been mentiioned much, but I mostly did that because of the vaugeness of the Bad End and for some reason the others haven't had reason to come up much, and when they DO come up it's contradicted.

    * Gave in after being mind-controlled into killing Chrom. This could work and I have a delicious scene in mind, but unsure how to execute it

    * Something about Robin's home variation of the FEA timelines is... off somehow. There's things I've done that imply it, but it would need an explanation and elaboration.

    Please, anyone else with knowledge of the game, give me advice as to which to go with or offer suggestions of your own. Both really help!

    I was going to add a third thing also involving Robin, but this post took me three hours to write and it's late so I'll save it for later. For now I'm just glad to be home.
