    Considering various stances people have on spoilers, I've know people to avoid the official site because they don't want to know anything past a certain point. Here's what I suggest based on the strictest personal spoiler policy I'm aware of:

    1. Any new Pokemon or Evolution beyond Rowlet, Litten, Popplio, and the Box Legendaries should be considered spoilers - i.e. Don't blab about any new mon you see on the offical site or in Coro Coro leaks or if they confirm what past gen mon are going to be available in Alola

    2. Any new mechanics or devices are spoilers - stuff like New Moves, abilities, the Poketch, P*DA, etc

    3. Specific locations within Alola are spoilers - i.e. Don't go screaming through the chats and threads if GF throws a curve ball and gives us a Dark-type Gym, GF make a hotel based on the Hilton Rainbow Tower, etc

    4. All characters beyond the ones we saw in the Stater trailer are Spoilers.- i.e. No mentioning Gym Leaders, Evil teams, E4 members, Champions, etc.

    5. The Regional Professor, The Rival and The Mother are not spoilers, but any details about them beyond their names and pictures are. - Don't say anything if, for example, GF reveals that if the mother like making cloths so she makes all sorts of special outfits for you, if GF reveals The Professor has a specialized field of study, or if GD reveals the Rival has a unique hobby etc.

    All spoilers written in the thread should be marked as such and be written in Spoiler text and only spoken freely via PMs, private chats, designated threads, etc.
