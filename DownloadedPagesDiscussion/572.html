    Titan works for Alexiel, so I assume he'd also be working in the Resources department. He's probably the one who holds the fort whilst Alexiel is away on his excursions.

    Speaking of Cipher, I finally got around to writing a bio for Aelia, since as the first named "shady scientist" she's probably going to be showing up fairly often.

    Cipher Character

    Name: Aelia Morgan

    Gender: Female

    Age: 24

    Affiliations: Works as a researcher for Cipher as a contractor, but is not formally employed by the organisation (yet). In terms of rank, she would be equivalent to a Peon.

    Region of Origin: Sunyshore City, Sinnoh

    Appearance: Aelia is of medium height and is fairly slender. She has long blonde hair tied back in a ponytail and blue eyes. Usually she wears half-rimmed glasses and a lab coat, but will abandon this in favour of a Cipher Peon uniform if she believes the situation necessitates it.

    Personality: Aelia's defining trait is probably her arrogance. She is convinced of her own genius and apathetic to those around her, to the point where her reason for working with Cipher is merely to further her own research without moral restrictions. She lacks empathy and is a casual sadist, often carrying out experiments for her own amusement rather than any real scientific purpose. Though she hates taking orders from authority figures, believing that she should be the one in charge, she is perfectly willing to follow her orders to the letter - mostly in the hope that doing so will earn her a promotion.

    History: Aelia was born and raised in Sunyshore, and like most Pokepan residents received her starter on her 10th birthday. She had no interest in being a trainer, however, and left for Holon to become a researcher. From there, she has worked for anyone willing to tolerate her lack of ethics. Though she has taken contracts from Cipher on many occasions, she does not actually work for the organisation (yet) since she would rather keep her options open.

    Pokemon

    Aelia doesn't bother naming her Pokemon, since people who enjoy torturing mons for fun generally aren't renowned for forming emotional attachments to them.

    • Species:    Shadow    Prinplup
    • Gender: Female
    • Biography: Formerly Aelia's starter, Prinplup was Shadowed by Cipher as a "reward" after one of her contracts was completed. Prior to her Shadowfication, Prinplup was shy and often wondered why her trainer never paid her much attention - now, though, she is the same mindless killing machine as any other Shadow mon. Aelia doesn't keep Prinplup with her whilst she's working in Holon, as an unrecallable Shadow Pokemon is impossible to keep under control.

    • Species: Delta Luxray (Electric/Steel typing)
    • Gender: Male
    • Biography: Luxray is Aelia's main Pokemon whilst she is working in Holon, and like her he loves tormenting others for his own amusement. He is highly hostile, growling at anyone he considers suspicious - which, considering that Aelia often works with Cipher, is most people.

    • Species: Delta Golbat (Poison/Rock typing)
    • Gender: Male
    • Biography: Aelia's first Golbat is largely indifferent to the world around him. Unlike Luxray, he doesn't particularly enjoy sadism for sadism's sake - he follows Aelia mostly because she keeps him fed.

    • Species: Delta Golbat (Poison/Ground typing)
    • Gender: Male
    • Biography: Aelia's second Golbat is something of a coward, only battling when he has no other alternative or when Aelia is yelling at him to do so. He follows Aelia out of fear of what she'll do to him if he doesn't.

    Let me know if there's any thoughts/concerns/comments.
