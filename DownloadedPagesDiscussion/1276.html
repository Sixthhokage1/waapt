    Alright, Best Berry character time, I suppose!

    Name: Culverton Blake

    Age: Early-to-mid 40s

    Gender: Male

    Ethnicity: Black Unovan

    Appearance: Culverton is a man of unremarkable height and build. The first thing most people notice about him is his bizarre hair - Day-Glo green dreadlocks that go down to his shoulders, tapering off to thin points that give his hair a strong resemblance to leaves. His face is surprisingly youthful for a man definitely no younger than 40, with a constant boyish energy twinkling in his black eyes. Culverton's typical wardrobe consists of the following - a long orange-brown blazer, with ornate buttons shaped like various Berries; long fabric gloves of a similar shade to his coat, with metal studs on the knuckles; white dress pants; comfortable fabric loafers, once again in the same shade as his coat; and, last but not least, a large pair of aviator goggles perched on his forehead, which do actually serve a purpose due to Culverton being a licensed pilot who frequently attends far-flung business meetings by quaint biplane.

    Ties: VP Acquisitions and Mergers, Best Berry Inc.

    Region of Origin: Lentimas Town, Unova

    Personality: On the surface, Culverton Blake can come off as nothing more than a cheerful, foppish eccentric, albeit one with a certain gift of the gab. However, even brief private conversations will quickly reveal a far more intellectual and cunning side to his personality that makes it clear he didn't become a prominent businessman through mere luck. In reality, any impression of foolishness Culverton gives off is an act - sure, he's genuinely flamboyant, but confuse his liveliness and quick talking with scatterbrainedness, or underestimate his ability to appeal to others with carefully applied affability, and you'll quickly find yourself outplayed. Indeed, Culverton enjoys covertly flirting with the dark side of business so much that he's started to become bored - hence his recent interest in working with criminal organisations...

    Backstory: Born and raised in Lentimas Town, Culverton Blake's business acumen developed early - as a schoolchild, he ran a playground smuggling ring that provided such commodities as sweets and toys to the bored kids of the isolated, arid desert town, all while carefully managing his operations to ensure he never openly broke the school rules, let alone the law. It was a short trip from there to business college, after which he spent years working middle management positions with various Unovan businesses. Coworkers universally recall him as likable, yet with a slight hint of shadiness that only came out if you spent too long around him - but he was a highly efficient employee, so the bosses never complained.

    After a long period of slowly climbing the ranks and building up a respectable CV, Culverton eventually left Unova for Alola, deciding it would be easier to wring opportunities from a relatively unspoilt natural paradise than the crowded Unovan business scene. It was here that he met a mysterious young entrepreneur by the name of Sam Laughlin, with whom he became one of the founders of a fruit distribution company by the name of Best Berry Inc. As of right now, not much of interest to anyone but those obsessed with fruit production has happened with Best Berry, but that's all about to change...

    While living in Castelia City, Culverton met his wife Tanya, a lawyer who currently works for Best Berry's legal team. They have two university-age children named Uzuri and Gaius.

    Pokemon Team

    Culverton specialises in Fairy-type Pokemon. His team's Theme Naming revolves, fittingly, around fruit.

    Species: Primarina

    Gender: Male

    Ability: Torrent

    Description: Dorian is possibly the most vain creature on the planet. Almost every word he speaks is devoted to how beautiful and graceful he is, with the few that aren't usually being scathing insults directed at anything he perceives as a threat to his beauty. As a result of this self-obsession, Dorian is considerably more amoral than his trainer, being quite willing to do anything so long as he doesn't have to get dirty or put up with ugly peoplemons.

    Species: Ribombee

    Gender: Male

    Ability: Shield Dust

    Personality: Anansi considers himself a practical joker, which would be fine if his definition of a practical joke wasn't dangerously broad. Fortunately, his diminutive Ribombee size makes any sort of physical prank hard to pull off, but this just means he's had to master the art of the psychological prank. Basically, don't trust the little bugger farther than you can throw him, and Ribombee travel quite a way when thrown.

    Species: Sylveon

    Gender: Female

This little Sylveon has been entrusted with information Culverton would never tell a human, and with the J-Team on his case that may well come back to bite him - especially given the events of Piper's past...
