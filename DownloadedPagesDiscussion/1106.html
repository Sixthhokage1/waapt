    And sorry to introduce a new topic so soon, but since I've shown enough I feel I can lay it out, I figured it'd be best to do so now-ish.

    Anyway...

    Vineglitch Plot

    What is it?

    A plot for this arc in which characters meant to loosely mirror the villains of FreshPrettyCure go and spread vineglitches for various reasons. (with an emphasis on loosely) Eventually they'll be dealt with themselves.

    What's a Vineglitch?

    Essentially Tangela-esque vines that can be created by anyone who has been vineglitched and has sufficient control to do so and attach to an object or personmon. When attached to an object, it essentially lets them control the object and have it do stuff. When attached to a personmon, it's usually when they're experiencing a strong emotion and the vineglitcher wants to twist that emotion to accomplish something. This often does something odd to them like turn them into a giant or such. They can more or less be dealt with by getting rid of the vines.

    Can [Mon Character of mine] get Vineglitched?

    Most likely, sure! Just bring it up with me and we'll work something out. (Also would be helpful if people want to be involved so I'm hopefully not just pulling characters out of the ether for this.)

    Can I catch a vineglitched wildmon?

    If they're one of the main villains for this (like Eas), I've likely got plans for them, so I'd prefer other people not, though I might be able to be convinced otherwise depending on the reason.

    Any other case, once they're no longer vineglitched, yeah. If anyone has any suggestions for mons they want to introoduce this way I'm up for it.

    Vineglitched is a horrible name for this

    Quite possibly! If you have better name suggestions for this or anything else, I am up for them.

    Anyway, that's the plotthing as it is... Thoughts, concerns, questions? (And still give thoughts on earlier topics too)
