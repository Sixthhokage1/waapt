    30291 Uilleam8th Sep 2016 04:57:21 AM from Bonnie Scotland Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Pining for the fjords
    Egads!
    Darkness: There's no hard limit on how dark or "edgy" a character can be, but you should at least make sure he fits into a verse influenced by typical Pokemon canon, where the world is overall a quite light-hearted Adventure-Friendly World where evil and horror are looming threats rather than a controlling force. I've done some pretty grim shit in my own time and there are plenty of other users who've utilised grimdark characters and story elements, not to mention the franchise itself having completely canon bursts of relatively extreme darkness (the Orre games anyone?), but don't go overboard and make sure they could believably exist in the Pokemon world. Do that, and you should be fine.
