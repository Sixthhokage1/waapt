    31509 keys2tkingdom25th Dec 2016 03:51:46 PM from a world of my own Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: Maxing my social links
    I am thou. . . Thou art I. . .
    Weighing in with my opinions and votes - some of which have changed since I last spoke about them.

    Tapus

    I feel that any mon with true Legendary status has the ability to allow themselves to communicate with other beings whether it be psychic powers, magic, or whatever. That said I think the Tapus can directly communicate with anyone they chose - they communicate with the Kahunas the most frequently since the Kahunas are directly chosen by the Tapus, but don't communicate with the average human or Pokemon unless they find interest with them.

    Ultra Beasts

    Catching Them

    • I agree that they should be treated more like Shadows (extremely dangerous, etc) and one PC can't have too many.

    Understanding them

    • I with the others who say just having been armbanded or understand monese is NOT enough to understand UBs. The character in question would need to be a Faller.

    Fallers

    I am personally for the Secondary Characters only. For the same reason characters with aura and obscura are now restricted - it could lead to over saturation of Fallers when they are obviously not supposed to be very common and players need to ensure that a character meshes well with the RP. The latter is mainly because of an argument along the lines of "My PC's home universe is different, so my PC being a [Stu/Sue] is completely justified." would actually become a valid argument (and imo THAT is a scary thought).

    Type: Null

    I am firmly on the side of having the Legendary Clause include Type: Null. As I think even if the PEFE could somehow find a way to create more in a more humane way, it's still doing what others did to create Mewtwo and Genesect. And that is more on the darker and murkier end of the ethics scale. I have no qualms with villains and evil teams doing it, as it just confirms that they have little to no qualms of ignoring ethics and morals.

    Arc Plot

    I am incredibly in favor of adapting the SM plot with the J-Team being the MCs. And I really like Dune's idea for the plot, since it provides challenges for older and newer players.

    Z-Moves

    While am very much in favor of maintaining the four move slots, I am also in favor of not limiting to a move that has been used. It allows for extra depth in battle and allows a PC to use a Z-Move as their first attack (Z-Conversion springs to mind as an example for the latter point).

    The way I thought of is having to publicly say what move that one is using to convert to a Z-Move. If you less than four slots active, you can freely choose which one of the attacking mon's moves are used to convert into the Z-Move. But if that mon has Already used four moves in a Battle, you have to select one of those for moves.

    Although this is for regulated battles. in fight for your life scenarios it wouldn't be limited the four move limit or One Z-Move per battle.

    i.e. Z-Move spam is possible, but the trade off is your character is like to hurt or kill themselves after the first attack depending on their physical condition.

    Z-Rings

    If one isn't directly gift a stone by one of the Tapus - and That's not something that should be common imo - I think a formal request to any one of the Kahunas. It may take a while to get all the the stones needed for the PCs who what them from the Tapus, as a sudden influx of several dozen people wanting Z-Rings all at the same probably isn't the norm.

    Trials and the League

    I agree that the trial should be the prerequisite for the league. That said, I think the Alola League would start to publicize that any challengers who want to challenge the league when it opens need to start working on getting the trials done.

    As for the Trials, I am in favor of the Captains being able to set the trial difficulty based on the challenger. And in Ilima's case even get to decide which Totem appears - i.e. Ilima can override the day/night appearance thing if he wants to make it more challenging.

    As far as the E4 goes, I think them multitasking is the way to go.

    Champion

    I'm a bit more receptive to rotating Champions, but with the caveat that none of J-Team gaining the title of Champion until after the arc. And I very much support the idea of Jacob getting named Champion of Alola as the Alola Arc ends as that works exceeding well with his Verax's idea for Jacob.

    Time Line

    Since we've already thrown out the Canon Timeline for the RP!Delta Episode - since if we followed canon, the RP!Delta Episode would have happened right after what would have been Gamer taking down Aqua and Magma - I see no reason that SM could have happened later than canon too.

    Rotomdexes

    I think base Rotomdexes can be widely available (note I mean just the non-possessed device by itself, not as the sentient, possessed device we all know and love), but the only way for a Rotom to actually possess one it to actually acquire one for yourself. Which for native Alolans means importing one.

    Ride Pokemon

    The anime shows Wailmer being an alternative to Lapras for the "slower paced surfer" that can carry two riders. This means that we could have our P Cs' Pokemon could be Ride Pokemon.

    However, I personally think there might be a Driver's License-esque test and approval think where a Pokemon is deemed capable of being a Ride Pokemon and a human PC is capable of handling Ride Pokemon. Unlike a Driver's License, I imagine the minimum age is MUCH lower. Like 11 (10 is the age one can get ta trainer license) - enough time for for a trainer to have some experience with higher level mon, but doesn't restrict Our P Cs in any severe way.

    Further, I also think it should be possible for PCs to register other character's mon as Ride Pokemon so long as they have permission from the owner.

    Battle Bond

    I'm ambivalent on Battle Bond, but I do agree if we do implement it IRP it should be available to mon other than Greninja.
