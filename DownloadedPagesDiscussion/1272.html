    Also, since I think we're in a bit of a lull/limbo for Alola Arc discussion, I think it's time for a compilation post of what's been decided in the meantime and what still needs to be in hopes of spurring discussion. (I'd prefer to stick to voting on the Undecided stuff, but if someone wants to being the Decided back up for Discussion again, we can do that I suppose. As long as we get everything decided in the end)

    Decided

    • We're likely going with B's idea of one Anabel
    • We are also likely going with the Alola Arc happening in the post game.
    • There are good snowy mountain locations for Crabrawler to potentially evolve to Crabominable in most of the past regions

    Undecided

    • Arc Plot:

    I'll go into more detail on this one. I think we should decide on one or more of these and build on them as a group to make sure we have something if people try and just build on separate ideas and they fall through for one reason or another. And since this one's probably the one affecting potentially the most people, let's try and focus on discussion of this

    Anyway, to that end, potential plots seem to be:

    Tagg's suggestion in the first part of this post

    Dune's Team of Cid's (Or at least, I think that's what he's going for)

    The 4 ideas Uill suggested here

    Tangent's Team Dole suggestion

    Zeal's idea here

    Any I might have missed or other things people want to suggest?

    And for more minor stuff...

    • Who takes the role of League Champion? One person specifically? Do we have it in flux and let whoever's league challenge it is decide until/unless we get a more definite Champion?

    • What do we do with Team Skull? I'm fine with B's suggestion of Guzma still bossing them around some or Uill's suggestion for a plot idea involving them. Or are they just disbanded with remnants hanging around Po Town?

    • Are Ultra Beasts catchable, but with limitations (Only with a Beast Ball, only one per person with permission asking beforehand, remember they're going to be more aggressive than non-UB Pokemon) or not catchable, period?

    • Do Ultra Beasts speak like Pokemon of the dimension they've gone to for convenience and the precedent set by previous dimensional travellers, or something different because they're too different from the other travellers for them to really be precedent?

    • Where does Crabrawler evolve in Hoenn? Shoal Cave? One of the mountains? Can it evolve in Hoenn?

    • Lunala

    Unlike the other non-main plot concerns, I'll try and suggest some stuff here.

    Pretty much, Keys and Uill both want to be main writers for Lunala. The ways I can see this resolving are like such:

    • We try and decide which one we like more and that one writes for Lunala as a main one (Not too fond of this, it'll really only leave one person happy)

    • The aforementioned suggestion, but whoever doesn't write Lunala writes Solgaleo with the personality they suggested (By the lack of response from Keys or Uill when I suggested this previously, I don't think either of them were too wild about this one either)

    • Lunala doesn't have a main writer and whoever wants to write for them just does so (I have conflicted feelings on this. Sure, it's really convenient for people to just pick up a legendary for a quick thing they need, potentially ignoring precedent to do so, but Uill and Keys both want to write Lunala as a distinct character and I feel like this'll just lead to them duelling with each other over inconsistencies presented by the other)

    • The WAAP Tverse has two Lunala, Solgaleo probably existing, but not showing up as much as a character (This... might be a bit weird to pull off, but I think we could make it work if everyone would be fine with it. Heck, Keys and Uill could potentially write their respective Lunala bouncing off each other, if they want)

    Anyway, yeah, that's everything to decide? Thoughts? I'd like it if we could come to a (near) unanimous decision on most of these, but I'll try and tally people's opinions on this stuff from here on out in case it comes to that.

    Also, I very much acknowledge I'm being hypocritical in saying this, but can we try and focus the discussion at this point on what we want to do rather than what we don't? Focusing on the don't without an accompanying do just kind of leads us in circles and right now we're kind of in a limbo and I'd just prefer we decide on something that'll hopefully make people happy. Heck, I've made it clear I have strong opinions on some of the things we're undecided on, but I just want us to come to some sort of decision at this point, even if it's one I don't like.

    Some bullet points in response to Crow's post:

    • The Skull and Aether ideas were more proposals for a way to maintain the presence of both Team Skull and a villainous Aether Foundation in a post-game plot than ideas for a plot in and of themselves. I honestly lean having Dune's Team Iron as the primary threat and storyline for Alola, since I love the buildup they've had so far.

    • I still don't see why the Ultra Beasts would be unintelligible. Given how many different forms of vocalisation ordinary Pokemon alone have, it's fairly clear to me that understanding "Monese" entails a lot more than simply understanding a single language; why would the UBs be different just because they hail from outside our universe? Most every other caveat that applies to Earth Pokemon ultimately applies to them as well. As for their catchability, I say we should make them capturable, albeit with something similar to the Delta Clause attached - Interpol only have a limited supply of Beast Balls and they won't just give them to any schmuck who asks, but they may well be willing to loan a few to a group of famous heroes.

    • On the thorny issue of Lunala and Solgaleo... well, first off, my main reason for wanting her over Solgaleo is that the personality I came up with for her many moons ago still mostly works now that we know they're not exactly conventional Legendaries. With Solgaleo, though, my personality idea revolved around pride in his Legendary status, which doesn't exactly work anymore now he's established to be a cosmic traveller who doesn't quite fit in with the other Legendaries (plus he was intended as a Foil to my Lunala, which won't work at all if Lunala is portrayed by another writer as something far removed from my idea). As for how to solve the issue? Eh, I think I'll leave that argument untouched for now. As one of the individuals involved, I'd likely just end up being massively biased.

    For my own thoughts...

    I'd like Dole and the UB plot Tagg suggested to exist in some form, if not as the A-plot. And since the Team Iron thing is seeming the most popular... I understand your concerns, Zeal and I think Dune himself expressed that he'd need help with keeping things running, so... ultimately if it did end up being an arc plot, I think we'd need to make sure there's

    • Transparency in what Team Iron's going to do

    Not like, 'we know all your secrets' but stuff like 'We generally know in which direction this is going to go and plan the arc around it'

    • Freedom for people to use Team Iron characters for their own purposes

    Whether that's having certain characters on the top be handled by people other than Dune or freedom to handle grunts or other people affiliated with it as long as we know how they'd generally be expected to behave, it'd let us expand on stuff with it aside from the main plot (which is usually how I like to get my own characters invested in a given plot, personally) and allow it so it isn't just Dune running things, if I explained that okay

    If Dune thinks the plot can work with stuff like this or some variations on it, I'm probably cool with it being the main plot, since hopefully we can make stuff more personal then, maybe

    I likewise prefer flux for the Champion

    Re: Skull, Lunala, and Crabrawler, I'm okay with whatever makes the most people happy

    I prefer 'capturability with limits,' but I can make Legendary Clause work, if I get outvoted

    Language... thinking about the discussion, I've actually kind of flipped and now have a way of the U Bs not being intelligible at first I'd actually be kind of interested in. That said, I understand if it might overcomplicate things or some of the things I think would be integral to how we'd handle it. If people aren't happy with this idea, I'm perfectly happy with just having U Bs speak Monese, but since I might as well suggest it...

    UBese

    So, currently there are two main methods of communication in WAAPT, "Human" and <Monese.> If people think it would be interesting, I'm realizing {UBese} could work as a 'not generally understood at first, but we grow to understand it' in a way I'd be happy with it. Personally, I'm probably going to interpret it like... essentially a variant of standard Monese, but with a really thick accent that makes understanding hard for peoplemons who don't understand it, but I'm not going to push that view IRP for people who'd rather interpret it like a separate language.

    But to continue...

    Who can understand whom

    Let's say we have Human 1, who doesn't understand Monese, Human A who does, a non-UB Pokemon, and a Fresh Out of The Wormhole UB. If none of these peoplemons have had contact with any of the other ones, none of them will be able to understand what the other is saying.

    If we're taking telepathy into account... I kind of headcanon that to being tied to language as it's usually used so, say, assuming a Psychic Sinn Human who doesn't know Monese and a Sinn Kadabra are communicating, they'd be able to understand each other, but if the Kadabra only knows [Quechua], they probably wouldn't be able to unless they somehow thought in... just concepts, I suppose. I guess what I'm saying is 'I don't think they'd be able to understand each other unless they found some way to communicate with just mental images.' I'm willing to bend on that point, though.

    So how do they get to understand each other

    Unlike humans understanding Monese, I don't think there should be some implement that makes UBs, humans, and non-UB Pokemon understand each other instantly rather I think it would be more interesting if they learned to understand each other through exposure to the other. Like, if the group of 4 spent enough time with each other, as friends, enemies, whatever, and learned the intricacies of each other's speech, the UB would be able to understand what the other three are saying and the two that understand Monese would be able to understand the UB in turn. The human who doesn't understand Monese now might be able to understand the UB as soon as they learn Monese, but I could also see them needing to be exposed to the UB more.

    And I guess... that's how it could maybe work, if people are up for that?
