    In order to take a load off ES, I have an offer to you all!

    Would any of you like either the Lucario or Greninja amiibo I've been meaning to give as gifts? (I got them with the 'zard I gave Tangent for his birthday since local Gamefilch had 'em cheap.) Are you also hyped for Sun and Moon? Then I have a fun giveaway in mind!

    Guess A Type Combo, Get An amiibo!

    So, the biggest and yet also least spoilertastic mysteries about Sun and Moon right now are its version mascots. Right now all we have are their looks and leaks of what are likely their names, but one thing's missing: Their types. So my challenge to you, dear WAAP Ters, is this: Guess what Aslan and Moonbat's type combos are!

    Here's how it works:

    • Fill out the following form:

    Your name:

    Which amiibo you want: (Lucario, Greninja, or either)

    Your prediction for the Sun mascot's typing:

    Your prediction for the Moon mascot's typing:

    • Post it here in Discussion so I can compile into a Google Doc.

    • When the mascot type combos are revealed:
      • Any who manage to guess both instantly receive their amiibo of choice! The other goes to the most willing and close runner-up.
      • If you manage to guess one, you're a top pick unless someone guesses them both.
      • Getting half the type right gives you priority unless it's solely the obvious choice (Sun mascot being half-Fire and Moon mascot being half-Flying,respectively). You'll still get priority for guessing one being single-typed correctly, though, or a new type!
      • Complete misses for secondary type will have winners determined by super-effectiveness of actual types against it.
      • All else fails, I'll pick the most creative guesses or ones I would've guessed but got proven wrong.
    • The winners will have their amiibo shipped to them from me straight away, likely with bonuses of some sort! (Note I cannot ship to outside the US, and you must give me your address privately or have someone who can relay the package to you send me theirs; if you are not comfortable with that, please do not enter this "contest").

    But feel free to submit guesses or ask any questions, people.
