    Okay, some more detailed thoughts on the matter from me now that I've thought over some things:

    • Chainable Conversion and Conversion 2 are pretty comparable to Protean. Chainable Reflect Type is mostly Defensive, but could be used offensively in Double Battles. Chainable Camouflage is pretty situational and more difficult to abuse.

    • Might have been better if I had rememebered this last night, but what I'm getting is that the main reason against this idea is that you can use something Protean-esque in tandem with another ability. At the same time, you do lose some move variability, but in practice, it'll depend on whether an extra ability

    • Defensive side, I feel like you can only compare it to chaining Protect if you're exploiting an immunity. If you're exploiting a resistance, it's a bit more like chaining Iron Defense or Amnesia, if you get the attacking type right. Protean and chainable type changing moves would allow more leeway for that, but yeah

    • What Dune said about chaining and Speed of moves

    • For a potential criticism to the idea that we should keep in mind, let's use Porygon-Z (since to my knowledge, due to moves and abilities, it'd get the biggest potential buff):

    Situation 1: Protean Mon uses Resistance to its advantage and attacks with STAB

    x0.5 to opponent's attack, x1.5 to own (equivalent to +2 Def, +1 Atk)

    Situation 2a: Adaptability Conversion-chaining Porygon-Z uses Resistance to its advantage and attacks with STAB

    x0.5 to opponent's attack, x2 to own (equivalent to +2 Def, +2 Atk)

    Situation 2b: Download Conversion-chaining Porygon-Z uses Resistance to its advantage and attacks with STAB on the opponent's lower stat

    x0.5 to opponent's attack, x2.25 to own (equivalent to +2 Def, between +2 and +3 Atk)

    Situation 2c: Analytic Conversion-chaining Porygon-Z uses Resistance to its advantage and attacks with STAB after the opponent

    x0.5 to opponent's attack, x1.95 to own (equivalent to +2 Def, almost +2 Atk)

    That said, the Porygon situations would take longer to pull off, so I guess it's up to people how they feel about that..

    And at least with that in mind... I'm leaning more 'I'm fine with it if we can do it in a way that alleviates everyone's concerns.'
