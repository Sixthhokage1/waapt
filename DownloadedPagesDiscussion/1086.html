    Hrm... Well, to help...

    First off, going with an outright antagonistic PC is a no-no, especially one with genocidal intentions, due to issues of people with villainous or semi-villainous P Cs in the past.

    Second, Blu Beri Pi is correct in that the vast majority of Legendaries - including many of the more menacing ones like Darkrai, Giratina, and Yveltal - are benevolent or at least not truly malicious, and exceptions are few and far in between. And normal mons are individuals that don't necessarily adhere to stereotypes - a Gallade can be a total jerk while a Hydreigon can be a sweetheart.

    And yeah, there's a bit of an in-universe cosmic rule saying Pokemon can't harm humans unprovoked without serious karma.

    That said, I think a character with a grudge/phobia/whatever against Legendaries or a specific normal mon might be viable, but head over to Signups for that?
