    The siren, like the more famous axolotl (And probably the olm of Europe as well), is a species of amphibian that exhibits neotony, which is the retention of traits normally seen solely in young numbers of a species. In their case, it's the gills, aquatic habitat, and lack of back limbs. (Salamanders and newts form the front legs first as opposed to the back legs of frogs and toads, you see.)

    (For an example evolutionarily speaking closer to home, the floppy ears and begging behaviors found in many breeds of dogs.)

    Long answer: We aren't using expies of the party members of any of Persona games for NPCs. And although several of the group's issues and Shadow Selves do resemble several of the Investigation Team's from P4, most of this was unintentional.

    Teddie's role when he became a party member is a magic only Red Mage, getting lots of Ice, Healing, Buffing, and Debuffing spells. In short, Teddie has a severe case of 8-Slot Syndrome. Although IRP the one emulates Teddie's Party Role is probably Mondo and (currently) RP!Pent

    Yousuke's role in the party was basically a Jack-of-All-Stats early game but falling into Master of None in late and post game; getting buffs, debuffs, status, wind magic, status, and physical attacks. Once everyone in the current group gets their own Persona or Pokesona, the one who would probably fit the best is Ren or RP!Silent.
