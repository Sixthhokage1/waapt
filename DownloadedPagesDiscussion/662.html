    Name: Bethany Hudson

    Age: 13

    Gender: Female

    Region of Origin: Unova

    Appearance: Surprisingly tall for her age and gender. Long, slightly wild brown hair, brown eyes.

    Personality: Bethany seeks to be the strongest, but she doesn't really realize what that means. Due to her love of badassery, she exclusively trains dragons. Though she appreciates the cuter members of her team, she loves strength. Therefore, Bethany will never turn down a battle, no matter what disadvantage she has.

    Backstory: Like her brother, Bethany is from Virbank. She asked for dragon eggs for her twelfth birthday. Of course, her parents didn't trust her with a Hydreigon (yet), so they gave her a Swablu egg and a Trapinch egg. Then she set off!

    Pokemon:

    • Thibault the Altaria (Male): Thibault, in stark contrast to the rest of his species, is a serious, humorless curmudgeon. He reacts poorly to hugs, though he doesn't mind hugs from Bethany.

    • Steve the Flygon (Male): Steve is as chill as can be. Nothing fazes him, and he acts friendly and personable to all. Bethany has him out when she wants to get on someone's good side.

    • Altaria the Zweilous (Male): Altaria is, well, an Altaria in a Zweilous' body. He loves hugs, and he loves to sing (though his voice is dreadful)

    • Bonnie the Dragonite (Female): Bonnie is bold and quick-tempered. She rules over the rest of the team, but not unkindly.

    • Grendel the Shelgon (Male): Grendel is charmingly awkward. Were he a human, he would be played by Martin Freeman. In a pinch, though, he can show his line's fearsome reputation.

    • Squishy the Goomy (Male): In the hug category, Squishy is the best of both worlds between Thibault and Altaria in huggability and willingness to hug. The perfect friendly dragon. Caught in Kalos.

    Imperial Commissar
    So, remember when I said that Herbert wouldn't be getting any new mons during Kalos? I might have been lying. I've been thinking about giving him a Fennekin for a while, but I just wanted to make sure that the character idea is acceptable, since her psychic abilities would work rather differently from most Fennekin-line mons.

    Basically, said Fennekin would be an Expy of Dinah Alcott from Worm in terms of powers, if not ability. Dinah's powers are probability determination and limited clairvoyance - she can't see the future, but she can predict the likelihood of a certain course of events coming to pass. The scenario has to be one she can visualise, however. So, to apply this set of powers to the RP, she'd be able to give a chance of success for the next Cipher raid, but not what kind of opposition they'll be facing during it. Outside of hard numbers, she's basically helpless.

    I just wanted to ask to make sure this kind of powerset wouldn't be too OP/special for Herbert's Fennekin.

    Name: Samantha Kearney

    Gender: Female

    Region of Origin: Sinnoh

    Age: 17

    Appearance: Average height, pale skin. Red haired, usually worn in a loose, flowing ponytail. Green eyes (but no freckles). Dresses in various shades of greys and blacks.

    Personality: When we last saw Samantha, she was a smug, condescending Ace Trainer with little skill to back it up. She has trained a lot since then, and now has sufficient skill to back up her claims. She acts condescending and smug towards people she dislikes, but provides undying loyalty to her friends (though not without a significant amount of snark).

    Backstory: Samantha hails from Celestic Town, and decided at a very early age to devote herself to battle. She studied battle for most of her life, but faltered time after time in tournaments. Then, after her loss to Joseph in Holon, she got inspiration and worked that much harder not just to succeed in theory.

    Samantha and Joseph will be at each others throats almost all the time. They see each other as Sitcom Arch Nemeses.

    Pokemon:

    • Rowan the Leafeon (female): Rowan speaks very little, but she doesn't need to. Leaf Blades get various points across easily.

    • Phelps the Floatzel (male): Enthusiastic about battle and...well, everything.
