    So, yeah, I'm feeling loads better after last week, the anxiety dreams seem to have made their exit, and I'm feeling better enough to come back to this thread. Good to be back, guys.

    And as the person who wrote the comission guide page and the list of comission artists, some of my own tips for Major:

    - Autobottestla's the guy you want if you want a comission of an actual Pokemon, they're a specialty of his and he'll gladly do shots of any species, including with a trainer, for relatively cheap.

    - Audgreen is AMAZING at combining whatever refrence images you have into something awesome, and will do colored sketches of single characters for cheap, so a good go-to if you just want a human or a non-Pokemon.

    - Zu-Art is pricier but is really speedy while still being damn good quality, and is also great at nailing human characters with the right refrences.

    - Kynim is fantastic at humanized Pokemon in particular, and while a bit pricey and tending to take a while because she's in    med school   , whatever she produces tends to decidedly be worth both the wait and the money.

    - Mustachossom is another great mix of skilled, speedy, and cheap, and has a really nice style.

    - Miss-Somnis is a freind of Verax's he's trying to signal boost and does humans and humanoids for relatively cheap, so check her out too!
