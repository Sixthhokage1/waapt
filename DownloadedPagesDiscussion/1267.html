    I am taking a break from the RP for the Alola arc. I feel utterly betrayed and disgusted by how the fact that cowardice and lack of motivation to move beyond previous failures and to overwrite them with a success has tainted the majority of this otherwise stellar group's judgment of a great plot.

    I may not be the best writer, but as an artist who dabbles in various mediums and have had Pokemon be with me every step of my way through getting my college degree and as well as being inspirational to many of my projects; I can not and will not betray my creative principles and go along with the decision that will dismiss the one chance I will have to be a part of an adaptation of a mainstream Pokemon plot.

    I don't know why, but I appear to be the only one can actually see Sun and Moon working as a plot for a successful arc for WAAPT story arc - an arc that I would have been proud to share with my friends and family - and willing to not let other people's fears taint that vision.

    But as it stands, I will not participate further in any plans for the Alola arc. I will finish my revamped plot for Pokesona which I will post details for tomorrow. I hope you enjoy your Alola plot and I won't mind when the door hits me on my way out until probably the XBC plot.
