    Dune's Two Cents

    Sunne/Moone

    The way I see it, the Altar of the Sunne is in Sun and Altar of the Moone is in Moon. So, why not have the emblem change whenever the time changes? Make it the Altar of the Sun during daylight, and once the sun sets change it to the Altar of the Moone - and reverse it with the Lake. Think of it like the Altar being the main home, and the lake being a sort of "vacation retreat", where there's still a home for whatever Legendary isn't in cycle that is theirs, even when the time of day that they represent isn't active.

    UB Ownership

    I'm personally thinking that U Bs, if ownership is allowed at ALL, should be treated similarly to Shadow Pokemon. Both are dangerous, alien to some degree, and have history of injury to unprepared trainers/other Pokemon.

    Arc Plot

    I'm in support of us following the Sun/Moon plot - Ludmilla being the exact right age range notwithstanding - partially because we also have the framework to support all of the cast for such an arc. Neo Illusion and Dr. Cid's Team Iron will/could be very very interested in the Ultra Wormholes, creating a two-tiered villainous threat - Team Skull and Villainous Aether for the not-as-experienced members, and possibly Neo Illusion and Team Iron for the members that have been around since before Orre. That way we can have the best of both worlds - one where the older members aren't having to mercilessly beat on Team Skull, but also one where the newer members can have a journey more towards a standard Alolan pace.

    Type: Null

    Honorary Legendary status is what I would go with, partially because I suspect the creation of a chimaeric amalgamation/attempted recreation of Arceus is an unequivocal act of true Mad Science and would preclude some serious moral questions.

    PEFE/Aether

    How we go about this could be heavily determined by what we do with the plot. For example:

    1: We go with the Sun/Moon plot (enhanced or otherwise). This would mean Gladion has personal experience with members of PEFE as well as other trainers in their immediate circle, and the idea of teaming the two up for conservation activities is basically a given - he's seen that this organization is definitely a force for good, just like how Aether was/is supposed to be.

    2: We come in after the plot. This would mean that Gladion has NO experience with anyone from PEFE, and to him it would just seem like another organization with similar goals on the outside, but like his mother demonstrated that could very well mean jack diddly. Much more work would be needed to court overtures of cooperation.

    Trials/League

    Following the basic trials with some changes for experience seems fine for me - it's a little demeaning to have a three-time champion chasing Ratatta around in circles. For the League, my sentiments echo Verax.

    Mega Evolution

    I feel that the Pokedex entries for Mega Evolution may not be completely accurate due to the nature of scientific observation being distant and cold, which was exactly the opposite of what was stated to be a healthy Mega Evolution-worthy relationship back in Kalos.

    In short, I think that the scientists who wrote the entries didn't account for the relationship between trainer and Pokemon stabilizing the transformation and it's quirks.

    Ride Pokemon

    Full support for registering personal ride Pokemon, or even registering ride Pokemon belonging to other trainers who give permission (I.e. Ludmilla riding Cun Annwn as her canine companion).
    A single dish, providing a balance of nature's blessing: meat, grains, and vegetables. How could anyone hate such a thing?

    Silent's Thoughts on the matter

    As for thoughts on stuff that's come up in the meantime...

    Tapus

    I'm voting no on the 'only protagonists can understand them' Silent just suggested because I guess I'm not much a fan of specific characters being treated like protagonists and others not from an in-universe perspective.

    Battle Bond

    I'd kind of prefer it's Greninja exclusive, but I'm probably outvoted on that and I don't feel especially strongly on it anyway.

    Game Plot

    I will very much admit I love the S/M plot (Part of the reason I'm doing an Egg Run when Bank comes out is so I can play through it a 3rd time), but I've got some qualms about it getting adapted as-is to the plot. If we end up making it work, cool, but here are some things related to that that I don't think gel

    Precedent

    The last example of us adapting a canon plot and trying to fit it over a whole group was the Delta Ep and while I'm fine with that as a subplot, I don't think we want a repeat of that for the main plot. I'd kind of prefer we figure out ways to avoid some of the problems that ran into before I'd feel good trying to adapt the Sun/Moon plot rather than just go into it as a good faith attempt.

    The Island Challenge

    Mechanically, it might serve a similar place to the Gym Challenge, but lore-wise, it feels like it's more tied to Alola's culture, I guess, and it being expanded to allow other people in as a pre-requisite for the League would be a big thing. In the game's plot, it's some Alolan 11-year olds (since the main protag moved there) expanding the challenge. If our characters filled that role, it'd be a bunch of people who probably normally shouldn't be participating in it making it decide to be expanded, which... seems kinda weird, IMO. So, if we do the main game plot, I'd much rather we alter things so the League's already a thing (or, at least, the Island Challenge has been explicitly expanded to allow people who aren't doing it as a coming-of-age Alolan thing to do it for the Alola League)

    Kukui

    I know he's the one you battle for the Champion title at the end of the game, but, IIRC, Kukui doesn't want to be the champion of the Alola League and that challenge felt more like wanting to battle the Champion rather than take the position for himself. So, I strongly vote we pick some person/people who aren't him for the Champion or come up with a reason why he'd be taking the Champion position when I don't think he wants it in game.

All I know for certain is that, for fairness' sake, I'm not even going to consider writing for more than one of them. Also, given that there are several instances of completely random Pokemon saying explicable phrases in Su/Mo (Totem Mimikyu springs to mind immediately), I'd say the "IT IS TIME" before Tapu Koko's battle is just another example of this quirk and only the Kahunas can understand them in WAAPT canon without the usual methods of learning Monese.

    Solgaleo and Lunala

    Still interested in Lunala, but I think I'll let anyone else who wants dibs on Solgaleo take 'em - I recall Keys expressing interest in the sun lion, for one. This is partly because, given their canon roles and nature, my concept a while back for a human-obsessed Lunala would still work with a little tweaking while my original conception of Solgaleo's personality is not nearly so functional anymore. Plus, two legendaries is enough for now, innit? Which, if either, was Nebby should be left ambiguous IMHO, since that's our policy for every other in-game element that differs between versions. On that note, both Altars and Lakes existing as we did with Black City and White Forest sounds like the best option to me.

    Ultra Beasts

    I think the Ultra Beasts should be capturable under the Delta Clause, since the game itself seems to treat them as nothing more than unusual and rare Wild Pokemon as opposed to outright Legendaries. Their power level is merely that of powerful ordinary mons for much the same reason. I reckon it shouldn't require any fiddling with dimensional wormholes to understand the Beasts, since understanding Monese implies understanding a wide variety of forms of communication emitted by vastly different creatures in the first place plus it's convenient.

    It's heavily implied the Ultra Beasts were only acting violently because they'd been intoxicated by the overeager Nihilego. This would make sense - after all, we all saw what a once loving mother and Friend to All Living Things became when the dimension-hopping jellyfish got her - so I'm mostly against UB cleanup being a focus of the Alola arc unless the Nihilego are still trying to pull the same shit.

    I see no real reason why the Gates and Beast Boost should be connected, since the Gates are based on various concepts from world mythology and spirituality and are thus quite different in execution - for instance, your Xurkitree who just Beast Boosted four times isn't going to die in agony like someone who breaches the fourth Gate layer will.

    Being a Faller should be restricted to secondary characters, since it implies ambitious alternate universe shenanigans a newbie would likely mess up. On the other hand, I have no problem with Ultra Space being accessible via Entralink.

    Aether

    The reformed Aether Foundation would likely be doing... well, exactly what they were supposed to be doing before the Nihilego fucked everything up - caring for wounded and sick Pokemon and performing humane scientific studies. Dealings with PEFE before their HeelFace Turn, and Pokefutures after it, would make sense in-universe, since big organisations rarely exist in a vacuum.

    Type: Null

    I'm in favour of putting the third extant Null under the Legendary Clause. Of course, this means someone will need to write for it and I'm not all that interested in doing so.

    Z-Moves

    I'm in favour of Z-Moves only being usable if you've already used whatever move you're basing it on for balance reasons. Z-Rings I reckon should be given out ceremonially by the Kahunas, though under what circumstances I'm not quite sure. As for the Z-Crystals, I imagine the ones you don't earn on the Challenge could just be found the same way Mega Stones were found in the Kalos arc - lying around in obscure places.

    Anabel

    Abstaining on commentary for this one because I have neither a problem with nor a preference for any of the proposed solutions, nor do I have any better ideas myself. Do whatever the other guys decide on.

    Champion

    The rotating Champion idea appeals to me because of the freshness of it, as well as our ability as humans to put challengers that weren't programmed into the game on that seat. Champion Guzma making Bug monotypers everywhere proud, anyone?

    Trials

    As I'm the one who suggested making the Trials the League entry requirement a la Gyms in the first place, I obviously support that idea. I support making the Trials variable, both because the canon ones are quite obviously eleven-year-old beginner tier and because, as stated when I vouched for rotating Champions, I'm a sucker for letting people exercise their creative muscles. I also happened to be the one who pointed out that certain important figures in the Island Challenge are currently up in the Pokemon League, and the aforementioned part of me wants to shuffle it around and put other canon characters in the vacant roles, but that would cause enough meta arguments that having the E4 multitask is probably the most viable solution.

    Plot

    I am very strongly against rerunning the canon plot for the reasons those who also oppose the idea have already stated. I would appreciate a more concrete plot than the past few arcs have had, though, since I have several big ideas rattling around in my cranium (will share if desired, since I'm selfish and love to run my mouth) and feel it would help others overcome the slowness caused by a mixture of loose plotting and increased responsibilities.

    Timeline

    Concept art is not really a hard and fast guideline, even if it is often useful. I say we just keep the timeframe as it usually is, with the plot of the games just having resolved itself and Grimsley being (INSERT NUMBER HERE) years older instead of any fixed value.

    Mega Evolution

    The canon examples are enough to convince me the darker Dex entries are talking what happens when Mega Evolution goes wrong, rather than what always happens. It helps that I'm a big fan of the theory that the entries are that much Darker and Edgier because Rotomdex is trolling the player character with messed-up dex content.

    Team Skull

    Skull were more louts who had nowhere else to go than hardened criminals or ideologues, and I doubt Guzma stating through clearly gritted teeth that they've officially disbanded will change that. I imagine Skull Grunts are still loitering in Po Town, though they're no longer an active threat now that Lusamine isn't secretly using them as distractions and attack dogs. Plumeria gladly dumped them once they "disbanded" and is now trying to make it legitimately, based on her dialogue before you fight her as a Champion challenger. Guzma has no such qualms, though, and will occasionally show up and boss the Grunts around despite the porkies he told about Skull no longer existing and him no longer being its boss.

    Rotomdex

    I reckon these should be mostly off-limits, as the extant Rotomdex is very much an experimental concept piece. It's not like it does much more than your average Pokedex can do, anyway.

    Ride

    You can ride whatever mons you want, solely because that's the convenient solution. Convenience is nice, after all.

    Lillie and Lusamine

    These two are in an ambiguous enough situation that I advocate not even touching on them unless we get more information in the future.

    Poke Pelago

    If Lillie and Lusamine are out of the picture, then there isn't much reason to worry about plot shenanigans involving their reunion with Mohn. Gladion is... more complicated, to the point where I have no real solutions for potential Gladion/Mohn interaction yet.

    Canon Players Characters

    Treat these guys the same way we treat all the others - they both existed, but neither of them have any bearing on the plot or can be encountered. If it hasn't been done since Hobgoblin made Lyra from HG/SS a character back in ye olden dayes, I see no reason for us to start now.

    Red and Blue

    The other Battle Tree opponents are likely commuting unless they all just dropped their jobs, so I see no reason why Red and Blue can't be doing the same.

    Battle Bond

    Abstaining on commentary for this.
