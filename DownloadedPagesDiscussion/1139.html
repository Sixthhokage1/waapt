    Well, hopefully.

It really doesn't want to be, and I shouldn't force it if it doesn't fit. So each dungeon is going to have a different genre, which will affect the usefulness of abilities and weapons.

    So what's Blue's Dungeon? o3o

    Portal: Eldritch Horror style, puzzle meets survival horror. o3o It's going to be a gigantic Rubik's Cube, where when anyone tries to enter a room, it will trigger a rotation of the cube, isolating groups of people quickly, and requiring the group to try and independently find the exit as quickly as possible. Of course, there will be traps and puzzle to watch out for, so be careful.

    What will the enemies be like? Well, uh, there won't be any. Well, except one. o3o There's a horror in the mindscape, trying to track the group, and will instinctively follow the people closest to it with the lowest sanity. Now, because it's a creature of the mind, it can... mess with the rules a little. When it enters a room, it MIGHT change the rotation... but it might not. I may or may not announce when it happens, we'll see as it gets closer.

    As far as I know right now, it'll go back to the RPG elements when you get to the boss, but I'm not sure, it may be a puzzle boss as well. I'll keep everyone posted as it gets closer. Now that I've actually got a basic idea of the dungeon though, it SHOULD be functional in a couple of weeks, as long as I can find a time we're all or mostly all willing to do it, and if anyone has any ideas for puzzles/mayhem, I'm all ears.

    Thanks for your attention, and if you have any ideas, please let me know. I'll be eagerly waiting to use them. o3o
