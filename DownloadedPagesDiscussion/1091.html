    27269 Umbramatic25th Dec 2015 04:39:42 AM from WAAPT or PEFE Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: In another castle
    The eternal dusk returns
    Alright, folks, after months of telling you I've been getting stuff ready so I can finally drop this post, I'm finally fucking dropping it. Henceforth, at last I give you...

    One hell of an update on Heroes After All! (No, I haven't forgotten about it. Yes it's still gonna be a thing eventually. Rejoice! REJOICE DAMMIT)

    Yes, this post's one big and really overdue (Last notable mention I could find about it in Discussion was in frigging April) update on Heroes After All, my next big project after WAAPT that nevertheless has close ties to it. It was originally supposed to be a thing this fall, but copious delays both RL and RP wise delayed it big time, so I figured I'd throw you guys a bone with this update to let you know that yes, this is a project I still full well plan on doing that means a hell of a lot to me.

    Anyway...

    "Wait, I'm new/I forgot! What the heck is a Heroes After All?"

    Heroes After All is a bit of a WAAPT spinoff/prequel story I'm writing and posting after I retire, in a decently similar vein to Tagg's Northern Shadow (if you're wondering why he hasn't gotten past the prologue, he full well intends on continuing it but has been crazy busy with grad school; rock on Tagg), and will likewise be posted on the second forum. I've been keeping the bigger details on the fic ambiguous because I feel some aspects are more fun as a surprise (though I've run them by several others for approval); I did drop a few very vague hints in an older post, which I'll drop an updated-to-accommodate-recent-developments version of here:

    • The fic is set at some point before the RP's equivalent of the events of RBY, though how long before is a mild spoiler.

    • The fic features a proto-J-Team of sorts.

    • Said proto-J-Team features predecessors of Lucius, Vierr, Tagg, Psyche, PERFECTSIS, and some of their mons. A Constantin predecessor shows up as well. (All with permission from their original authors, of course.)

    • Two actual WAAPT characters of mine (as in, the exact same peoplemons) will have a prominent role. At least two others will have minor roles.

    • At least one canon character will make an appearance, though naturally they'll be younger.

    • Mega Evolution factors into the plot in a pretty major way.

    • One of the villains is a character I was gonna use for WAAPT but decided to use for this fic instead. The Big Bad has a connection with a pre-existing WAAPT character. Constantin predecessor is obviously a third villain.

    • There are exactly two protagonists. They don't meet until midway through the plot.

    • A part of this fic's plot involves... "Reconstructing" something. Said something has to do with another something that happened in the RP.

    • On a tangentially related note to the above point... you know my mention in the Pokemon Perspective post I'm planning on using future stories to kinda kick the "humans are a waste of space in the Pokeverse" mentality I'm catching whiffs of elsewhere where it hurts? This is a BIG one in that department.

    Since as a spinoff/prequel it at least effectively take place within RP history, I've been dropping foreshadowing like it's hot since I first decided I'd do it over two years ago (and some of the Orwellian Retcons I've been doing with my older stuff on-and-off for various reasons involve minor tweaks to accommodate said foreshadowing), to the point the overarching events of the story are established to have happened somehow but 99% of the fine details are very intentionally completely ambiguous as to whether or not they actually happened, and I'll be keeping it that way until at least after I retire (more on that later). Additionally, while I said the fic takes place before the events of RP!G1, that could be anywhere from 30 to 3000 years before... But regardless I've designed the fic to be temporally and geographically displaced from the events of the RP proper enough to have essentially no bearing on them whatsoever (and all of the foreshadowing (except two extremely minor bits I approved with the players involved) and literally all the bitty retcons are relevant to my characters alone... Who will have all exited stage left by the time this thing goes live).

    tl;dr even though I'm writing this as a spinoff/prequel to the RP I'm trying to make damn well sure it doesn't step on the toes of the RP itself, and I have no desire for it to be required reading for any WAAP Ter, past, present, or future.

    (Though... I'd still very much like if any of you did read it. Since, you, know, next big project after I go that I'm really passionate about? ^_^;)

    Additionally, since there's things about this story I feel non-WAAP Ters will enjoy (mainly because I told some friends outside of here about it and they were anywhere from surprisingly intrigued to surprisingly excited) I'll be posting it on places like the Serebii forums and F Fnet as well as the second forum, with the versions posted elsewhere being part of the shared universe of my "normal" Pokemon fics. (Exactly how the hell I intend to try and pull that off will be elaborated on later in this post as well).

    But now that I've re-established the main points of what HAA even is and what I'm trying to do with it, some further things...

    The Ghost Lord's plot on... plot. And characters. And shit.

    So, most of the stuff I've been doing over the past few months is prep for when this thing finally drops (currently intended to be first half of next year, though that all depends on when I finally finish my run in the RP proper plus some other factors) and while a few people I'm coordinating the story with have seen the progress I'd made with that (and I'll be showing much of the others sometime after this post), I'd figured I'd give everyone else a more general summary of what I've been doing to get this story ready and the progress I've made on that in the past few months...

    • Basically the main thing I've been using to get this story ready is a rather expansive planning doc, which consists of the following:

      • Some general notes on what I'm trying to do with the story (Progress update: Not much, only some minor tweaks needed so far)

      • tl;dr of premise (Progress update: Zilch, none needed :P)

      • Character bios of all the major characters, sorted by group/role with important details on each (Progress update: This is one of two things I've gotten a hell of a lot done with; for the longest almost all these were just skeletons with just names and species; since I started hammering away at them most of them are now MUCH more detailed, and while I didn't manage to give them all that treatment before putting things on hold to focus back on the endgame more I got all I feel I needed to for now, which is great.

      • Worldbuilding notes, since the fic's set in a rather out-of-the way part of the Pokeverse and so I felt it be a good idea to elaborate on its setting and several related things plus Kynim's Tolkien-tier worldbuilding she does for her White Nuzlocke inspired me, dammit. (Progress report: This is another thing I've made big progress on, and in a similar fashion to the character bios; there used to be like, one entry and it wasn't even finished, but now not only has that one expanded upon I've added plenty of others, all pretty expansive. There's one entry that still needs slight tweaking and one pending actually being written out, but I'll be consulting others on them soon enough.)

      • A list of some of the bigger kinks to work out, plot and character wise (Progress report: Most are still a bit up in the air, but I worked out one of the most important ones; it was a non-spoilerly general goal of finding a way to make the antagonists more effective, but exactly how I worked it out is SPOILER LEVEL OFF THE CHARTS)

      • A list of planned silly bonus chapters not canon to the story, loosely inspired by ones my kinda-sorta-big-name out-of-WAAPT friend Dragonfree did for one of her most famous fanfics, Morphic. (Progress report: I've come up with some fun ones, though a couple will likely have to be retooled/replaced in light of something else I'll mention near the end of this post.)

    • I'm also working on a tentative plot outline, though haven't updated it in a while; I've currently got... 55-60% of the plot laid out so far? I've got pretty much the whole thing planned out in my head, but trying to outline it in a laconic fashion takes fits and spurts of motivation.

    • I'm also doing a hell of a lot of research for this fic, but most of it ties into that premise I think is more fun as a surprise, so I'll keep quiet for now on that. I will say two pending bits of research are reading CS Lewis' The Four Loves and re-reading The Epic of Gilgamesh, since they tie heavily into the story's themes.

    • And finally... I've tentatively prewritten a chunk of the first chapter. It was only the first several paragraphs, and I haven't touched it since then, but something happened I felt writing the very beginnings of the story long before the rest was an appropriate celebration of.

    But yeah, I've been doing a lot to get this story to eventually actually happen. And with that I will note there's one thing I need more public help with...

    Shining New Guest Star

    As I noted above, I've already gotten permission to work "proto"-versions of Lucius, Vierr, PERFECTSIS, Tagg, Psyche, and Constantin into the story along with appropriate mon partners. However, there's a bit of a problem... Proto!Lucius, Proto!Vierr, and Proto!PERFECTSIS were meant to be a trio, but I realized the first two were too similar to have a good dynamic, so I wound up reassigning Proto!Vierr to the same group as Proto!Tagg and Proto!Psyche since he'd work better there. Question is who I replace him with in the trio, and I'm asking your guys to volunteer: Feel free to offer up a human character and mon partner I can proto-fy for the story. As for what I need...

    Requirements

    • The character must have a personality sufficiently distinct from the other two members of the trio.

    • The character must be one I am sufficiently familiar with or otherwise can get ready access to advice in writing a facsimile of.

    Things that aren't required but would be ideal and increase your character's chances of being recruited

    • Would be nice if character was female, since other two are male.

    • Partner mon should ideally be first stage of a "lesser loved" mon's evolutionary line (ie: Widely ignored or despised by the wider Pokemon fandom, though for "despised" not due to It's Popular, Now It Sucks or Tier-Induced Scrappy-related reasons; looking more Granbull or Vanilluxe than Charizard or Wash Rotom) while still being one that can put up an at least decent fight (so no extremes like Luvdisc or male Combee).

    • Character should preferably be one from a player that hasn't contributed any others, though I'm loosest on this one.

    But yeah, feel free to offer anyone up and I'll take them into consideration.

    Now, for a bit of a plot twist that occurs before the story itself...

    One Fic For The Price Of Two

    As I mentioned near the beginning of this post, in addition to posting the story on the second forum I was also going to post it on places like the Serebii forums and F Fnet as part of my Pokemon "ficverse", and I know you've all had "wait, how the everloving fuck is Umbra gonna pull this one off?" in at least the back of your minds reading all this. Well...

    I initially conceived, planned, and designed this fic as something that could feasibly fit into both worlds unaltered with careful enough writing and plotting, but as the story took shape and I consulted more and more other people on it - some of whom were outright iffy on the approach, and some who felt it had a good chance of simply not working - I realized it was very much unfeasible.

    But... Well, to use a reference appropriate for this time of year that I'm fond of making anyway... I got an idea. An awful idea. A wonderful, awful idea.◊

    What is it? Well, I'm going to do two somewhat different versions of the story:

    • The WAAPT Edition, the actual WAAPT spinoff version, posted on the second forum and treated as any other second-forum oneshot series under full playerbase suprevision.

    • The Ficverse Edition, the version part of my shared universe of non-WAAPT Pokefics, posted on Serebii, F Fnet, and maaaaaaaaaaaaabye Bulbagarden with a wider audience in mind.

    It's gonna definitely take some extra work on my part, but I feel it'll be worth it because:

    • As things are currently planned, most needed changes are relatively minor; both versions fill big blank spots in their respective 'verses, and the whole thing was planned as a "fits both worlds" kind of thing long enough that the overarching story would remain about the same and the things to change would just be fine details. It doesn't hurt the ficverse draws very heavily from WAAPT regardless.

    • Again, the WAAPT edition's under full community supervision, and I'll be able to tweak/retool anything necessary (in particular I'll be running worldbuilding stuff by Tagg) while being more free to do my own thing with the ficverse edition.

    • This method will allow me to make more fun nods to both overarching canons, though some will stay in both to be Continuity Nod in one version and Mythology Gag in another.

    And if in the end that approach still isn't enough to let the WAAPT edition be fully canon, I'll be fully willing accept it remaining as ambiguously canon as I have currently and will continue leaving it as such within my stuff in the RP proper, so long as no one confirms or denies the fine details; the only reason I wanted it to be full RP canon was due to a subject weighing horribly on me for a very long time, but recent improvements there have left me feeling the story being ambiguously canon would be sufficient to finally ease my mind.

    But I like this approach enough I've already done a pre-emptive split of the planning docs (though ficverse one still needs to be updated with tweaked versions of much of the WAAPT's one's updates); it's crazy, yeah, but A. it should be established by now I'm the kind of guy batshit insane enough to try anyway, and B. on a likely related note... I just care that much and am super-deeply passionate about this story.

    Which... Leads into the final bit...

    A Labor of Love

    I will fess up right now: Many aspects of developing HAA over the past two years have led to utter psychological hell on my part. In getting it ready I've been exposed to so much hate... Vicious, toxic, virulent hate that has worn at my sanity and turned my anxiety and depression up to 11 to the point the slightest trigger can send me into panic attacks that at their worst last for days over the possibility I'll be flamed and cyberbullied over certain aspects of the fic, both WAAPT-related and otherwise, and even convincing myself I'm a horrible person for even wanting to write the story. It's gotten to the point where I've tried to ragequit more times than I can count - to the point it was jading one R Per that the story would ever happen for a time and I felt deeply ashamed of that - but the hate and anxiety and depression made me near-suicidal over this story at times... To the point where it fed into me actually attempting suicide last month.

    ...But despite it all I've kept on fighting. Even with all the swerves to giving up, I've had enough emotional boosts and restorations in faith to keep on fighting to make this story a reality, both from within the RP and outside of it. You know Nico's speech about bravery during HTYD? That was inspired by a friend from a community outside the RP saying pretty much the same thing about doing HAA to me. And for my friends here and elsewhere who have shown so much suport in regards to HAA, I'm going to keep on being brave in making it happen, because...

    Well, despite the name, Heroes After All is a love story. One I care truly, deeply about. One worth fighting for.

    But to end on a lighthearted note... I'll say it isn't a romantic or even familial love story. It's best summed up by three words...

    Epic. Fucking. Bromance.

    But that's all on that end for now; feel free to leave any thoughts, questions, and suggestions.

    And... Merry Christmas, everyone. It will likely (and admittedly hopefully) be my last here, so thank you all for sticking by me throughout.
