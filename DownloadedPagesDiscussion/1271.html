    Since we are still making plans, I'm going to go ahead and say my plans for this next year are.

    1. Finish Pokesona. I'm aiming for "end of March" to round out the Persona shout out.
    2. After Pokesona, Ren will be in Alola on and off, but he will not be participating in the plot because of obligations outside the J-Team, specifically the choosing of the next head of the Clavis Family. No this does NOT mean Ren is getting a huge buff as character. If anything, the end results will be an overall nerf.
    2a. First and foremost is that Martin - Ren's older brother - will be turning 40 this year. This means that according to Clavis Family tradition, it is time to pick the new family patriarch from the current candidates. Yes, this means Ren is in the running but I actually have a plan for this.
    2b. Since the Clavis Family succession is determined by what is essentially elective gavilkind, it means that all heads of branch families who carry the Clavis name will vote on whether Martin or Ren will become the next successor. The one who is chosen will go on to be the next head while the other will be spun off as a branch family.
    2c. Obviously, Martin will be chosen as the successor. What this means at the end of the day is that Ren will lose his infinite funds after the grace period is over. The grace period is there to allow any spun off member time to establish a role in society.
    2d. The results of spun off member's efforts directly affect said member's standing with the family, so Ren will actually need to focus on Non-J-team stuff. e.g. Ren starting a successful business will result in a secure position with in the family with support from the main family, but squandering the time or just out right failing at the endeavor will result in anything from from being cut off from the support of the main family until Ren prove himself worthy again to out-right being thrown out of the family entirely.
    3. So in order for Ren to continue to enjoy a lifestyle similar to what he's had for the last 32 years, Ren will then spend a few months solidifying the foundation he's been laying since even before his appearance in the RP. This will involve several call backs and actually doing what I wanted after the Kalos Arc - Ren winning all four Grand Festivals.
    3a. This will be his third attempt at the feat. But this time I think he has several good in-universe reasons to win this time:
    • Ren has the experience as a coordinator (which he lacked the first time)
    • Ren has no enchantments on him that can cause nasty surprises (which is what happened last time)
    • Ren has his drive for coordination back (thanks to Pokesona)
    • And most importantly - a motive to win: Ren needs the recognition that comes with the achievement. Remember, winning the four Grand festivals doesn't give any sort of official position or awards. All it means is that Ren will be very well placed to have a long term job as a coordination advisor and be likely asked to do promotion deals which will lead into powerful connections in the Coordination industry.

    2 & 3 tl;dr version: Ren will be spun off as a branch member of the Clavis Family and thus lose his "Infinite Funds" status he's been enjoying. And because he no longer has infinite funds, Ren needs to win the Four Grand festivals next spring in order to secure his position within his family and support himself.
