    So as some of you may already have heard (or guessed), I've been considering a return to WAAPT - not just the community, but the RP itself. Now, the last time I tried this, I ended up storming right back out again. It was partly due to personal dissatisfaction with my own writing skills, but that doesn't change the fact that I conducted myself in a way that was completely inconsiderate to others and I'd like to apologise again for how I acted. I was self-centred, stand-offish and just kind of a general dick.

    Given the way I've behaved in the past, I can understand why some people might have reservations about my return. I have a fair few myself - things are probably going to get really hectic for me towards the end of the year, and I'm sure my writing's only gotten worse in my absence. But the more I think about it, the more I want to come back. I miss making plots and interacting with other people's characters. I've done a bad job of communicating it in the past, but I had some really great times here, and it's one of the few places where I've made things I'm actually proud of.

    I'm putting this post out here because if anyone has any objections to me coming back, or wants to tell me aspects of my behaviour that I ought to improve in future, I don't want anybody to hesitate in letting me know. I made a complete mess of handling my characters last time around and I'm still not sure whether I should try to glue the remaining pieces back together or just leave them alone and move on. I've got plenty of new ideas flying around, and I'll try to communicate them properly to avoid some of the problems there were last time I was here.

    Whatever ends up happening in the future, I want to say again that my experiences with WAAPT and its community have been overwhelmingly positive overall, and thank you guys for making them that way. Sorry if that came off as nauseatingly cheesy but I really couldn't think of any other way to put it.

    25813 Herbert40k22nd Jul 2015 04:50:48 PM from Merry Olde England Get RP Mod , Relationship Status: I-It's not like I like you, or anything!
    Imperial Commissar
    After a lot of time spent scratching my head and occasionally bashing it against walls, I've finally settled upon a character concept to test the waters with. I've still got a few others that could be developed further if this one looks problematic, so if there are any issues do let me know.

    Trainer

    • Name: Shun Hontou
    • Age: 14
    • Gender: Male
    • Ethnicity: Mixed (Japanese/African-American)
    • Region of Origin: Celestic Town, Sinnoh
    • Organisational Ties: Works for the International Police as a freelance detective.
    • Appearance: Even for his young age, Shun is short and slightly built. He has dark hair that comes to rest in an untidy mop no matter how many attempts are made to tidy it and bright brown eyes which always seem to be scanning his surroundings. His usual attire is a navy blue pinstriped suit, matched with a grey tie and black leather gloves. No matter what the circumstances, he is never seen without his distinctive blue cap.
    • Backstory: From early childhood, Shun was always enamoured with detective stories. His parents and grandparents were renowned police inspectors, and so he grew to idolise them as seekers of truth and justice, hoping to one day follow in their footsteps. However, his childhood of fantasy came to an end when his parents were killed and his brother paralysed by a landslide on Mt Coronet. The orphaned boy was taken in by his grandfather and soon became convinced that the death of his parents was no accident - rather, it was a case that he had to solve. He set out on his Pokemon journey with the aim of becoming an investigator for the International Police, which he managed after several years thanks to a mixture of intelligence and sheer bloody-minded persistence. Now he travels across the regions in search of cases to solve, but he has never forgotten about the mystery that began his journey in the first place.

      Shun's insight and analytical skill are impressive, but he also has an inflated sense of his own abilities. This can lead to others viewing him as arrogant (often with good reason). His serious nature causes him to struggle with concepts like humour and making friends. Though his ideal of a detective is undoubtedly romanticised, he has enough sense to focus on doing the right thing rather than following the letter of the law. More than anything else, he hates being judged or looked down upon due to his age, something that is commonplace in his interactions with his superiors at the International Police. Bringing it up in conversation is an excellent way to try his otherwise considerable patience.

    Pokemon

    • Nickname: Gregson
    • Species: Sableye
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Keen Eye
    • Biography: Gregson is Shun's starter, given to him by his grandfather shortly after the accident that killed his parents. He has an irreverent sense of humour that contrasts with his trainer's dour nature, but he also possesses an insight into people and their motivations that Shun sometimes lacks. Since he no way to effectively communicate such insights, however, his role on the team is mostly limited to spotting stuff.

    • Nickname: Morstan
    • Species: Elgyem
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Analytic
    • Biography: Morstan is a Pokemon of few words. For the most part, he is content to silently observe the world around him, absorbing knowledge for later use. This makes him an excellent ally to have around when Shun needs some fact-finding done, despite his taciturn attitude. He has a particular interest in psychic phenomena and ancient history that can occasionally distract him from more pertinent details of a situation.

    • Nickname: Ferrier
    • Species: Patrat
    • Gender: Male
    • Ability: Keen Eye
    • Biography: In Ferrier's world, everything is part of the Conspiracy - capitalised because there is only one of its kind, and its sinister influence knows no bounds. He is convinced that everything and everyone could be part of a sinister plot against him and his trainer. As a result, he is permanently on the lookout and always ready to protect Shun against agents of the Conspiracy, whether Shun wants him to or not.
