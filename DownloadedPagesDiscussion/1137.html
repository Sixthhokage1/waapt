    I am thou. . . Thou art I. . .
    I like to express my opinion that almost everyone here is overdoing the drama on the matter concerning Umbra.

    While yes, I'm agreeing this is an unpleasant and unwelcome curve ball, I'm also someone who has experienced what Umbra is going through firsthand. As such, I can say that while the whole debacle is likely wholly unwelcome, it's not as bad as people are making it out to be.

    With that said, there really isn't much we can or could have done. Umbra's family likely had been concerned about Umbra for a while and things apparently reached an extremely tense point with him recently, prompting emergency plans to be made.

    What we CAN do for Umbra is:
    1) Stop the drama in the main chat and Discussion thread. It makes everyone more stressed than they should. Which as our history shows, when a lot of us are more stressed than normal, people get angry and things get said that make other feel awful. If you need to voice concerns beyond passing messages to Umbra, I feel that should be done in the NL chat.

    2) Offer hopes and prayers (or whatever it is you use) for him - preferably ones that don't involve saying his family is completely at fault and that Umbra didn't deserve this. I don't who is at fault for what, but measures like these are last resorts for most sensible people, so whatever happened reached a certain panic threshold. So until we all know the full story, let's assume nothing.

    3) Welcome Umbra back with a smile and open arms. When Umbra gets back, he's probably not going to want to discuss this incident right away. So let's not pester him for answers or unduly remind him of this. Let's keep things normal and pick up where we left of before he left.
