    A bunch of shit on: WAAPT Christmas Shenanigans!

    Yep, Christmas is dropping this Friday, and there's some stuff I wanna go over regarding that for both the RP as a whole and some personal stuff on my end:

    Hangouts for the Holidays

    It's pretty much tradition by now for most of the J-Team to have a big gathering for the days around Christmas, and we've done it in a decent variety of locations. However, last year's was at the very start of Hoenn-2 (let that sink in a second) and was in the Idri Mansion in Petalburg; while that's still a viable option, it's probably kinda boring, so I've thought of two other options...

    A New Frontier For Christmas

    That's right, why not have a rocking Christmas at the Battle Frontier? Most people have been lulling in Ever Grande since October, so I thought a Christmas celebration there would be a fun excuse to get our asses over there, plus it would be fun if we could incorporate the facilities and such into the celebrations somehow.

    Next might be a bit less feasible and I'd need to coordinate with Crow, but I still felt it a fun enough idea to suggest:

    A very merry X-Naut-Mas

    Since Nico's now leader of the Thwapian division of the X-Nauts and hasn't seen most of the rest of the J-Team in over a year, I figure he could come back in style by hosting a Christmas party at a retooled X-Naut facility! Exactly where is yet to be decided, but the whole J-Team will have a host of X-Nauts partying with them and serving their needs (and you can write for some of them if you need to or so desire).

    But tell me which option sounds more fun.

    Anyhoo...

    Holiday Ties

    This is the stuff relevant to my characters specifically:

    New Year's Resolution: Be more social.

    There are four specific characters I particularly want to focus on having interactions with the rest of the J-Team during holiday shenanigans: Nico, Seishirou, Fuuma, and Robin. The reasons for each are as follows:

    - Nico obviously hasn't seen much of anyone in the J-Team for over a year and wants to catch up more personally, plus he'd find it nice to have a lot of company while he's sick. (Him hosting the Christmas shenanigans as suggested above would be helpful in those regards.)

    -Seishirou and Fuuma both felt in universe they should get to know the J-Team better after what happened to them at the end of Werehuman Madness, and meta-wise I felt it'd be better if they actually interacted with other characters and players more, and it would be fun for them to start learning how to deal with their new state of affairs over holiday shenanigans.

    - Robin I just want to show being happier and mentally healthier (especially with recent reveals on his end even further driving home how much his life has utterly sucked) now that the Delta Episode and his character arc have concluded chronologically (though I'm gonna still damn well keep the TSed actual completion of both going throughout; more on that in a bit).

    But yeah, offers for help with all of them in that regard are very, very, very much appreciated. Though on their note...

    Last-Minute Gift Plans

    Of the four characters above... I've already planned with Crow how Nico's gonna give back to Team Umbra and the HTYD crew for Christmas; however, I want Robin to do the same with Team Umbra and the Delta Episode crew, and that's a bit trickier. Main problem for DE crew is giving gifts that are significant yet sufficiently unspoilery, so I'll probably have to ask the people involved for help there, so Bitters, Verax, Pippi, and Tagg, get to me ASAP on it. As for him getting gifts for the rest of Team Umbra, that's relatively simpler, so anyone feel free to throw suggestions there - I'd deeply appreciate it.

    As for the jerkbros, they aren't giving any gifts, but I want them both to revive at least one - specifically a Pokemon egg for each! The obvious candidate for who gives them those is Joseph, but he's given a lot of my characters eggs already, so I'd prefer other characters/players do it this time, so please, feel free to offer for either and I'll tell you what egg I want. Any of you who want to give them some other silly gift is free too - so Dogs, feel free to do that if you want. :P

    But that's it for Christmas stuff. There's two other things I wanna adress in this thread tonight but composing from phone is suffering and I want to hear everyone's thoughts on these first.
