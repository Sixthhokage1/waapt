    Most of you have probably noticed that I haven't been posting very often. This has been for a variety of reasons. Mostly, I haven't had motivation to post, due to lack of ideas for Psyche's character arc and insecurities over the way I've been portraying her.

    But lately, I've been noticing something strange. WAAPT isn't as enjoyable for me anymore. Not because anything's really changed in it. But because it's not filling a need I've had for a long time. As most/all of you know, I'm trans, and being a part of WAAPT has helped me tremendously in escaping from meatspace's conservative demands of masculinity. And while it can help with that still, it does involve a certain level of mind space to do so, which can get taxing at times. I've been presenting as female around campus lately, and that's helped tremendously filling the role that WAAPT did for so long. As such, I actually enjoy talking to people now. I actually enjoy being outside of my room. And as such, I hate to say, but I'd much rather enjoy life outside of my computer as opposed to inside it. I'll still be in chats, but I probably won't be in much.

    1000 apologies, everyone. I'm scared I came across as rather blunt writing this. I still love each and everyone one of you guys, but my heart's just not in it anymore.
