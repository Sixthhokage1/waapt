    Okay so just throwing an idea for a side arc.

    FATE/WAAPT - Title Pending. Very much WIP

    Okay so as it's been thrown around in the chat, and I'm making an official suggestion here as the one (arrogant tone) obviously best suited to help structure it and facilitate it.

    In order to cut down on all the lengthy exposition that would be necessary to explain how the Fate system works, I'll explain only what's necessary.

    Essentially this is just an excuse for a grand battle royale with old historical figures in the rp, with some connections to ancient artifact and an Alakagross construct designed to siphon and control the energy of said artifact.

    Said artifact would either be something similar to an Elder Scroll, a "fragment of creation", essentially, or like the Lunar Pendant or Wish Stone as an omnipotent wish granting device. The construct would be a Living MacGuffin or golem or other sapient-ish thing meant to obtain it's essence in a controllable form, however since that's impossible as is, it created a convoluted system for using that power and breaking it down to a form it can harness.

    Said system happens to be our Holy Grail War analogue, using artifacts belonging to mythological or historical beings, they're summoned back to the present day filling one of seven roles they associate closest as superpowered forms of themselves to and then promptly slaughter each other in a big strategic battle royale and convoluted mind game of the ages all in the name of powering this artifact and being able to use it for themselves, being anchored to the present day through a normal companion.

    That's the working premise, if it ends up actually being a thing I'll be handling how exactly any summoned beings are adapted by this system.

    Working current idea for me is the golem construct thing is frozen in a glacier in northern Sinnoh and is freed and or discovered by or because of glitchnanigans.

    Thoughts, critiques, complaints, head shakes, thumbs ups are all appreciated.
