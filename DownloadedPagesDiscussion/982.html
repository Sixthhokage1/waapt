    In other news:

    I'm thinking of doing a media plot!

    There are few games that I'm eyeing right now, though they're only from two seperate series. Here, let me give a basic summary of what I'm considering right now. Let me know which ones interest you and what ideas you might have.

    Megaman Zero

    How I'd do it: I'm a huge fan of the Megaman series and the Zero series is my favorite arc. I could really port any of the four games, or all four, but if I had to choose one game it would probably the the first in the series. Some basic adaptations would be that reploids would be replaced with pokemon and Neo Arcadia itself would be replaced with a cult of some nature, perhaps located in a desert. The reploids vs humans conflict would become pokemon vs humans and Copy X may or may not be a devilish Pikachu. Zero would wind up being a trainer's pokemon, with the trainer themselves filling the roll of Ciel. I debated the idea of having Honedge appear as a reference to the Z-saber.

    Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword

    How I'd do it: The biggest issue for me in Skyward Sword is probably Skyloft. I'm not sure how to parody a flying village. Parodying the loftwings is easy peasy. I could replace them with literally any flying pokemon or a specific species of flying pokemon if I wanted to be more accurate. The Skyward Sword is played straight up until the character filling the roll of Link draws it out, at which point it turns out to be a Honedge that binds itself to the wielder's arm and refuses to leave until the wielder fulfills their plot mandated destiny. It also offers telepathic guidance as a reference to Fi. A lot of the dungeons would need further expanding that I don't feel like doing before I even know if I'm going to use SS, but I have ideas for Ghirahim and Demise off the top of my head. Ghirahim would be a second Honedge possessing the body of either a trainer or a Bisharp. This zombie knight would harass the Link throughout the plot. After his final defeat the honedge would abandon it's host and bind itself an evil spirit returning from the spirit world. That spirit would be a trainer, a Pangoro or a Tyranitar. I do imagine the honedge evolving. I also wonder about tie ins with Wolf's TP plot from ages ago.
